In the fully dynamic maximal matching problem, the goal is to maintain a maximal matching in a graph undergoing an online sequence of edge insertions and deletions. The problem has been studied extensively in the oblivious-adversary setting, where randomized algorithms with polylogarithmic worst-case and constant amortized update time have been known for some time.

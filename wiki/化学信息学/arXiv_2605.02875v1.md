We present the ground-based imaging campaign and light curves of Markarian 817 as part of the multiwavelength monitoring program AGN STORM\,2. Observations were carried out over 1.4 years in \emph{uBgVriz} filters, with a median cadence of 0.4 days in \emph{g}. Reverberation lags are measured using three methods (ICCF, JAVELIN, and PyROA) with the Swift UVW2 band (1928 Å) as the reference light curve.

We analyze a simple extension of the Schwinger model, which we refer to as the $p$-Schwinger model, on a de Sitter background. In this theory, the charged massless fermions carry non-unit integer charge $p$. In Minkowski space, the $p$-Schwinger model has discrete zero- and one-form global symmetries that are spontaneously broken, yielding $p$ degenerate ground states.

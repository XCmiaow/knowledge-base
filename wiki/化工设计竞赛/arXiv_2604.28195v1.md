$ω$~Centauri, the most massive globular cluster in the Milky Way, exhibits a level of stellar population complexity that has long resisted a unified chemical characterisation. We exploit high-resolution near-infrared spectroscopy from the Milky Way Mapper survey (MWM DR19) to construct one of the largest homogeneously analysed samples of $ω$~Cen members to date.

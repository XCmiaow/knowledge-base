Strong-coupling experiments based on magnons enable the exploration into on-chip demonstrations involving numerous long-lived excitations. Yttrium iron garnet (YIG) has been considered for decades as a gold standard material for magnonics due to its low-loss magnonic properties. While YIG has successfully demonstrated strong-coupling in macroscopic device geometries, the strong coupling of magnons in truly sub-10 micron YIG structures to date has not yet been realized.

Network telescopes serve as a critical passive monitoring tool for capturing unsolicited Internet traffic, providing insights into global scanning and reconnaissance behavior. This study analyzes a 10-day dataset during January 2025 consisting of approximately 22 million packets collected by the ORION network telescope at Merit Network. By employing privacy-preserving metadata analysis and lightweight behavioral heuristics, we identify scanning and backscatter patterns without payload inspection

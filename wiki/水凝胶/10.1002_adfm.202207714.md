## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「a novel strategy is reported to construct the liquid‐free cellulose‐derived ionic conductive elastomer that is successfully applied in the wearable sensor and triboelectric nanogenerators (TENG)」** — 贡献在于把“无液体”“纤维素来源”“离子导电弹性体”与“传感/TENG应用”放进同一材料体系中，目标直指可持续柔性电子喵~
- **[原文]「This study paves a promising strategy for fabricating sustainable and multifunction flexible electronics that are suitable for harsh environments」** — 作者将其定位为面向苛刻环境的可持续多功能柔性电子材料方案喵~

## 方法

- **[原文]「the ionic conductive elastomer with physical and chemical dual‐crosslinking network is prepared via in situ polymerization of the polymerizable deep eutectic solvent」** — 制备方法是以可聚合低共熔溶剂为反应体系，通过原位聚合得到物理/化学双交联离子导电弹性体喵~
- **[原文]「the cellulose contributes to forming the dense hydrogen bond crosslinking network that can improve the recyclability, anti‐freezing, and solvent‐resistance performance」** — 纤维素的作用不是单纯填料，而是参与形成致密氢键交联网络，从而支撑可回收、抗冻和耐溶剂性能喵~

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The construction of dual‐crosslinking network improves the mechanical strength and toughness more than 2 times」** → 双交联网络带来了明确的定量收益，机械强度和韧性均提升超过 2 倍喵~
- **[原文]「the ionic conductive elastomer is successfully applied in the wearable sensors and TENG for monitoring human motion, and in harvesting mechanical energy to convert into stable electrical outputs to light the LEDs, charge the capacitor, and power the electronic watch」** → 该材料不仅能做人机运动监测，还能把机械能转成稳定电输出，支撑点亮 LED、给电容充电和驱动电子手表喵~
- **[原文]「maintains reliable sensing and energy harvesting performance even after recycling, soaking in organic solvent, or at low/high temperature」** → 回收后、浸泡有机溶剂后以及低温/高温条件下，传感与能量采集性能仍可保持可靠喵~

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出电导率、应变范围、循环寿命、低温/高温的具体温度值，以及 TENG 的电压、电流或功率密度等定量指标，因此当前只能确认其方向性优势，尚不足以判断它相对现有体系的绝对性能边界喵~

# Initiatorless Solar Photopolymerization of Versatile and Sustainable Eutectogels as Multi‐Response and Self‐Powered Sensors for Human–Computer Interface

**中文标题**: 无引发剂太阳光光聚合制备多功能可持续共晶凝胶用于多响应与自供能人机交互传感器

## 摘要

> 该工作针对“simultaneously achieve multi‐functional and multi‐response integrations through a facile and sustainable approach”的难点，提出了“a self‐healing, environment tolerant, intrinsically conductive, and recyclable eutectogel with multiple responses”，并采用“one‐step solar‐initiated polymerization of deep eutectic solvents (DESs) and ionic liquids (ILs)”完成制备。[原文]「However, it still remains a challenge to simultaneously achieve multi‐functional and multi‐response integrations through a facile and sustainable approach.」[原文]「Herein, a self‐healing, environment tolerant, intrinsically conductive, and recyclable eutectogel with multiple responses is developed via one‐step solar‐initiated polymerization of deep eutectic solvents (DESs) and ionic liquids (ILs).」
>
> 其材料基础是“Abundant hydrogen bonds and ion‐dipole interactions”，由此获得“high mechanical strength (8.8 MPa), ultra‐stretchability (>1100%), strong self‐adhesion (≈12 MPa), recyclability, and autonomously self‐healing ability”。[原文]「Abundant hydrogen bonds and ion‐dipole interactions impart eutectogels with high mechanical strength (8.8 MPa), ultra‐stretchability (>1100%), strong self‐adhesion (≈12 MPa), recyclability, and autonomously self‐healing ability.」
>
> 应用上，这种本征导电共晶凝胶既能对“strain, temperature, and humidity”产生响应，用于“wireless motion recognition and human–computer interaction control”，也能组装成单电极TENG，并在“approximately −40–60 °C”范围内维持稳定输出。[原文]「Furthermore, the intrinsically conductive eutectogels with appealing versatile sensations on strain, temperature, and humidity can serve as wearable sensors for wireless motion recognition and human–computer interaction control.」[原文]「More importantly, the eutectogel‐assembled single‐electrode triboelectric nanogenerator (TENG) exhibits extreme environment‐tolerant and fast self‐healable properties that contribute to maintaining excellent and stable electrical outputs in a wide work temperature range (approximately −40–60 °C), which appear to be promising in self‐powered flexible electronics with high environmental adaptability.」

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Herein, a self‐healing, environment tolerant, intrinsically conductive, and recyclable eutectogel with multiple responses is developed via one‐step solar‐initiated polymerization of deep eutectic solvents (DESs) and ionic liquids (ILs).」** — 核心贡献是在DES与IL体系中用一步太阳光聚合做出同时具备自愈、耐环境、本征导电、可回收和多响应特性的共晶凝胶。
- **[原文]「More importantly, the eutectogel‐assembled single‐electrode triboelectric nanogenerator (TENG) exhibits extreme environment‐tolerant and fast self‐healable properties that contribute to maintaining excellent and stable electrical outputs in a wide work temperature range (approximately −40–60 °C), which appear to be promising in self‐powered flexible electronics with high environmental adaptability.」** — 另一项关键贡献是把该材料进一步推进到单电极TENG器件层面，显示出面向自供能柔性电子的环境适应性。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Herein, a self‐healing, environment tolerant, intrinsically conductive, and recyclable eutectogel with multiple responses is developed via one‐step solar‐initiated polymerization of deep eutectic solvents (DESs) and ionic liquids (ILs).」** — 方法上采用DES与IL的一步太阳光引发聚合，路线短、组分清晰，摘要强调其目标是“facile and sustainable approach”。
- **[原文]「Abundant hydrogen bonds and ion‐dipole interactions impart eutectogels with high mechanical strength (8.8 MPa), ultra‐stretchability (>1100%), strong self‐adhesion (≈12 MPa), recyclability, and autonomously self‐healing ability.」** — 机理上依赖丰富氢键与离子-偶极相互作用，把力学强度、延展性、黏附性、可回收性和自愈性绑定到同一材料网络中。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「Abundant hydrogen bonds and ion‐dipole interactions impart eutectogels with high mechanical strength (8.8 MPa), ultra‐stretchability (>1100%), strong self‐adhesion (≈12 MPa), recyclability, and autonomously self‐healing ability.」** → 机械与界面性能的关键定量结果是8.8 MPa强度、>1100%拉伸率和≈12 MPa自黏附强度，并且同时保留可回收与自主自愈能力。
- **[原文]「Furthermore, the intrinsically conductive eutectogels with appealing versatile sensations on strain, temperature, and humidity can serve as wearable sensors for wireless motion recognition and human–computer interaction control.」** → 传感维度覆盖应变、温度、湿度三类输入，且应用场景直接落到可穿戴无线动作识别与人机交互控制。
- **[原文]「More importantly, the eutectogel‐assembled single‐electrode triboelectric nanogenerator (TENG) exhibits extreme environment‐tolerant and fast self‐healable properties that contribute to maintaining excellent and stable electrical outputs in a wide work temperature range (approximately −40–60 °C), which appear to be promising in self‐powered flexible electronics with high environmental adaptability.」** → 组装成单电极TENG后，器件在约−40–60 °C的宽温区间内仍能保持优良且稳定的电输出，说明其自供能应用不局限于常温环境。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要给出了8.8 MPa、>1100%、≈12 MPa和approximately −40–60 °C等关键结果，但没有给出循环寿命、响应速度、灵敏度系数、TENG输出电压/电流/功率密度等定量指标，因此暂时无法判断其长期稳定性和与现有方案相比的优势幅度。
- **[推断]** 摘要强调“strain, temperature, and humidity”多响应，但未说明不同刺激之间的解耦策略；如果用于复杂人机交互，后续需要验证多模态信号在实际场景中的选择性与抗串扰能力。

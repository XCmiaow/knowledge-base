## 摘要

> [原文]「Wearable electronics based on conductive hydrogels (CHs) easily suffer from prolonged response times, reduced wearing comfort, shortened service lives, and impaired signal accuracy in cold environments, because conventional CHs tend to freeze at subzero temperatures and lose their flexibility, adhesion, transparency, and conductivity, which will limit their applications in extreme environments.」文章指出，传统导电水凝胶在亚零度环境中易冻结，并丧失柔性、黏附性、透明性与导电性，导致响应变慢、佩戴舒适性下降、寿命缩短和信号失真。  
> [原文]「Inspired by the way psychrotolerant creatures and superabsorbent materials interfere with the hydrogen bonding networks of water, a freeze‐resistant conductive organohydrogel (COH) is facilely fabricated.」与[原文]「The synergy effect between charged polar terminal groups and a binary solvent system of water–ethylene glycol weakens the hydrogen bonding between water molecules and endows the COH with remarkable freezing tolerance (−78 °C).」作者受耐冷生物和高吸水材料启发，构建了导电有机水凝胶，并通过末端带电极性基团与水–乙二醇二元溶剂的协同作用削弱水分子间氢键，使材料具备 −78 °C 的抗冻能力。  
> [原文]「Additionally, the obtained COH is ultra‐stretchable (≈6185%), tough (9.2 MJ m−3), highly transparent (≈99%), self‐adhesive (10.2–27.8 kPa), and biocompatible.」与[原文]「Benefiting from the exceptional low‐temperature tolerance of the prepared COH, these devices exhibit fast response with delay‐free signals even at −40 °C.」该材料同时兼具高拉伸、高韧性、高透明、自黏附和生物相容性，并可支撑应变传感器和手环式心电传感器在 −40 °C 下实现快速、无延迟信号输出。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Inspired by the way psychrotolerant creatures and superabsorbent materials interfere with the hydrogen bonding networks of water, a freeze‐resistant conductive organohydrogel (COH) is facilely fabricated.」** — 提出一种受耐冷生物与高吸水材料启发的抗冻导电有机水凝胶设计思路。  
- **[原文]「This versatile COH is assembled into a strain sensor and a well‐designed bracelet electrocardiogram sensor.」** — 将该有机水凝胶进一步集成为应变传感器与手环式心电传感器，证明其可穿戴器件适配性。  
- **[原文]「Overall, this work proposes a strategy to develop multifunctional COHs for supporting human health in cold environments.」** — 工作的总体贡献是为寒冷环境中的健康监测提供多功能导电有机水凝胶开发策略。

## 方法

- **[原文]「The synergy effect between charged polar terminal groups and a binary solvent system of water–ethylene glycol weakens the hydrogen bonding between water molecules and endows the COH with remarkable freezing tolerance (−78 °C).」** — 方法核心是利用末端带电极性基团与水–乙二醇二元溶剂体系的协同效应，削弱水分子之间的氢键网络，从而实现 −78 °C 抗冻。  
- **[原文]「Inspired by the way psychrotolerant creatures and superabsorbent materials interfere with the hydrogen bonding networks of water, a freeze‐resistant conductive organohydrogel (COH) is facilely fabricated.」** — 制备逻辑不是单纯加防冻剂，而是模仿生物与高吸水材料对水氢键网络的干预机制来完成材料构筑。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The synergy effect between charged polar terminal groups and a binary solvent system of water–ethylene glycol weakens the hydrogen bonding between water molecules and endows the COH with remarkable freezing tolerance (−78 °C).」** → 该材料的抗冻下限达到 −78 °C，显著超出一般可穿戴水凝胶的低温工作范围。  
- **[原文]「Additionally, the obtained COH is ultra‐stretchable (≈6185%), tough (9.2 MJ m−3), highly transparent (≈99%), self‐adhesive (10.2–27.8 kPa), and biocompatible.」** → 材料同时给出全部关键性能数据：拉伸率约 6185%、韧性 9.2 MJ m−3、透光率约 99%、自黏附强度 10.2–27.8 kPa，并具备生物相容性。  
- **[原文]「Benefiting from the exceptional low‐temperature tolerance of the prepared COH, these devices exhibit fast response with delay‐free signals even at −40 °C.」** → 器件层面的直接结果是，在 −40 °C 条件下仍能快速响应且无延迟输出信号。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要强调了低温性能与器件演示，但未交代循环加载稳定性、长期皮肤佩戴可靠性、复杂环境下抗脱水能力以及大规模制备成本，这些应是后续走向实际应用前需要补充验证的关键环节。  
- **[推断]** 目前展示的器件主要是应变传感和心电监测，后续可进一步验证该策略在更多寒冷环境生理信号采集场景中的通用性。

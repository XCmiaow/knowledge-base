## 摘要

> [原文]「Quasi‐solid aqueous zinc ion batteries (AZIBs) based on flexible hydrogel electrolytes are promising substitutions of lithium‐ion batteries owing to their intrinsic safety, low cost, eco‐friendliness and wearability.」该工作将柔性水凝胶电解质定位为可穿戴水系锌离子电池替代锂离子电池的候选路线喵~  
> [原文]「Herein, an all‐around hydrogel electrolyte is constructed through a convenient energy dissipation strategy via the rapid and reversible intramolecular/intermolecular ligand exchanges between Zn2+ and alterdentate ligands.」作者用 Zn2+ 与交替配位配体的分子内/分子间快速可逆配体交换，构筑了兼顾能量耗散与多性能协同的全能型水凝胶电解质喵~  
> [原文]「The Zn||V2O5 full cells exhibit large capacity of 230.6 mAh g−1 and high capacity retention of 75.2% after 1000 cycles. Furthermore, flexible AZIBs operate stably even under extreme conditions including low temperature (−40 °C) and large bending angle (180°).」该电解质不仅支撑高容量与长循环，还能在低温和大形变条件下维持柔性器件稳定工作喵~

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「However, it remains a challenge to lower the freezing point without sacrificing the fundamental advantages of hydrogel electrolytes such as conductivity and mechanical properties.」** — 论文直指该领域核心矛盾：抗冻性提升常常以导电性和力学性能受损为代价喵~
- **[原文]「Herein, an all‐around hydrogel electrolyte is constructed through a convenient energy dissipation strategy via the rapid and reversible intramolecular/intermolecular ligand exchanges between Zn2+ and alterdentate ligands.」** — 贡献不只是做出一种配方，而是提出了基于可逆配体交换的能量耗散设计策略，为“强韧 + 导电 + 抗冻 + 黏附”协同提供了统一机制喵~
- **[原文]「This work offers a facile strategy for developing mechanically deformation‐resistant, dendrite‐free, and environmentally adaptable AZIBs.」** — 作者把结果上升为一条可迁移的器件设计路线，目标是机械形变耐受、无枝晶和环境适应性的柔性 AZIB 喵~

## 方法

- **[原文]「Herein, an all‐around hydrogel electrolyte is constructed through a convenient energy dissipation strategy via the rapid and reversible intramolecular/intermolecular ligand exchanges between Zn2+ and alterdentate ligands.」** — 方法核心是利用 Zn2+ 与交替配位配体之间快速、可逆的分子内/分子间交换来实现能量耗散，从而在网络受力时分散应变并保持结构完整喵~
- **[原文]「Due to the alterdentate ligands help to improve the zinc ion solvation structure and guide uniform Zn deposition, the Zn||Zn symmetric cells show stable plating/stripping behavior and long‐term cycle stability.」** — 该方法不只调控凝胶力学，还通过改善锌离子溶剂化结构和引导均匀沉积来抑制枝晶，这是电化学稳定性的关键设计点喵~

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The as‐obtained hydrogel exhibits excellent mechanical properties, fatigue resistance, high Zn‐ion conductivity (38.2 mS cm−1), good adhesion (19.1 kPa), and ultra‐low freezing point (−97 °C).」** → 这组数据表明材料同时实现了力学强度、抗疲劳、离子传输、自黏附与极端抗冻，多目标性能不是单点优化而是整体平衡喵~
- **[原文]「Due to the alterdentate ligands help to improve the zinc ion solvation structure and guide uniform Zn deposition, the Zn||Zn symmetric cells show stable plating/stripping behavior and long‐term cycle stability.」** → 对称电池结果说明该凝胶不仅“能导电”，还真正改变了锌沉积行为，因此与“dendrite‐free”目标直接相关喵~
- **[原文]「The Zn||V2O5 full cells exhibit large capacity of 230.6 mAh g−1 and high capacity retention of 75.2% after 1000 cycles.」** → 全电池在 1000 次循环后仍保持 75.2% 容量保持率，且初始容量达到 230.6 mAh g−1，说明该电解质对实际器件输出与寿命都有支撑作用喵~
- **[原文]「Furthermore, flexible AZIBs operate stably even under extreme conditions including low temperature (−40 °C) and large bending angle (180°).」** → 材料性能成功传递到器件层面，在 −40 °C 和 180° 弯折这两类极端工况下仍可稳定运行，突出其环境适应性与柔性应用潜力喵~
- **[原文]「The mechanically damage‐resistant hydrogel can also be utilized in flexible strain sensors.」** → 该凝胶并非只服务于储能，还具备向柔性应变传感拓展的跨场景应用价值喵~

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出应变传感器的灵敏度、线性范围、响应/恢复时间，也未报告 Zn||Zn 对称电池和低温工况下的具体寿命数据，因此传感性能上限、低温长期可靠性与无枝晶机制的证据强度仍需结合正文进一步确认喵~

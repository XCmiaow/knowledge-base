## 摘要

> 这篇综述聚焦可在极端条件下工作的水凝胶及其在生物电子系统中的应用，核心问题是传统水凝胶在极端环境下会失去稳定性与功能性。[原文]「Traditional hydrogels face challenges when working under extreme conditions, causing a loss of stabilities and functionalities.」[原文]「This review provides a systematic overview of hydrogels capable of working under extreme conditions, with a focus on their applications in bioelectronic systems.」
>
> 文中把相关水凝胶归纳为抗机械损伤、抗脱落、抗溶胀、抗冻结和抗异物反应等方向，并进一步引入材料开发与结构设计两类实现策略。[原文]「These hydrogels are summarized into categories of anti‐mechanical damage, anti‐detachment, anti‐swelling, anti‐freezing, and anti‐foreign body response.」[原文]「Strategies including material development and structural design that can endow hydrogels with the above extreme properties are introduced.」
>
> 结尾部分把讨论推进到极端水凝胶生物电子器件与系统的当前挑战和新机会。[原文]「Finally, current challenges and new opportunities in developing extreme hydrogel bioelectronic devices and systems are discussed.」

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「This review provides a systematic overview of hydrogels capable of working under extreme conditions, with a focus on their applications in bioelectronic systems.」** — 文章的核心贡献是把“可在极端条件下工作的水凝胶”作为统一综述对象，并把落点明确放在生物电子系统应用上。

## 方法

- **[原文]「These hydrogels are summarized into categories of anti‐mechanical damage, anti‐detachment, anti‐swelling, anti‐freezing, and anti‐foreign body response. Strategies including material development and structural design that can endow hydrogels with the above extreme properties are introduced.」** — 方法上先按极端性能类别组织文献，再从材料开发与结构设计两个维度整理赋能策略。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「Traditional hydrogels face challenges when working under extreme conditions, causing a loss of stabilities and functionalities.」** → 关键发现之一是领域的基础瓶颈已被明确界定为极端条件下稳定性与功能性的同步受损。
- **[原文]「These hydrogels are summarized into categories of anti‐mechanical damage, anti‐detachment, anti‐swelling, anti‐freezing, and anti‐foreign body response.」** → 关键发现之二是作者将极端工况需求具体落实为抗机械损伤、抗脱落、抗溶胀、抗冻结和抗异物反应几个方向。
- **[原文]「Finally, current challenges and new opportunities in developing extreme hydrogel bioelectronic devices and systems are discussed.」** → 关键发现之三是研究重点已不只停留在材料层面，而是延伸到器件与系统层面的挑战与机会。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要强调这是综述性工作，但没有给出具体性能指标、实验条件或横向对比数据，因此仅凭摘要无法判断不同策略在实际生物电子场景中的性能边界与优先级。

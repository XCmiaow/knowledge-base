## 摘要

> [原文]「Hybrid nanocomposite hydrogels consist of the homogeneous incorporation of nano‐objects in a hydrogel matrix.」该文将杂化纳米复合水凝胶定义为纳米对象均匀嵌入水凝胶基质的体系。  
> [原文]「The latter, whether made of natural or synthetic materials, possesses a microporous, soft structure that makes it an ideal host for a variety of polymer and lipid‐based nano‐objects as well as metal‐ and silica‐based ones.」与 [原文]「This review aims to provide an overview of the state of the art in hybrid hydrogels highlighting key aspects that make them a promising choice for a variety of biomedical applications.」表明，微孔且柔软的水凝胶网络可容纳多类纳米对象，并因此成为多种生物医学应用的有前景平台。  
> [原文]「Strategies for the preparation of hybrid hydrogels are discussed by covering the selection of individual components.」与 [原文]「The review will also explore the physico‐chemical and rheological characterization of these materials, which is essential for understanding their structure and function, ultimately satisfying specifications for the intended use.」说明本文不仅综述制备策略，也强调通过理化与流变表征理解结构-功能关系并对接应用需求。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「This review aims to provide an overview of the state of the art in hybrid hydrogels highlighting key aspects that make them a promising choice for a variety of biomedical applications.」** — 核心贡献是对杂化水凝胶领域现状进行系统综述，并提炼其面向多类生物医学应用的关键优势。
- **[原文]「By carefully choosing the composition and the proportions of the different constituents, hybrid hydrogels can display a wide array of properties, from simple enhancement of mechanical characteristics to specific bioactivity.」** — 文章强调该体系的价值不只是“加入纳米材料”，而是通过组分与配比设计实现从力学增强到特定生物活性的可调性能空间。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Strategies for the preparation of hybrid hydrogels are discussed by covering the selection of individual components.」** — 方法论主线是从“组分选择”切入梳理杂化水凝胶的制备策略。
- **[原文]「The review will also explore the physico‐chemical and rheological characterization of these materials, which is essential for understanding their structure and function, ultimately satisfying specifications for the intended use.」** — 分析框架覆盖理化表征与流变表征，用于建立结构、功能与目标应用规格之间的联系。
- **[原文]「Successful examples of biomedical applications will also be presented, and the main challenges to be met will be discussed」** — 综述结构还包括应用案例归纳与挑战讨论，形成“设计-表征-应用-挑战”的完整评述链条。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The latter, whether made of natural or synthetic materials, possesses a microporous, soft structure that makes it an ideal host for a variety of polymer and lipid‐based nano‐objects as well as metal‐ and silica‐based ones.」** → 关键认识是水凝胶基质本身的微孔柔软特征，为聚合物、脂质、金属及二氧化硅等不同类型纳米对象提供了兼容宿主。
- **[原文]「By carefully choosing the composition and the proportions of the different constituents, hybrid hydrogels can display a wide array of properties, from simple enhancement of mechanical characteristics to specific bioactivity.」** → 关键结论是性能来源于可设计的组分与配比，而非单一纳米填料效应，因此该体系具备高度可调性。

# High Fire‐Safety and Multifunctional Eutectogel for Flexible Quasi‐Solid‐State Supercapacitors

## 摘要

> [原文]「The preparation of high‐performance and fire‐safe electrolytes for flexible quasi‐solid‐state supercapacitors is challenging.」论文开篇明确问题：面向柔性准固态超级电容器，同时做到高性能与防火安全的电解质制备并不容易。  
> [原文]「In this work, a novel fire‐safe and multifunctional deep eutectic solvent gel (DESG) is fabricated using acrylic acid and urea as hydrogen bond donors and choline chloride as a hydrogen bond acceptor.」作者用 acrylic acid、urea 和 choline chloride 构建出一种新型防火安全、多功能的深共晶溶剂凝胶（DESG）。  
> [原文]「DESG shows a high ionic conductivity (0.552 S m−1), good electrochemical performance (specific capacitance: 106.8 F g−1), and a wide operating temperature range (−20–90 °C), being a promising candidate for flexible solid‐state supercapacitors. Furthermore, it exhibits a good thermoelectric conversion capability (Seebeck coefficient: 1.56 mV K−1), being ideal for thermoelectric capacitors and fire‐warning sensors. The prepared DESG rapidly self‐extinguishes after removal from fire, reaching a limiting oxygen index value of 38.0% and demonstrating its excellent flame retardancy. In addition, DESG has a good self‐healing performance (healing efficiency of 84.3%).」该材料同时给出 0.552 S m−1 离子电导率、106.8 F g−1 比电容、−20–90 °C 工作温域、1.56 mV K−1 Seebeck 系数、38.0% 极限氧指数和 84.3% 自愈效率，覆盖储能、热电和火灾预警相关场景。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「In this work, a novel fire‐safe and multifunctional deep eutectic solvent gel (DESG) is fabricated using acrylic acid and urea as hydrogen bond donors and choline chloride as a hydrogen bond acceptor.」** — 核心创新是做出一种兼具“防火安全”和“多功能”属性的 DESG，而不是只优化单一电化学指标。
- **[原文]「The work provides new insights into the preparation and application of high fire‐safety and multifunctional eutectogels for flexible quasi‐solid‐state supercapacitors.」** — 作者把这项工作定位为柔性准固态超级电容器用高防火安全多功能共晶凝胶的制备与应用思路扩展。

## 方法

- **[原文]「In this work, a novel fire‐safe and multifunctional deep eutectic solvent gel (DESG) is fabricated using acrylic acid and urea as hydrogen bond donors and choline chloride as a hydrogen bond acceptor.」** — 方法上以 acrylic acid 和 urea 作为氢键供体、以 choline chloride 作为氢键受体，制备深共晶溶剂凝胶 DESG。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「DESG shows a high ionic conductivity (0.552 S m−1), good electrochemical performance (specific capacitance: 106.8 F g−1), and a wide operating temperature range (−20–90 °C), being a promising candidate for flexible solid‐state supercapacitors.」** → 中文可译为：DESG 具有 0.552 S m−1 的高离子电导率、106.8 F g−1 的比电容，并可在 −20–90 °C 范围内工作，作者据此将其视为柔性固态超级电容器候选材料。
- **[原文]「Furthermore, it exhibits a good thermoelectric conversion capability (Seebeck coefficient: 1.56 mV K−1), being ideal for thermoelectric capacitors and fire‐warning sensors.」** → 中文可译为：该材料还具有 1.56 mV K−1 的 Seebeck 系数，作者将其对应到热电电容器和火灾预警传感器用途。
- **[原文]「The prepared DESG rapidly self‐extinguishes after removal from fire, reaching a limiting oxygen index value of 38.0% and demonstrating its excellent flame retardancy.」** → 中文可译为：DESG 离火后可快速自熄，极限氧指数达到 38.0%，表现出优异阻燃性。
- **[原文]「In addition, DESG has a good self‐healing performance (healing efficiency of 84.3%).」** → 中文可译为：DESG 还具备 84.3% 的自愈效率。

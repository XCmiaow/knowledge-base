## 摘要

> [原文]「Eutectogels, a family of emerging materials, integrate the characteristics of deep eutectic solvents (DES) and gels, presenting unique application potential in biomedical engineering.」共晶凝胶是一类将深共熔溶剂（DES）与凝胶特性整合起来的新兴材料，在生物医学工程中具有独特应用潜力。  
> [原文]「This review first introduces the composition of eutectogels and their unique properties generated by the interaction between DES and the gel matrix. Subsequently, the classifications and advantages of eutectogels are comprehensively discussed according to their cross‐linked preparation methods, including physical, chemical, and multiple cross‐linked.」本文先梳理共晶凝胶的组成、DES 与凝胶基体相互作用产生的独特性质，以及按物理、化学和多重交联划分的制备分类与优势。  
> [原文]「Then, the eutectogel's application prospects in biomedical engineering are elaborated in detail, including their application in drug delivery to improve drug stability and precise control release, enhancing curative effects and reducing side effects. Also, the functional utilization in tissue engineering, biosensing, bioadhesives, and related biomedical domains are comprehensively explored. Finally, existing challenges and prospective research directions of eutectogels are delineated.」文章随后聚焦其在药物递送、组织工程、生物传感、生物黏附等生物医学场景中的用途，并以现存挑战和未来研究方向收束全文。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「This review first introduces the composition of eutectogels and their unique properties generated by the interaction between DES and the gel matrix.」** — 文章的第一项贡献是把“材料组成”与“DES-凝胶基体相互作用导致的独特性质”作为理解共晶凝胶的起点。
- **[原文]「Subsequently, the classifications and advantages of eutectogels are comprehensively discussed according to their cross‐linked preparation methods, including physical, chemical, and multiple cross‐linked.」** — 文章的第二项贡献是按交联制备路线系统整理共晶凝胶，并明确覆盖物理、化学和多重交联三类框架。
- **[原文]「Then, the eutectogel's application prospects in biomedical engineering are elaborated in detail... Also, the functional utilization in tissue engineering, biosensing, bioadhesives, and related biomedical domains are comprehensively explored.」** — 文章的第三项贡献是把材料性质与药物递送、组织工程、生物传感、生物黏附等应用场景对接，形成面向生物医学工程的综述视角。

## 方法

- **[原文]「the classifications and advantages of eutectogels are comprehensively discussed according to their cross‐linked preparation methods, including physical, chemical, and multiple cross‐linked」** — 这篇综述以“交联制备方法”为主线组织文献，属于按制备策略分层梳理的综述写法。
- **[原文]「By analyzing the properties of eutectogels, such as mechanical performance, thermal properties, conductivity, injectability, and their structure‐function relationships, the application potential of eutectogels is overviewed.」** — 文章进一步以“性质分析 + 结构-功能关系”为桥梁，把机械性能、热性质、电导率和可注射性等指标连接到应用潜力评估。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「By analyzing the properties of eutectogels, such as mechanical performance, thermal properties, conductivity, injectability, and their structure‐function relationships, the application potential of eutectogels is overviewed.」** → 共晶凝胶的应用潜力主要围绕机械性能、热性质、电导率、可注射性及其结构-功能关系展开。[推断] 摘要未提供具体实验数值，因此这里的“发现”更偏向关键性能维度的归纳而非定量比较。
- **[原文]「including their application in drug delivery to improve drug stability and precise control release, enhancing curative effects and reducing side effects. Also, the functional utilization in tissue engineering, biosensing, bioadhesives, and related biomedical domains are comprehensively explored.」** → 在应用层面，摘要强调药物递送可用于提升药物稳定性与精准控释，并扩展到组织工程、生物传感、生物黏附及相关生物医学领域。[推断] 摘要没有给出百分比、温度或性能提升幅度，说明当前摘要重心在应用版图总结而非单项效果量化。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 基于[原文]「the latest progress of eutectogels and their applications in the biomedical field remain to be summarized and refined」与[原文]「Finally, existing challenges and prospective research directions of eutectogels are delineated.」，可推知该领域虽然应用面广，但标准化评价、不同交联体系的横向对比以及向实际生物医学转化所需的证据链仍待进一步细化。

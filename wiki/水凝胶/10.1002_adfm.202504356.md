## 摘要

> [原文]「This review systematically summarizes recent advances in long‐term moisturizing hydrogels under ambient conditions」本文是一篇综述，系统梳理了环境条件下长效保湿水凝胶的最新进展。  
> [原文]「focusing on the mechanisms for inhibiting free‐water evaporation within hydrogels」与[原文]「from the macroscopic to the molecular scale」文章主线是解释水凝胶内部自由水蒸发抑制机制，并按宏观到分子尺度组织保湿调控策略。  
> [原文]「The review also discusses potential applications and identifies knowledge gaps, offering future research directions」与[原文]「a multiscale design strategy is emphasized」全文最终落脚于多尺度设计思路，同时总结应用前景、知识空白与后续研究方向。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Based on evaporation thermodynamics theory, the review clarifies the intrinsic relationship between free water/bound water states and evaporation rates.」** — 文章的核心理论贡献是把自由水/束缚水状态与蒸发速率直接关联起来，用同一框架解释保湿问题。
- **[原文]「Strategies to enhance water retention include: 1) physical barrier encapsulation, such as hydrophobic polymer membranes or shell structures; 2) network structure optimization, like interpenetrating or dual‐network designs to improve water‐holding capacity and regulate pore distribution; 3) molecular‐level control, incorporating hydrophilic components (e.g., glycerol, ionic liquids) via hydrogen bonding to manipulate water states.」** — 文章把增强保水性的策略归纳为 3 类，分别对应物理屏障、网络结构和分子层级调控。
- **[原文]「Notably, eutectogels using low‐volatility deep eutectic solvents outperform traditional water‐based systems, achieving excellent mechanical performance and long‐term moisturization.」** — 文章特别强调以低挥发性深共熔溶剂构建的共晶凝胶，认为其在力学性能和长期保湿方面优于传统水基体系。

## 方法

- **[原文]「This review systematically summarizes recent advances in long‐term moisturizing hydrogels under ambient conditions」** — 研究类型是系统性综述，目标是整合该方向的近期进展。
- **[原文]「focusing on the mechanisms for inhibiting free‐water evaporation within hydrogels and elaborating on moisture‐regulation mechanisms and strategies from the macroscopic to the molecular scale」** — 文章以“抑制自由水蒸发”为核心问题组织文献，并采用从宏观到分子尺度的分析路径。
- **[原文]「Based on evaporation thermodynamics theory」** 与 **[原文]「incorporating hydrophilic components (e.g., glycerol, ionic liquids) via hydrogen bonding to manipulate water states」** — 其分析方法一端依托蒸发热力学理论，另一端落实到氢键等分子作用对水状态的调控。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「Strategies to enhance water retention include: 1) physical barrier encapsulation, such as hydrophobic polymer membranes or shell structures; 2) network structure optimization, like interpenetrating or dual‐network designs to improve water‐holding capacity and regulate pore distribution; 3) molecular‐level control, incorporating hydrophilic components (e.g., glycerol, ionic liquids) via hydrogen bonding to manipulate water states.」** → 关键发现是保湿增强策略可以被完整归纳为 3 条技术路线，而且每条路线都对应不同尺度的调控对象。
- **[原文]「Hydrogels are soft‐wet materials with unique properties and multiscale network structures. However, traditional hydrogels suffer from water evaporation under ambient conditions, leading to structural changes and functional decline.」** → 文章首先明确问题背景：环境蒸发会导致传统水凝胶发生结构变化和功能衰退，因此长期保湿是材料走向实际应用的前提。
- **[原文]「eutectogels using low‐volatility deep eutectic solvents outperform traditional water‐based systems, achieving excellent mechanical performance and long‐term moisturization.」** → 在体系比较上，共晶凝胶被视为相对传统水基体系更有前景的方向。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要仅以 [原文]「potential applications」和 [原文]「knowledge gaps」概述应用与空白，未给出具体应用场景、失效模式或评价指标，因此需要正文补足不同方案的适用边界。
- **[推断]** 摘要强调 [原文]「a multiscale design strategy is emphasized」并同时列出 1)、2)、3) 三类策略，这意味着后续研究价值很可能在于把物理屏障、网络结构和分子调控做跨尺度协同，而不是孤立优化单一层级。

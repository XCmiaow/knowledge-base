## 摘要

> [原文]「Aqueous zinc‐ion batteries (AZIBs) based on hydrogel electrolytes are considered promising flexible power supplies owing to their intrinsic safety, competent volumetric energy density, and eco‐friendliness.」该研究将基于水凝胶电解质的水系锌离子电池定位为有前景的柔性电源，原因是其兼具本征安全性、较高体积能量密度与环境友好性。
>
> [原文]「However, severe mechanical deterioration of the hydrogel electrolytes caused by insufficient inter‐component contact, zinc (Zn) dendrites, and freezing prevents their commercialization.」作者指出这类电解质的商业化瓶颈在于由组分接触不足、锌枝晶和冻结共同引发的严重力学劣化。
>
> [原文]「Herein, it is found that, by doping a trace of Fe3+ ions to afford Fe3+‐carboxylate supramolecular interaction, the practicality of an archetypal cellulose nanofiber‐reinforced hydrogel electrolyte is significantly improved in a couple of aspects, including three and eight times increase in tensile strength and toughness without loss of ion conducting ability (up to 32 mS cm−1) and being room‐temperature self‐healable and strongly adhesive to various battery components. Together with the use of an anti‐freezing mixed Zn salt, the resulting hydrogel electrolyte is able to deliver ultrahigh Zn cycling reversibility (averaging 99.4%), the great cyclability of AZIBs (a high specific capacity of 180 mAh g−1 and capacity retention of 81%), and render the batteries operable under severe abuse conditions of 180° folding, exposure to liquid nitrogen, and cutting–rehealing cycles. This work unlocks the enormous potential of Fe3+‐carboxylate chemistry in the development of self‐healable, anti‐freezing, and extreme‐environment‐adaptable gel electrolytes for flexible energy storage devices.」其核心思路是用微量 Fe3+ 引入 Fe3+‐羧酸盐超分子相互作用，并结合抗冻混合锌盐，在不牺牲导电性的前提下同时增强力学性能、自愈性、黏附性与极端环境适应性。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「This work unlocks the enormous potential of Fe3+‐carboxylate chemistry in the development of self‐healable, anti‐freezing, and extreme‐environment‐adaptable gel electrolytes for flexible energy storage devices.」** — 贡献不只是单点性能提升，而是把 Fe3+‐羧酸盐化学明确为一条可同时统筹自愈、抗冻和极端环境适配的凝胶电解质设计路线。

## 方法

- **[原文]「by doping a trace of Fe3+ ions to afford Fe3+‐carboxylate supramolecular interaction」** — 方法核心是在纤维素纳米纤维增强水凝胶中引入微量 Fe3+，利用 Fe3+‐羧酸盐超分子配位重构网络相互作用。
- **[原文]「Together with the use of an anti‐freezing mixed Zn salt」** — 电解质配方还叠加了抗冻混合锌盐，用来补足低温工作能力并与配位网络协同。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「including three and eight times increase in tensile strength and toughness without loss of ion conducting ability (up to 32 mS cm−1) and being room‐temperature self‐healable and strongly adhesive to various battery components.」** → 引入 Fe3+ 后，拉伸强度提升 3 倍、韧性提升 8 倍，且离子电导率仍可达 32 mS cm−1，同时具备室温自愈与对多种电池部件的强黏附能力，说明其不是以牺牲传输性能换取机械增强。
- **[原文]「the resulting hydrogel electrolyte is able to deliver ultrahigh Zn cycling reversibility (averaging 99.4%), the great cyclability of AZIBs (a high specific capacity of 180 mAh g−1 and capacity retention of 81%), and render the batteries operable under severe abuse conditions of 180° folding, exposure to liquid nitrogen, and cutting–rehealing cycles.」** → 在器件层面，该体系实现了平均 99.4% 的 Zn 循环可逆性、180 mAh g−1 的高比容量和 81% 的容量保持率，并且在 180° 折叠、液氮暴露和切割–愈合循环后仍能工作，证明其面向柔性与极端工况的实用性。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出 Fe3+ 的具体掺杂量、不同温度下的定量性能窗口、切割–愈合循环次数后的衰减曲线，也没有说明与其他金属配位体系的横向对比，因此最佳配方范围、机理普适性和长期可靠性仍需依赖正文与补充信息验证。

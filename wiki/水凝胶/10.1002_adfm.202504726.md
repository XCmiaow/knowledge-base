## 摘要

> [原文]「Aqueous zinc‐ion batteries (AZIBs) based on hydrogel electrolytes are considered promising flexible power supplies owing to their intrinsic safety, competent volumetric energy density, and eco‐friendliness.」水凝胶电解质型水系锌离子电池被定位为兼具本征安全性、体积能量密度与环境友好性的柔性供能方案。  
> [原文]「However, severe mechanical deterioration of the hydrogel electrolytes caused by insufficient inter‐component contact, zinc (Zn) dendrites, and freezing prevents their commercialization.」其商业化障碍被明确归因为组分接触不足、锌枝晶与冻结共同导致的严重机械劣化。  
> [原文]「Herein, it is found that, by doping a trace of Fe3+ ions to afford Fe3+‐carboxylate supramolecular interaction... This work unlocks the enormous potential of Fe3+‐carboxylate chemistry in the development of self‐healable, anti‐freezing, and extreme‐environment‐adaptable gel electrolytes for flexible energy storage devices.」本文通过微量 Fe3+ 引入 Fe3+‐羧酸盐超分子作用，并结合防冻混合锌盐，同时提升材料与器件层面的综合性能，目标是构建可自修复、抗冻且适应极端环境的柔性储能电解质。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「by doping a trace of Fe3+ ions to afford Fe3+‐carboxylate supramolecular interaction, the practicality of an archetypal cellulose nanofiber‐reinforced hydrogel electrolyte is significantly improved in a couple of aspects」** — 核心贡献是在纤维素纳米纤维增强水凝胶这一原型体系中，用 Fe3+‐羧酸盐配位把“实用性提升”定义为多性能同步优化，而非单指标改进。
- **[原文]「including three and eight times increase in tensile strength and toughness without loss of ion conducting ability (up to 32 mS cm−1) and being room‐temperature self‐healable and strongly adhesive to various battery components.」** — 该工作把力学增强、高离子传导、室温自修复与对电池多组分的强黏附耦合到同一电解质设计中。
- **[原文]「Together with the use of an anti‐freezing mixed Zn salt, the resulting hydrogel electrolyte is able to deliver ultrahigh Zn cycling reversibility... and render the batteries operable under severe abuse conditions」** — 贡献不仅停留在材料表征，还延伸到防冻电解质配方与电池级极端工况可用性验证。

## 方法

- **[原文]「by doping a trace of Fe3+ ions to afford Fe3+‐carboxylate supramolecular interaction」** — 方法核心是掺入微量 Fe3+，构建 Fe3+‐羧酸盐超分子配位作用。
- **[原文]「the practicality of an archetypal cellulose nanofiber‐reinforced hydrogel electrolyte」** — 被改造的对象是原型纤维素纳米纤维增强水凝胶电解质。
- **[原文]「Together with the use of an anti‐freezing mixed Zn salt」** — 体系进一步叠加防冻混合锌盐，以支持低温与极端环境适配。
- **[原文]「being room‐temperature self‐healable and strongly adhesive to various battery components」** — 方法目标包含构建室温自修复能力与对多种电池部件的强黏附界面。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「including three and eight times increase in tensile strength and toughness without loss of ion conducting ability (up to 32 mS cm−1)」** → 拉伸强度提升 3 倍、韧性提升 8 倍，且离子传导能力仍可达 32 mS cm−1。
- **[原文]「being room‐temperature self‐healable and strongly adhesive to various battery components」** → 材料具备室温自修复特性，并且能够强黏附于多种电池组件。
- **[原文]「deliver ultrahigh Zn cycling reversibility (averaging 99.4%)」** → Zn 循环可逆性平均达到 99.4%。
- **[原文]「the great cyclability of AZIBs (a high specific capacity of 180 mAh g−1 and capacity retention of 81%)」** → AZIBs 实现 180 mAh g−1 的较高比容量，并保持 81% 的容量保持率。
- **[原文]「render the batteries operable under severe abuse conditions of 180° folding, exposure to liquid nitrogen, and cutting–rehealing cycles」** → 电池在 180° 折叠、液氮暴露以及切断–愈合循环等严苛滥用条件下仍可工作。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出 Fe3+ 具体掺杂量、防冻 mixed Zn salt 的组成、循环测试边界条件与失效机理，因此暂时无法判断该方案的参数敏感性、长期稳定性和与其他 AZIB 水凝胶电解质的横向优势是否稳固。

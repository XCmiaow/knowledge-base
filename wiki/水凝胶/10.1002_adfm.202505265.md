# Self-Powered Ultra-Stretchable Organo-Hydrogel Sensors with Excellent Stability Based on a Solvent Replacement Strategy

**中文标题**: 基于溶剂置换策略的具有优异稳定性的自供能超拉伸有机-水凝胶传感器

## 摘要

> [原文] "Herein, a dynamic hydrophobically associated organo-hydrogel network, which is composed of polymer chain backbone (acrylamide and lauryl methacrylate), dispersant (sodium dodecyl sulfate), conductive filler (lithium bis(trifluoromethyl)sulfonimide), and crosslinker (containing dynamic imine bonds) is constructed." 该工作通过疏水缔合、导电填料与动态亚胺键交联共同构建了有机-水凝胶导电网络喵~
>
> [原文] "Based on these performances, they are used as the working electrode of the triboelectric nanogenerators... A self-powered sensor is also constructed, which can realize the function of material recognition with the assistance of machine learning." 该材料进一步被用于摩擦纳米发电机工作电极，并延伸到自供能传感与机器学习辅助材料识别场景喵~

## 核心贡献

- **[原文] "Then, through a solvent replacement strategy of glycerol, the conductive organo-hydrogels are obtained with excellent mechanical properties... outstanding environmental stability... and room-temperature self-healing ability"** 文章的核心贡献是用甘油溶剂置换把力学性能、环境稳定性与室温自愈能力整合到同一类导电有机-水凝胶中喵~

## 方法

- **[原文] "a dynamic hydrophobically associated organo-hydrogel network... is composed of polymer chain backbone (acrylamide and lauryl methacrylate), dispersant (sodium dodecyl sulfate), conductive filler (lithium bis(trifluoromethyl)sulfonimide), and crosslinker (containing dynamic imine bonds)... through a solvent replacement strategy of glycerol"** 方法上先构建含丙烯酰胺、甲基丙烯酸月桂酯、十二烷基硫酸钠、双(三氟甲磺酰)亚胺锂和动态亚胺键交联剂的网络，再通过甘油置换获得目标有机-水凝胶喵~

## 关键发现

- **[原文] "excellent mechanical properties (1.21 MPa strength, 12.87 MJ m−3 toughness, and 3501% ultra-stretching), outstanding environmental stability (work stably in a wide range from −20 to 60 °C), and room-temperature self-healing ability (self-healing efficiency over 90%)"** 该材料同时实现了1.21 MPa强度、12.87 MJ m−3韧性、3501%超拉伸、−20到60 °C稳定工作以及超过90%的室温自愈效率喵~
- **[原文] "an output voltage of 127 V, a power density of 205 mW m−2, and the ability to maintain a stable output over 10 000 cycles"** 作为摩擦纳米发电机工作电极时，器件可输出127 V电压、205 mW m−2功率密度，并在10 000次循环后仍保持稳定输出喵~
- **[原文] "A self-powered sensor is also constructed, which can realize the function of material recognition with the assistance of machine learning."** 该体系不仅能发电，还被推进到机器学习辅助的材料识别型自供能传感器喵~

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要强调了力学、自愈和发电性能，但没有交代生物相容性、长期佩戴工况、机器学习识别精度与数据规模，因此后续需要补足面向实际可穿戴应用的系统验证喵~

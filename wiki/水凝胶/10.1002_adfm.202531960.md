## 摘要

> 这是一篇综述文章，主线是梳理水凝胶为何适合做植入式医疗器械、如何提升其关键性能，以及其在治疗和诊断中的代表性应用进展 **[原文]「Hydrogels exhibit a range of advantageous properties, including high mechanical strength, electrical conductivity, biocompatibility, and strong adhesion in wet environments that render them highly suitable for use as implantable medical devices. In this review, we systematically introduce the enhancement strategies for the key performance of hydrogels.」**  
> 文章覆盖的应用版图包括组织工程、组织黏合、生物传感以及能量采集与存储，并将落脚点放在临床转化所面临的挑战与未来方向 **[原文]「We then sort out the cutting‐edge cases and core progress of hydrogels in biomedical applications in disease therapy and diagnosis, including tissue engineering, tissue adhesives, biosensing, and energy harvesting and storage. Finally, we critically analyze the key challenges and future development directions faced in the clinical transformation process of this field」**

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「In this review, we systematically introduce the enhancement strategies for the key performance of hydrogels. We then sort out the cutting‐edge cases and core progress of hydrogels in biomedical applications in disease therapy and diagnosis」** — 核心贡献是把“性能增强策略”与“生物医学应用进展”放在同一综述框架中，形成从材料性能到器械应用的完整叙述链条。

## 方法

- **[原文]「In this review, we systematically introduce the enhancement strategies for the key performance of hydrogels. We then sort out the cutting‐edge cases and core progress of hydrogels in biomedical applications」** — 方法上采用系统性综述路径，先归纳水凝胶关键性能的增强策略，再按应用场景整理前沿案例与核心进展。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「Hydrogels exhibit a range of advantageous properties, including high mechanical strength, electrical conductivity, biocompatibility, and strong adhesion in wet environments」** → 作者强调水凝胶适用于植入式医疗器械的基础，在于其同时具备高机械强度、电导性、生物相容性和湿环境强黏附性。
- **[原文]「including tissue engineering, tissue adhesives, biosensing, and energy harvesting and storage」** → 已形成较明确的四类重点应用方向：组织工程、组织黏合、生物传感、能量采集与存储。
- **[原文]「aiming to promote the efficient transformation of hydrogel‐based implantable medical devices from basic research to clinical application」** → 文章的最终目标不是单纯总结研究现状，而是推动该方向从基础研究走向临床应用。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有提供任何定量性能指标、对比基线或临床结果，因此无法仅凭摘要判断不同增强策略和不同应用方向的相对成熟度与优先级。
- **[推断]** 既然作者将“clinical transformation process”列为重点挑战，后续深读时应重点查正文如何展开长期稳定性、体内安全性、制造一致性与监管路径等临床转化瓶颈。

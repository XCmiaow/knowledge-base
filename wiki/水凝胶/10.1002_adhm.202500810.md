# pH and Glucose Dual‐Responsive Hydrogels Promoted Diabetic Wound Healing by Remodeling the Wound Microenvironment

**中文标题**: pH 与葡萄糖双响应水凝胶通过重塑创面微环境促进糖尿病伤口愈合

## 摘要

> [原文]「The microenvironment of diabetic wounds is complicated and characterized by hyperglycemia, hyperinflammation, persistent infection, hypoxia, and ischemia, making wound restoration and healing extremely challenging.」这篇工作首先界定了糖尿病创面的核心难点，即高糖、高炎症、持续感染、缺氧与缺血并存，导致创面修复极其困难。
>
> [原文]「In this study, a pH/glucose dual‐responsive hydrogel based on phenylboric acid‐modified carboxymethyl chitosan (CMCSPBA), aldehyde‐terminated polyethylene glycol (PEGCHO), and polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) has been developed for diabetic wound treatment via Schiff base and phenylboric ester interactions. Glucose oxidase (GOX), catalase (CAT), and deferoxamine mesylate (DFO) are incorporated into the hydrogel to endow it with multi‐functionality.」作者构建了一个由 CMCSPBA、PEGCHO、PVA 组成并负载 GOX、CAT、DFO 的 pH/葡萄糖双响应水凝胶，用于糖尿病伤口治疗。
>
> [原文]「In the hyperglycemic environment of diabetic wounds, a benign feedback loop is formed through the synergistic action of each component of the hydrogel, which enables the reshaping of the microenvironment of diabetic wounds by adjusting the pH and glucose, alleviating oxidative stress and hypoxia, regulating the inflammatory response, inhibiting bacterial infection, and promoting angiogenesis, thus accelerating diabetic wound healing in streptozotocin (STZ)‐induced diabetic mice.」其核心逻辑是通过组分协同形成良性反馈回路，同时调节 pH 与葡萄糖、缓解氧化应激和缺氧、调控炎症、抑菌并促进血管生成，最终加速 STZ 诱导糖尿病小鼠的伤口愈合。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「In the hyperglycemic environment of diabetic wounds, a benign feedback loop is formed through the synergistic action of each component of the hydrogel, which enables the reshaping of the microenvironment of diabetic wounds by adjusting the pH and glucose, alleviating oxidative stress and hypoxia, regulating the inflammatory response, inhibiting bacterial infection, and promoting angiogenesis, thus accelerating diabetic wound healing in streptozotocin (STZ)‐induced diabetic mice.」** — 核心贡献不只是“做了一个水凝胶”，而是提出了一个面向糖尿病创面复杂微环境的系统性重塑方案，把代谢调控、抗氧化、抗炎、抗菌和促血管生成整合到同一材料平台中。

## 方法

- **[原文]「In this study, a pH/glucose dual‐responsive hydrogel based on phenylboric acid‐modified carboxymethyl chitosan (CMCSPBA), aldehyde‐terminated polyethylene glycol (PEGCHO), and polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) has been developed for diabetic wound treatment via Schiff base and phenylboric ester interactions. Glucose oxidase (GOX), catalase (CAT), and deferoxamine mesylate (DFO) are incorporated into the hydrogel to endow it with multi‐functionality.」** — 方法上通过 Schiff base 与 phenylboric ester 相互作用构建 pH/葡萄糖双响应网络，再加入 GOX、CAT、DFO 赋予材料多功能响应能力。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The microenvironment of diabetic wounds is complicated and characterized by hyperglycemia, hyperinflammation, persistent infection, hypoxia, and ischemia, making wound restoration and healing extremely challenging.」** → 研究针对的是一个多重病理因素叠加的难治场景，因此其“微环境重塑”定位具有明确问题导向。
- **[原文]「In the hyperglycemic environment of diabetic wounds, a benign feedback loop is formed through the synergistic action of each component of the hydrogel, which enables the reshaping of the microenvironment of diabetic wounds by adjusting the pH and glucose, alleviating oxidative stress and hypoxia, regulating the inflammatory response, inhibiting bacterial infection, and promoting angiogenesis, thus accelerating diabetic wound healing in streptozotocin (STZ)‐induced diabetic mice.」** → 摘要给出的关键结果是，该水凝胶在 STZ 诱导糖尿病小鼠中实现了多维微环境改善，并最终加速伤口愈合。
- **[推断]** 摘要未提供伤口闭合率、时间缩短比例、pH 变化范围、葡萄糖浓度变化、温度条件或统计学指标，因此目前只能确认作用方向，不能仅凭摘要判断效应强度。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 目前摘要层面的证据强调“多功能协同”与“加速愈合”，但没有拆分 GOX、CAT、DFO 各自的独立贡献，正文需要重点查看对照组设计是否足以支持“良性反馈回路”这一机制判断。
- **[推断]** 摘要只明确提到 streptozotocin (STZ)‐induced diabetic mice，外推到更复杂临床慢性创面前，还需要结合正文确认其长期生物相容性、材料稳定性和感染模型表现。

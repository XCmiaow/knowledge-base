## 摘要

> [原文]「Diabetic oral wound healing is significantly impaired due to chronic inflammation, oxidative stress, and immune dysfunction, often resulting in delayed recovery or severe infections.」文章指出，糖尿病口腔创面愈合受慢性炎症、氧化应激和免疫功能紊乱显著阻碍，临床后果是恢复延迟甚至严重感染。

> [原文]「Hydrogels have emerged as promising biomaterials for treating diabetic oral wounds due to their excellent biocompatibility, high drug‐loading capacity, strong adhesion, and tunable degradation properties.」[原文]「This review examines recent advancements in hydrogel‐based therapies for diabetic oral wound healing, with a particular focus on their applications in mucosal repair, periodontal tissue regeneration, and bone defect restoration.」本文将水凝胶定位为糖尿病口腔创面治疗的有前景生物材料，并综述其在黏膜修复、牙周组织再生和骨缺损修复中的应用进展。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Additionally, the antibacterial, anti‐inflammatory, antioxidant, and immune‐modulating properties of hydrogels are discussed, alongside their adhesion, mechanical strength, and controlled drug release behavior in complex oral environments.」** — 文章的核心贡献是把水凝胶的功能机制与口腔复杂环境中的材料行为放在同一框架下讨论，强调其抗菌、抗炎、抗氧化、免疫调节及控释等综合治疗价值。

## 方法

- **[原文]「This review examines recent advancements in hydrogel‐based therapies for diabetic oral wound healing」** — 这是一篇综述性论文，方法上不是开展单一实验，而是系统梳理糖尿病口腔创面水凝胶治疗的最新研究进展。
- **[原文]「with a particular focus on their applications in mucosal repair, periodontal tissue regeneration, and bone defect restoration」** — 综述按应用场景展开，重点覆盖口腔黏膜修复、牙周组织再生和骨缺损修复三类问题。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「with a particular focus on their applications in mucosal repair, periodontal tissue regeneration, and bone defect restoration」** → 关键发现之一是，现有水凝胶治疗研究已经形成三个明确应用方向：黏膜修复、牙周组织再生、骨缺损修复。
- **[原文]「Despite their significant therapeutic potential, challenges remain regarding material degradation kinetics, drug release profiles, and potential cytotoxicity.」** → 关键发现之二是，水凝胶虽有显著治疗潜力，但材料降解动力学、药物释放曲线与潜在细胞毒性仍是转化瓶颈。
- **[原文]「Future research is expected to focus on the development of multifunctional stimuli‐responsive hydrogels and AI‐enabled hydrogel systems」** → 关键发现之三是，未来方向正在转向多功能刺激响应型水凝胶与 AI 赋能系统，以提升治疗精度和有效性。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要明确指出挑战集中在降解动力学、释药行为和潜在细胞毒性，但没有给出不同水凝胶体系的对比标准，因此当前证据更适合做方向性判断，不足以直接支持临床方案优选。
- **[推断]** 作为综述摘要，文中未提供具体定量疗效指标、百分比或温度条件，说明后续深读正文时应重点补足材料性能参数、动物/临床模型差异与真实疗效数据。
- **[原文]「Future research is expected to focus on the development of multifunctional stimuli‐responsive hydrogels and AI‐enabled hydrogel systems, providing more precise and effective solutions for the treatment of diabetic oral wounds.」** — 展望上，研究将进一步走向多功能、智能响应和 AI 辅助设计，以适应糖尿病口腔创面的复杂微环境。

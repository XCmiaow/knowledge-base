# Recent Progress in High‐Strength and Robust Regenerated Cellulose Materials

**中文标题**: 高强度与高韧性再生纤维素材料的最新进展

## 基础信息

| 属性 | 值 |
|------|-----|

| **期刊** | Advanced Materials |
| **作者** | H. Tu; Mengxiang Zhu; Bo Duan; Lina Zhang |
| **年份** | 2020 |

## 摘要

> [原文]「High‐strength petroleum‐based materials like plastics have been widely used in various fields, but their nonbiodegradability has caused serious pollution problems.」与[原文]「Cellulose, as the most abundant sustainable polymer, has a great chance to act as the ideal substitute for plastics due to its low cost, wide availability, biodegradability, etc.」本文从高强度石油基材料的污染问题切入，强调纤维素因低成本、来源广和可生物降解而具备替代塑料的潜力。  
> [原文]「Herein, the recent achievements for developing cellulose “green” solvents and regenerated cellulose materials with high strength via the “bottom‐up” route are presented.」与[原文]「The cellulose solvents, dissolving mechanism, and strategies for constructing the regenerated cellulose functional materials with high strength and performances, together with the current achievements and urgent challenges are summarized, and some perspectives are also proposed.」这是一篇综述，主线是绿色溶剂、纤维素溶解机制，以及通过“bottom‐up”路线构筑高强度再生纤维素功能材料的策略。  
> [原文]「Cellulose can be regenerated to produce films/membranes, hydrogels/aerogels, filaments/fibers, microspheres/beads, bioplastics, etc., which show potential applications in textiles, biomedicine, energy storage, packaging, etc.」与[原文]「Importantly, these cellulose‐based materials can be biodegraded in soil and oceans, reducing environmental pollution.」作者认为再生纤维素已经形成覆盖多种材料形态和应用场景的平台，并兼具环境降解优势。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Herein, the recent achievements for developing cellulose “green” solvents and regenerated cellulose materials with high strength via the “bottom‐up” route are presented.」** — 文章把“绿色溶剂开发”与“高强度再生纤维素材料构筑”合并为一个统一综述框架，明确了领域推进的主路径。
- **[原文]「The cellulose solvents, dissolving mechanism, and strategies for constructing the regenerated cellulose functional materials with high strength and performances, together with the current achievements and urgent challenges are summarized, and some perspectives are also proposed.」** — 核心价值不只是列举材料类型，而是把溶剂、机制、构筑策略、当前进展、紧迫挑战和前景整合起来，便于研究者定位问题链条。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Herein, the recent achievements for developing cellulose “green” solvents and regenerated cellulose materials with high strength via the “bottom‐up” route are presented.」** — 所谓方法主线不是单一实验技术，而是以“bottom‐up”路线为纲，从纤维素溶解到再生构筑高强度材料。
- **[原文]「The cellulose solvents, dissolving mechanism, and strategies for constructing the regenerated cellulose functional materials with high strength and performances」** — 作者关注的方法层级包括溶剂体系、溶解机制和功能材料构筑策略三层，说明性能提升依赖从分子到结构的连续设计。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「Cellulose can be regenerated to produce films/membranes, hydrogels/aerogels, filaments/fibers, microspheres/beads, bioplastics, etc., which show potential applications in textiles, biomedicine, energy storage, packaging, etc.」** → 关键发现是再生纤维素已不局限于单一形态，而是扩展到薄膜、水凝胶/气凝胶、纤维、微球和生物塑料，并进入纺织、生物医用、储能和包装等应用方向。
- **[原文]「Importantly, these cellulose‐based materials can be biodegraded in soil and oceans, reducing environmental pollution.」** → 与传统高强度石油基材料相比，再生纤维素的重要优势在于可在土壤和海洋中降解，从而直接对应其环境治理价值。
- **[原文]「The hope is that many environmentally friendly materials with good properties and low cost will be produced for commercial use」** → 作者把“good properties”和“low cost”并列，说明该领域的成功标准不仅是性能，还包括商业化可行性。
- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出具体强度、模量、韧性或降解速率等定量数据，因此这篇摘要层面的信息更适合用于把握研究版图，而不适合直接进行性能横向比较。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 由于摘要只给出综述范围与方向，没有报告统一的性能评价指标，说明当前领域虽然材料形态丰富，但跨体系比较标准仍可能不足。
- **[原文]「together with the current achievements and urgent challenges are summarized」** 与 **[原文]「The hope is that many environmentally friendly materials with good properties and low cost will be produced for commercial use」** — 展望聚焦于三件事：继续优化绿色溶剂体系、在保持高强度与高性能的同时控制成本、推动商业化落地。
- **[原文]「The near future will be an exciting era for high‐strength biodegradable and renewable materials.」** — 作者的总体判断是，高强度、可生物降解、可再生材料将在近期进入更快发展阶段。

# Additive Manufacturing of Molecular Architecture Encoded Stretchable Polyethylene Glycol Hydrogels and Elastomers

## 摘要

> [原文]「Polyethylene glycol (PEG) networks are widely used in biomedical applications and are emerging as solid‐state polymer electrolytes for next‐generation lithium batteries.」PEG 网络已广泛用于生物医学，并正在成为下一代锂电池固态聚合物电解质的候选材料。  
> [原文]「Here, DLP printable, highly stretchable foldable bottlebrush PEG networks are reported.」[原文]「The bottlebrush architecture enables high molecular weight PEG network strands that do not crystallize and remain elastic without solvents at room temperature.」本文报道了一种可 DLP 打印的高可拉伸折叠瓶刷 PEG 网络，其分子架构使网络链段在室温无溶剂条件下仍保持非晶与弹性。  
> [原文]「The resulting hydrogels and elastomers exhibit tissue‐like moduli ranging from ≈1 to ≈100 kPa and tensile breaking strains up to 1500%.」[原文]「The applications of bottlebrush PEG networks are demonstrated as matrices for highly stretchable and conductive solvent‐free polymer electrolytes at room temperature (≈900% strain and 1.2 mS cm−1), as well as resins for DLP printing of complex architectures, cytocompatible organ‐like geometries, functional devices, and multi‐material structures with seamless interface integration.」该体系兼具组织样力学性能与高伸长性，并展示了其在室温无溶剂聚合物电解质及复杂多材料 DLP 打印中的应用潜力。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「However, developing stretchable PEG networks, let alone making them 3D printable, remains a fundamental challenge.」[原文]「Here, DLP printable, highly stretchable foldable bottlebrush PEG networks are reported.」** — 工作直接瞄准“可拉伸 PEG 网络难以实现，更难做到 3D 打印”的瓶颈，并给出可 DLP 打印的瓶刷 PEG 网络方案。
- **[原文]「These networks are formed by rapid photopolymerization of low‐cost commercial chemicals in ambient air.」** — 体系依赖空气环境下的快速光聚合和低成本商用化学品，说明其制造路线具有较低工艺门槛。
- **[原文]「The bottlebrush architecture enables high molecular weight PEG network strands that do not crystallize and remain elastic without solvents at room temperature.」[原文]「Upon large deformation, the folded bottlebrush backbone unfolds to release stored length to enable extreme stretchability.」** — 文章的关键设计是用瓶刷分子架构同时解决 PEG 链段结晶与伸长受限问题，并通过主链展开释放储存长度来支撑极端拉伸。

## 方法

- **[原文]「These networks are formed by rapid photopolymerization of low‐cost commercial chemicals in ambient air.」** — 制备方法是基于低成本商用原料，在空气中进行快速光聚合，得到可光固化打印的网络。
- **[原文]「Leveraging additive manufacturing technologies, such as digital light processing (DLP)」[原文]「DLP printable, highly stretchable foldable bottlebrush PEG networks」** — 制造平台采用 DLP 增材制造，使该网络能够作为打印树脂进入三维结构构筑流程。
- **[原文]「The bottlebrush architecture enables high molecular weight PEG network strands that do not crystallize and remain elastic without solvents at room temperature.」[原文]「Upon large deformation, the folded bottlebrush backbone unfolds to release stored length to enable extreme stretchability.」** — 分子层面通过高分子量瓶刷网络链段抑制结晶，并利用折叠主链在大变形下展开的机制获得高延展性。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The resulting hydrogels and elastomers exhibit tissue‐like moduli ranging from ≈1 to ≈100 kPa and tensile breaking strains up to 1500%.」** → 该水凝胶与弹性体覆盖约 1 到 100 kPa 的组织样模量区间，同时断裂应变最高可达 1500%。
- **[原文]「highly stretchable and conductive solvent‐free polymer electrolytes at room temperature (≈900% strain and 1.2 mS cm−1)」** → 作为室温无溶剂聚合物电解质基体时，材料仍可实现约 900% 应变与 1.2 mS cm−1 电导率。
- **[原文]「resins for DLP printing of complex architectures, cytocompatible organ‐like geometries, functional devices, and multi‐material structures with seamless interface integration」** → 该体系不仅适用于单一材料打印，还支持复杂结构、细胞相容的器官样几何、功能器件以及无缝界面多材料结构。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** [原文]「at room temperature」[原文]「at room temperature (≈900% strain and 1.2 mS cm−1)」摘要重点报告了室温条件下的力学与电解质表现，但未给出循环耐久性、长期环境稳定性、体内表现或整电池验证，因此其实际服役可靠性和应用边界仍需进一步确认。

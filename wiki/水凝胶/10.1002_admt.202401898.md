# Flexible 3D‐Printed Cellulosic Constructs for EMI Shielding and Piezoresistive Sensing

## 摘要

> 这项工作针对3D打印CNF基材料在电子应用中“poor electrical conductivity and restricted mechanical flexibility”的瓶颈，提出将CNF三维打印骨架与导电聚合物通过CCVP耦合的方案。[原文]「However, the inherent limitations of 3D‐printed CNF‐based materials, such as poor electrical conductivity and restricted mechanical flexibility, pose barriers to their application in next‐generation electronics. The research addresses these challenges by integrating CNF‐based 3D printed frameworks with a conductive polymer via a process known as “cold chemical vapor polymerization” (CCVP).」
>
> 工艺上先进行“direct ink writing (DIW) of the CNF hydrogel”，再经“saturation with Fe3+ ions and freeze‐drying”形成离子嵌入骨架，随后利用CCVP在内部生成PEDOT导电网络。[原文]「The procedure initiates with the direct ink writing (DIW) of the CNF hydrogel, which then undergoes saturation with Fe3+ ions and freeze‐drying to produce ion‐embedded CNF frameworks. Subsequently, interconnected conductive pathways of poly(3,4‐ethylenedioxythiophene) (PEDOT) are generated within these structures using CCVP.」
>
> 最终材料同时实现“546 S m−1”导电率、“70% compressible”机械柔性，并在EMI屏蔽中表现出“0.71”吸收率和“3406.45 dB cm2 g−1”比屏蔽效能，还可作为高灵敏压阻传感器。[原文]「This methodology allows for precise customization of electrical conductivity, resulting in the production of highly conductive (546 S m−1) and mechanically flexible (70% compressible) patterned constructs. This advancement is highlighted by the development of grid‐based structures designed for electromagnetic interference (EMI) shields. These innovative shields demonstrate an absorbance of 0.71 and a specific EMI shielding effectiveness of 3406.45 dB cm2 g−1. Furthermore, these aerogels function as highly sensitive piezoresistive sensors, demonstrating the versatility of this sustainable approach for advancing wearable electronics and multifunctional technologies.」

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「The research addresses these challenges by integrating CNF‐based 3D printed frameworks with a conductive polymer via a process known as “cold chemical vapor polymerization” (CCVP).」** — 核心贡献是把可持续的CNF三维打印骨架与CCVP导电聚合策略结合，直接瞄准导电性和柔性这两个关键短板。
- **[原文]「This methodology allows for precise customization of electrical conductivity, resulting in the production of highly conductive (546 S m−1) and mechanically flexible (70% compressible) patterned constructs.」** — 文章强调该方法不仅能做出导电结构，还能精确调控电导，并同时保留高压缩形变能力。
- **[原文]「Furthermore, these aerogels function as highly sensitive piezoresistive sensors, demonstrating the versatility of this sustainable approach for advancing wearable electronics and multifunctional technologies.」** — 同一材料平台兼顾EMI屏蔽与压阻传感，说明其面向可穿戴电子和多功能器件具有平台型价值。

## 方法

- **[原文]「The procedure initiates with the direct ink writing (DIW) of the CNF hydrogel, which then undergoes saturation with Fe3+ ions and freeze‐drying to produce ion‐embedded CNF frameworks.」** — 方法前半段是先把CNF水凝胶通过DIW打印成形，再引入Fe3+并冻干，构建可供后续聚合的离子嵌入多孔骨架。
- **[原文]「Subsequently, interconnected conductive pathways of poly(3,4‐ethylenedioxythiophene) (PEDOT) are generated within these structures using CCVP.」** — 方法后半段用CCVP在骨架内部原位生成互连PEDOT导电通路，这是性能提升的关键结构步骤。
- **[原文]「This advancement is highlighted by the development of grid‐based structures designed for electromagnetic interference (EMI) shields.」** — 作者还将该工艺具体落实到栅格化结构设计，用于EMI屏蔽场景验证。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「highly conductive (546 S m−1) and mechanically flexible (70% compressible) patterned constructs」** → 材料同时达到546 S m−1电导率与70%可压缩性，说明导电性与柔性并未互相排斥。
- **[原文]「These innovative shields demonstrate an absorbance of 0.71 and a specific EMI shielding effectiveness of 3406.45 dB cm2 g−1.」** → 在EMI屏蔽应用中，0.71吸收率和3406.45 dB cm2 g−1比屏蔽效能表明其轻量化屏蔽潜力很强。
- **[原文]「Furthermore, these aerogels function as highly sensitive piezoresistive sensors」** → 除了屏蔽性能，这些气凝胶还能作为高灵敏压阻传感器，显示出结构电功能的一体化优势。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出压阻传感的定量指标、循环稳定性、频段范围、环境耐久性和长期服役数据，因此目前更像是概念验证，后续需要补足器件级可靠性与应用边界评估。
- **[推断]** 虽然强调了“precise customization of electrical conductivity”，但摘要未说明调控参数、分辨率上限和不同结构设计之间的性能代价，后续可围绕工艺窗口与结构-性能关系展开系统研究。

### 核心发现
- 文章报道 flexible 3D-printed cellulosic constructs，用于 EMI 屏蔽和压阻传感，说明纤维素体系已能在打印后同时承担结构和器件功能。
- 相比常见导电填料凝胶，这里更强调 cellulosic construct 的可成形性、柔性和多尺度导电网络构建。

### 方法亮点
核心方法是 3D 打印纤维素构筑体，再通过结构设计实现屏蔽与传感双功能。对本综述而言，这说明纤维素不仅能做基体，也能通过几何架构参与性能调控。

### 关键数据
- 可访问摘要确认两大应用指标是 EMI shielding 与 piezoresistive sensing。
- 当前未完整核到屏蔽效能、灵敏系数和循环耐久的具体数值。

### 与本综述（纤维素基宽温域水凝胶）的相关性
相关性较高，尤其适合支撑“纤维素基器件凝胶/构筑体”的应用部分。虽然它不以宽温域为主线，但对未来将宽温域凝胶接入柔性电子器件很有帮助。

### 局限与展望
宽温域稳定性并非文章重点。若后续研究把抗冻、耐热与 EMI/传感整合到同一纤维素打印体系，这条路线会非常强。

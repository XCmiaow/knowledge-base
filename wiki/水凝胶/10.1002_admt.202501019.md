# Hydrogel‐Based 3D‐Printable Stretchable Pressure Sensor

**中文标题**: 基于水凝胶的 3D 可打印可拉伸压力传感器

## 摘要

> [原文]「We introduce a 3D‐printable pressure sensor composed of carbon black‐functionalized double network granular hydrogels (CB‐DNGHs).」本文提出一种由炭黑功能化双网络颗粒水凝胶构成的 3D 可打印压力传感器。  
> [原文]「The granular structure of DNGHs enables localization of CB fillers in the interstitial spaces such that they reach percolation even if added at concentrations as low as 0.95 vol%, enabling them to combine piezoresistivity with toughness.」其关键机制是利用颗粒结构将炭黑限制在颗粒间隙中，使填料含量低至 0.95 vol% 也能形成渗流网络，从而兼顾压阻响应与韧性。  
> [原文]「We leverage this feature to 3D print a CB‐DNGH‐based shoe sole for gait analysis that can detect pressures as low as kPa to several 100 kPa.」作者进一步展示了用于步态分析的 3D 打印鞋底原型，检测范围覆盖 kPa 级到数百 kPa。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Conventional soft pressure sensors often entail concentrated fillers that tend to render them brittle, such that they risk failing if excessively strained.」** — 文章针对传统软压力传感器因高填料浓度而变脆、过度拉伸时易失效的问题，提出了兼顾可拉伸性与导电网络形成的新材料路线。
- **[原文]「The granular nature of the ink offers an additional advantage: it enables direct ink writing of materials with locally varying compositions and hence pressure sensing ranges.」** — 墨水的颗粒化特征不只改善导电填料分布，还支持局部配方可编程，从材料层面直接调节不同区域的压力感知范围。
- **[原文]「The combination of a wide pressure sensing range, high sensitivity, low signal drift, and high toughness renders this material a promising soft pressure sensor for underwater applications, point‐of‐care measurements, or measurements conducted in humid environments.」** — 论文将宽量程、高灵敏度、低漂移和高韧性整合到同一体系中，明确指向水下、POC 和高湿环境等典型软电子应用场景。

## 方法

- **[原文]「We introduce a 3D‐printable pressure sensor composed of carbon black‐functionalized double network granular hydrogels (CB‐DNGHs).」** — 方法核心是以 CB‐DNGHs 作为传感主体，把炭黑功能化与双网络颗粒水凝胶结构结合起来。
- **[原文]「The granular structure of DNGHs enables localization of CB fillers in the interstitial spaces such that they reach percolation even if added at concentrations as low as 0.95 vol%, enabling them to combine piezoresistivity with toughness.」** — 结构设计上通过“颗粒内部为水凝胶、颗粒间隙富集导电填料”的空间分工，在低填料条件下建立渗流通路。
- **[原文]「The granular nature of the ink offers an additional advantage: it enables direct ink writing of materials with locally varying compositions and hence pressure sensing ranges.」** — 制备路径采用 direct ink writing，并利用局部成分变化来实现不同压力响应区间的打印集成。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「added at concentrations as low as 0.95 vol%」** → 该体系在炭黑含量低至 0.95 vol% 时即可达到渗流，说明颗粒间隙中的填料定位显著提高了导电网络形成效率。
- **[原文]「detect pressures as low as kPa to several 100 kPa」** → 3D 打印鞋底原型的检测范围从 kPa 级延伸至数百 kPa，表明该材料兼顾低压感知与较高载荷场景。
- **[原文]「3D‐printable」** → 材料本身具备 3D 打印适配性，使其不仅是实验室配方，也具备向器件级几何结构集成推进的潜力。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** [原文]「high sensitivity, low signal drift, and high toughness」摘要只给出性能方向，未提供灵敏度系数、漂移幅度、机械循环次数或失效阈值，因此器件的长期可靠性和横向可比性仍需正文数据支持。
- **[推断]** [原文]「for underwater applications, point‐of‐care measurements, or measurements conducted in humid environments」应用场景描述很明确，但实际封装策略、环境稳定性测试和潜在生物接触约束是否充分解决，仍需结合全文验证。
- **[推断]** [原文]「materials with locally varying compositions and hence pressure sensing ranges」局部成分编程是很强的制造优势，但多区域打印后的界面一致性、标定复杂度与批量复现性可能成为后续工程化难点。

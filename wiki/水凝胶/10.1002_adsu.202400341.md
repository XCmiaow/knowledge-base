## 摘要

> [原文]「Researchers are drawn to the study of two‐dimensional (2D) materials/bacterial cellulose (BC) composites for energy and environmental applications due to the intriguing electrical, mechanical, electrochemical, and catalytic properties of 2D materials combined with the sustainability and biocompatibility of BC.」这篇综述强调，2D材料与细菌纤维素复合体系的价值，来自二维材料多维功能属性与BC可持续性、生物相容性的结合。

> [原文]「The recent progress on the application of advanced 2D materials/BC composites in energy storage (supercapacitors and batteries) and environmental remediation (water treatment, antimicrobial activity, and environmental gas sensing) are explained in detail highlighting future outlooks and challenges in the field.」文章聚焦这类复合材料在储能与环境修复两大方向的最新进展，并明确把未来展望与挑战作为收束重点。

- 这篇综述聚焦 2D materials 与 bacterial cellulose 的复合体系，覆盖可持续储能和环境修复两条主线。

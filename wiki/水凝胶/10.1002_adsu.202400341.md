# Composites of 2D Materials and Bacterial Cellulose for Sustainable Energy Storage and Environmental Remediation

## 摘要

> [原文]「Researchers are drawn to the study of two‐dimensional (2D) materials/bacterial cellulose (BC) composites for energy and environmental applications due to the intriguing electrical, mechanical, electrochemical, and catalytic properties of 2D materials combined with the sustainability and biocompatibility of BC.」这篇综述强调，2D材料与细菌纤维素复合体系的价值，来自二维材料多维功能属性与BC可持续性、生物相容性的结合。

> [原文]「The recent progress on the application of advanced 2D materials/BC composites in energy storage (supercapacitors and batteries) and environmental remediation (water treatment, antimicrobial activity, and environmental gas sensing) are explained in detail highlighting future outlooks and challenges in the field.」文章聚焦这类复合材料在储能与环境修复两大方向的最新进展，并明确把未来展望与挑战作为收束重点。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「In this review, the key strategies are explained to develop 2D materials/BC composites and highlight unique properties of such fascinating systems.」** — 文章的核心贡献是总结2D材料/BC复合物的构建策略，并把这些体系的独特性质作为分析主轴。

## 方法

- **[原文]「The recent progress on the application of advanced 2D materials/BC composites in energy storage (supercapacitors and batteries) and environmental remediation (water treatment, antimicrobial activity, and environmental gas sensing) are explained in detail highlighting future outlooks and challenges in the field.」** — 方法上这是一篇综述性梳理，按应用场景组织材料，覆盖超级电容器、电池、水处理、抗菌和环境气体传感等方向。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「Researchers are drawn to the study of two‐dimensional (2D) materials/bacterial cellulose (BC) composites for energy and environmental applications due to the intriguing electrical, mechanical, electrochemical, and catalytic properties of 2D materials combined with the sustainability and biocompatibility of BC.」** → 摘要中唯一明确出现的数字信息是“2D”，其关键发现不是单点性能数据，而是指出二维材料的电学、力学、电化学和催化特性与BC的可持续性、生物相容性之间存在功能互补。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出任何具体性能指标、实验条件或对比基线，因此这篇文章更适合作为研究地图与选题入口，而不适合仅凭摘要直接判断某一2D材料/BC体系的最优性能。
- **[推断]** 文中已将“future outlooks and challenges”列为重点，说明该方向虽然应用面广，但在规模化、稳定性、机理澄清或场景落地上仍存在尚待系统解决的问题。

# Hydrogel-Transformable Probiotic Powder for Targeted Eradication of Helicobacter pylori with Enhanced Gastric Mucosal Repair and Microbiota Preservation

**中文标题**: 水凝胶可转化益生菌粉末用于靶向根除幽门螺杆菌并增强胃黏膜修复与微生物群保留

## 摘要

> [原文]“This study presents a novel bacterium-mediated bacterial elimination strategy using an edible L. reuteri@HTP probiotic powder for targeted bacterial elimination.” 该研究提出一种可食用的 L. reuteri@HTP 益生菌粉末策略，核心目标是靶向清除幽门螺杆菌。  
> [原文]“Upon contact with water, the powder quickly transforms into a hydrogel, enhancing L. reuteri’s survival in the harsh gastric environment and ensuring selective release at H. pylori-infected inflammatory sites.” 其关键机制是遇水快速转变为水凝胶，从而提升罗伊氏乳杆菌在胃内恶劣环境中的存活，并在 H. pylori 感染炎症位点选择性释放。  
> [原文]“This strategy combines targeted eradication, mucosal healing, and microbiome restoration, offering a new approach to treating gastric infections.” 整体上，这一体系把靶向杀菌、黏膜修复与微生物群恢复整合到同一治疗框架中。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]“This study presents a novel bacterium-mediated bacterial elimination strategy using an edible L. reuteri@HTP probiotic powder for targeted bacterial elimination.”** → 贡献在于把“益生菌递送载体”与“益生菌主动杀菌”结合起来，形成面向 H. pylori 的新型细菌介导清除策略。
- **[原文]“Upon contact with water, the powder quickly transforms into a hydrogel, enhancing L. reuteri’s survival in the harsh gastric environment and ensuring selective release at H. pylori-infected inflammatory sites.”** → 该设计同时解决了益生菌制剂常见的两个难点：胃内存活率低，以及病灶部位递送不精准。
- **[原文]“Additionally, TA's antioxidant properties help alleviate inflammation, and HA supports gastric mucosal repair.”** → 体系不只关注杀灭 H. pylori，还通过 TA 和 HA 分别承担抗氧化抗炎与胃黏膜修复功能，体现多机制协同。
- **[原文]“L. reuteri@HTP powder preserves the integrity of the gut microbiota, facilitating the restoration of a healthy microbiome.”** → 相比传统抗生素方案，这项工作强调在清除病原体的同时尽量保留肠道菌群完整性。

## 方法

- **[原文]“The probiotic powder is obtained by grinding a lyophilized hydrogel composed of L. reuteri, hyaluronic acid (HA), tannic acid (TA), and polyvinyl alcohol (PVA).”** → 制备路线是先构建含 L. reuteri、HA、TA、PVA 的冻干水凝胶，再研磨成粉末，形成可储存、可重构的益生菌制剂。
- **[原文]“Upon contact with water, the powder quickly transforms into a hydrogel”** → 给药后材料通过遇水快速复水成胶，实现从“粉末储存态”到“水凝胶功能态”的原位转变。
- **[原文]“L. reuteri targets and reduces H. pylori colonization while secreting reuterin to eliminate the bacteria.”** → 功能执行层面由 L. reuteri 定向降低 H. pylori 定植，并通过分泌 reuterin 发挥杀菌作用。
- **[原文]“Additionally, TA's antioxidant properties help alleviate inflammation, and HA supports gastric mucosal repair.”** → 材料组分分工明确：TA 负责缓解炎症相关氧化压力，HA 负责支持胃黏膜修复。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]“the probiotic powder remains stable at room temperature for at least six months”** → 该制剂在**室温**下可稳定保存**至少六个月**，说明其储运可行性明显优于对冷链敏感的常规活菌制剂。
- **[原文]“enhancing L. reuteri’s survival in the harsh gastric environment and ensuring selective release at H. pylori-infected inflammatory sites.”** → 关键效果不是单纯装载益生菌，而是显著提升其胃内存活并实现感染炎症位点的选择性释放。
- **[原文]“L. reuteri targets and reduces H. pylori colonization while secreting reuterin to eliminate the bacteria.”** → 该体系表现出“降低定植 + 分泌抑菌分子杀灭”的双层抗 H. pylori 机制。
- **[原文]“L. reuteri@HTP powder preserves the integrity of the gut microbiota, facilitating the restoration of a healthy microbiome.”** → 结果层面还强调对肠道菌群完整性的保护，这一点对应其作为抗生素替代方案的潜在优势。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出样本规模、对照设计、根除率、菌落减少幅度或统计学结果，因此当前无法判断其相对标准抗生素疗法的定量优势。
- **[推断]** 摘要虽提到“selective release at H. pylori-infected inflammatory sites”，但未说明这一选择性释放的具体触发机制与体内分布证据，后续应关注材料响应机制和定位精度。
- **[推断]** 室温稳定性仅报告“at least six months”，但未说明更长期储存、不同湿度条件或生产放大后的活菌活性保持情况，转化应用仍需补充。

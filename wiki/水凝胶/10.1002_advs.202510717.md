# Mechanically Active Hydrogel for Healing Intestinal Fistulas through the YAP-Mediated Mechanosensitization of Intestinal Epithelial Cells

**中文标题**: 通过 YAP 介导的机械敏化促进肠上皮细胞修复肠瘘的机械活性水凝胶

## 摘要

> [原文]「Intestinal fistula is a serious condition characterized by abnormal connections between the intestine and surrounding tissues.」这篇工作首先把肠瘘界定为肠道与周围组织之间形成异常连接的严重疾病喵~
>
> [原文]「Herein, a mechanically active hydrogel (GNGP) that actively constricts intestinal fistulas in response to body temperature is presented.」作者提出了一种可在体温刺激下主动收缩肠瘘的机械活性水凝胶 GNGP 喵~
>
> [原文]「Moreover, GNGP hydrogel accelerates the re-epithelialization process of rabbit intestinal fistulas, ameliorates the inflammation of the fistula tract, and promotes the deposition of collagen into the damaged area to effectively facilitate intestinal fistula healing.」摘要声称该材料可加速兔肠瘘再上皮化、减轻瘘管炎症并促进胶原沉积，从而推动愈合喵~

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Herein, a mechanically active hydrogel (GNGP) that actively constricts intestinal fistulas in response to body temperature is presented.」** 这项工作的核心创新是把“体温触发的主动收缩”直接用于肠瘘封堵，而不是只提供被动填塞材料喵~
- **[原文]「It possesses broad-spectrum antimicrobial properties, excellent adhesion, suitable degradation properties, and a balance between injectable properties and mechanical activity—which, together, enable the in situ sealing of intestinal fistulas.」** 作者把广谱抗菌、强黏附、合适降解、可注射性与力学活性整合到同一材料中，目标是实现原位封堵喵~
- **[原文]「Together, this mechanically active hydrogel with injectable properties offers a promising strategy for the sealing treatment of intestinal fistulas.」** 摘要将这项工作定位为一种有前景的肠瘘封堵治疗策略喵~

## 方法

- **[原文]「Herein, a mechanically active hydrogel (GNGP) that actively constricts intestinal fistulas in response to body temperature is presented.」** 工程设计的起点是利用体温响应驱动水凝胶主动收缩喵~
- **[原文]「It possesses broad-spectrum antimicrobial properties, excellent adhesion, suitable degradation properties, and a balance between injectable properties and mechanical activity—which, together, enable the in situ sealing of intestinal fistulas.」** 材料设计强调可注射进入病灶后再依靠黏附、降解与力学性质完成原位密封喵~
- **[原文]「Mechanistically, shear stress is found to promote the proliferation of intestinal epithelial cells via Yes-associated protein-mediated mechanosensitization, through finite element simulations and in vitro experiments.」** 机制研究结合有限元模拟与体外实验，检验剪切应力是否经由 YAP 介导的机械敏化促进肠上皮细胞增殖喵~

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「GNGP hydrogel accelerates the re-epithelialization process of rabbit intestinal fistulas」** 该材料在兔肠瘘场景中被认为能够加快再上皮化进程喵~
- **[原文]「ameliorates the inflammation of the fistula tract, and promotes the deposition of collagen into the damaged area」** 摘要同时报告了减轻瘘管炎症和促进损伤区域胶原沉积这两个组织修复相关效应喵~
- **[原文]「shear stress is found to promote the proliferation of intestinal epithelial cells via Yes-associated protein-mediated mechanosensitization」** 关键生物学发现是力学剪切刺激可能通过 YAP 介导的机械敏化增强肠上皮细胞增殖喵~
- **[原文]「in response to body temperature」** 触发收缩所依赖的外界条件是体温，而不是额外外源设备或特殊环境喵~

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出具体收缩幅度、抗菌强度、降解时程、样本量或统计量，因此疗效强度与可重复性仍需阅读全文和实验数据验证喵~
- **[推断]** 机制证据来自有限元模拟与体外实验，而治疗结论还包含兔肠瘘结果，机制链条在体内是否完全闭环仍需查看全文喵~
- **[推断]** 摘要强调“promising strategy”，说明其更接近有前景的前临床方案，距离临床应用仍可能需要进一步安全性、长期封堵稳定性与转化研究喵~

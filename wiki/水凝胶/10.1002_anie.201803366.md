# Rational Fabrication of Anti-Freezing, Non-Drying Tough Organohydrogels by One-Pot Solvent Displacement.

**中文标题**: 通过一锅法溶剂置换理性制备抗冻、不干燥的高韧性有机水凝胶

## 摘要

> [原文] "Tough hydrogels, polymeric network structures with excellent mechanical properties (such as high stretchability and toughness), are emerging soft materials." 论文聚焦高韧性水凝胶，强调其作为软材料的核心特征是高拉伸性与高韧性。  
> [原文] "Despite their remarkably mechanical features, tough hydrogels exhibit two flaws (freezing around the icing temperatures of water and drying under arid conditions)." 这项工作要解决的关键问题是高韧性水凝胶在接近水冰点时会冻结、在干燥环境下会失水。  
> [原文] "a versatile and straightforward method is reported to fabricate extreme anti-freezing, non-drying CPA-based organohydrogels with long-term stability by partially displacing water molecules within the pre-fabricated hydrogels." 作者提出的核心方案是在预制水凝胶中部分置换水分子，从而获得兼具极端抗冻、抗干燥与长期稳定性的 CPA 基有机水凝胶。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文] "Inspired by cryoprotectants (CPAs) used in the inhibition of the icing of water in biological samples, a versatile and straightforward method is reported to fabricate extreme anti-freezing, non-drying CPA-based organohydrogels with long-term stability by partially displacing water molecules within the pre-fabricated hydrogels."** — 贡献不只是提出“抗冻材料”，而是把生物冷冻保护剂的思路转成了一种可操作的水凝胶改造路线：在保留原有高韧网络的前提下，通过部分溶剂置换把普通 tough hydrogel 转成抗冻、抗干燥的 organohydrogel。

## 方法

- **[原文] "by partially displacing water molecules within the pre-fabricated hydrogels."** — 方法核心是对“预先制备好的水凝胶”进行部分水分子置换，而不是从头重新设计聚合网络。  
- **[原文] "CPA-based Ca-alginate/polyacrylamide (PAAm) tough hydrogels were successfully fabricated with glycerol, glycol, and sorbitol."** — 具体体系是 Ca-alginate/PAAm 高韧水凝胶，所采用的 CPA 包括 glycerol、glycol 和 sorbitol，说明该策略至少在三类常见冷冻保护分子上可行。

## 关键发现

- **[原文] "The CPA-based organohydrogels remain unfrozen and mechanically flexible even up to -70 °C and are stable under ambient conditions or even vacuum."** → 结果层面的最强信息有两点：其一，材料在 **-70 °C** 仍然“不冻结且保持机械柔性”；其二，材料不仅能在常压环境下稳定，也能在 **vacuum** 条件下稳定，直接对应“抗冻”和“非干燥”两个目标。  
- **[原文] "Despite their remarkably mechanical features, tough hydrogels exhibit two flaws (freezing around the icing temperatures of water and drying under arid conditions)."** → 这一定义了评价基线：新体系的价值不是单纯提高力学性能，而是在保留 tough hydrogel 应用前景的同时，补上原体系“接近冰点冻结”和“干燥失水”这两个致命短板。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出 glycerol、glycol、sorbitol 三种 CPA 之间的性能差异、最佳配比、置换程度以及力学定量数据，因此正文需要重点核查“抗冻性能提升”和“韧性保持”是否同时成立。  
- **[推断]** 摘要虽提到“long-term stability”以及在 ambient conditions 和 vacuum 下稳定，但没有说明稳定性的时间尺度、测试条件和循环次数，后续阅读应重点查看其长期储存与反复冻融证据是否充分。

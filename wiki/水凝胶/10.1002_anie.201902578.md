# Inorganic Salts Induce Thermally Reversible and Anti-Freezing Cellulose Hydrogels.

**中文标题**: 无机盐诱导热可逆且抗冻的纤维素水凝胶

## 基础信息

| 属性 | 值 |
|------|-----|
| **DOI** | [10.1002/anie.201902578](https://doi.org/10.1002/anie.201902578) |

| **年份** | 2019 |

## 摘要

> [原文]「Inspired by the anti-freezing mechanisms found in nature, ionic compounds (ZnCl2 /CaCl2 ) are integrated into cellulose hydrogel networks to enhance the freezing resistance.」这项工作借鉴天然抗冻机制，把 ZnCl2/CaCl2 引入纤维素水凝胶网络，以提升材料的抗冻能力。  
> [原文]「cotton cellulose is dissolved by a specially designed ZnCl2 /CaCl2 system, which endows the cellulose hydrogels specific properties such as excellent freeze-tolerance, good ion conductivity, and superior thermal reversibility.」同时作者指出额外加入水或甘油可促进凝固速率，且该材料「may be suitable for the construction of flexible devices used at temperature as low as -70 °C」。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「ionic compounds (ZnCl2 /CaCl2 ) are integrated into cellulose hydrogel networks to enhance the freezing resistance」** — 核心贡献是用无机盐调控纤维素水凝胶网络，从而直接提升抗冻性。
- **[原文]「which endows the cellulose hydrogels specific properties such as excellent freeze-tolerance, good ion conductivity, and superior thermal reversibility」** — 材料性能并非单一抗冻，而是同时覆盖耐冻、离子导电和热可逆三个方向。

## 方法

- **[原文]「cotton cellulose is dissolved by a specially designed ZnCl2 /CaCl2 system」** — 方法核心是使用专门设计的 ZnCl2/CaCl2 体系溶解棉纤维素，并据此构建水凝胶。
- **[原文]「the rate of cellulose coagulation could be promoted by the addition of extra water or glycerol」** — 在成胶过程中，额外加水或甘油被用作促进纤维素凝固速率的调节手段。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「This new type of cellulose-based hydrogel may be suitable for the construction of flexible devices used at temperature as low as -70 °C.」** → 关键定量信息是该纤维素基水凝胶面向柔性器件时，最低适用温度被表述为 `-70 °C`。
- **[原文]「excellent freeze-tolerance, good ion conductivity, and superior thermal reversibility」** → 作者将性能结果概括为优异抗冻性、良好离子电导和更强热可逆性，但摘要中未给出这些性能的具体数值。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有提供机械强度、离子电导、热循环稳定性等定量数据，因此目前更像是概念验证，后续需要用系统性能指标支撑其柔性器件应用主张。
- **[推断]** 虽然作者提到额外加水或甘油可加快凝固，但摘要未说明作用机制与工艺窗口，后续可进一步澄清盐体系、溶剂组成与成胶动力学之间的关系。

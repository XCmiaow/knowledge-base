# Polyzwitterionic Hydrogels for Efficient Atmospheric Water Harvesting

**中文标题**: 用于高效大气取水的聚两性离子水凝胶

## 基础信息

| 属性 | 值 |
|------|-----|

| **年份** | 2022 |

## 摘要

> 这篇工作将聚两性离子水凝胶提出为一种有效的大气取水材料平台，[原文]「Here, polyzwitterionic hydrogels are developed as an effective AWH material platform.」并将其应用背景放在为内陆和干旱地区提供可持续淡水的需求之中，[原文]「Atmospheric water harvesting (AWH) is regarded as one of the promising strategies for freshwater production desirable to provide sustainable water for landlocked and arid regions.」
>
> 作者强调其性能机制来自抗聚电解质效应下吸湿盐与聚合物链的协同作用，可同时捕获水分并增强溶胀能力，[原文]「Via anti-polyelectrolyte effects, the hygroscopic salt coordinated with polymer chains could capture moisture and enhance the swelling property, leading to strong moisture sorption capacity.」
>
> 在定量表现上，该水凝胶在 30% relative humidity 下达到 0.62 g/g 的吸湿量并在 120 minutes 达到平衡，同时可日产 5.87 L kg-1 淡水，[原文]「The hydrogel shows superior AWH performance (0.62 g/g, 120 minutes for equilibrium at 30% relative humidity) and produces 5.87 L kg-1 freshwater per day.」

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Here, polyzwitterionic hydrogels are developed as an effective AWH material platform.」** — 核心贡献是提出了聚两性离子水凝胶这一新的大气取水材料平台，而不是仅在既有吸湿材料上做小幅改性。
- **[原文]「Via anti-polyelectrolyte effects, the hygroscopic salt coordinated with polymer chains could capture moisture and enhance the swelling property, leading to strong moisture sorption capacity.」** — 核心创新点在于利用抗聚电解质效应，把吸湿盐与聚合物链的配位、吸湿和溶胀增强机制绑定在一起。
- **[原文]「It is anticipated that the polyzwitterionic hydrogels with unique salt-responsive property could provide new insights to design and prepare next-generation AWH materials.」** — 这项工作不仅给出一种材料，还把“盐响应性”提出为下一代大气取水材料的设计线索。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Via anti-polyelectrolyte effects, the hygroscopic salt coordinated with polymer chains could capture moisture and enhance the swelling property, leading to strong moisture sorption capacity.」** — 方法上是通过抗聚电解质效应，让吸湿盐与聚合物链发生配位，从而同时提升捕水能力和凝胶溶胀能力。
- **[原文]「However, the introduction of much inorganic salts often leads to aggregation and leakage issues in practical use.」** — 该设计针对的是传统高盐吸湿体系在实际使用中的团聚与泄漏问题，因此方法逻辑是把盐“整合进”聚两性离子凝胶网络，而不是简单外加无机盐。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The hydrogel shows superior AWH performance (0.62 g/g, 120 minutes for equilibrium at 30% relative humidity) and produces 5.87 L kg-1 freshwater per day.」** → 关键定量结果包括：吸湿量 0.62 g/g、在 120 minutes 达到平衡、测试条件为 30% relative humidity、产水能力为 5.87 L kg-1 per day，这些数字共同定义了其性能上限与应用潜力。
- **[原文]「Hygroscopic materials have attracted widespread attention because of their water harvesting performance. However, the introduction of much inorganic salts often leads to aggregation and leakage issues in practical use.」** → 相比常见依赖大量无机盐的吸湿材料，这种体系的意义在于试图兼顾取水性能与实际可用性问题。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要只给出了 30% relative humidity 下的单一性能点，没有展示不同湿度、温度或昼夜循环条件下的表现，因此环境适应性仍需正文数据验证。
- **[推断]** 摘要没有说明吸附-解吸循环稳定性、盐流失抑制的长期效果以及实际器件级集成方式，因此离工程化应用仍有一段距离。
- **[推断]** “could provide new insights to design and prepare next-generation AWH materials” 说明这项工作更像材料设计范式的提出，后续可沿“盐响应性”和网络结构协同调控继续扩展。

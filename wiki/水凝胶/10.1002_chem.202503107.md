# Interpenetrating Polymer Network Eutectogels Derived From Polymerizable Deep Eutectic Solvents for Low-Temperature Supercapacitors.

**中文标题**: 源自可聚合深共熔溶剂的互穿聚合物网络共晶凝胶用于低温超级电容器

## 摘要

> [原文]「This study developed a polymerizable deep eutectic solvent (PDES) system composed of H2O, choline chloride, and acrylic acid, which exhibited a freezing point as low as -57.2?°C.」本文开发了由 H2O、choline chloride 和 acrylic acid 组成的可聚合深共熔溶剂体系，其低冰点特征使其适合低温电化学场景。  
> [原文]「The optimized IPN eutectogel demonstrates outstanding overall properties, including high mechanical strength (tensile stress up to 97.2?kPa) and toughness (fracture elongation of 235%), while maintaining high ionic conductivity (21.4?mS?cm-1 at 25°C) across a broad temperature range (-40 to 60°C).」优化后的 IPN 共晶凝胶同时兼顾力学强度、延展性与离子传导，并可在 -40 到 60°C 的宽温域内工作。  
> [原文]「They achieve a maximum energy density of 7.10?Wh?kg-1, retain 80% of the initial capacitance after 10,000 cycles, and show stable performance under bending, damage, and low-temperature (-30?°C) conditions.」基于该凝胶的超级电容器在能量密度、循环寿命以及弯折、损伤和 -30°C 低温条件下的稳定性方面都表现出明显应用潜力。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「By introducing sodium acetate and carrying out in situ polymerization within a polyacrylamide hydrogel, a high-performance eutectogel with an interpenetrating polymer network (IPN) structure was constructed.」** — 文章的核心贡献是把 polymerizable deep eutectic solvent、sodium acetate 与 polyacrylamide hydrogel 结合起来，构建出互穿聚合物网络共晶凝胶，从材料设计上同时解决低温适用性与凝胶力学/导电性的平衡问题。
- **[原文]「Supercapacitors using this eutectogel operate effectively within a 1.2?V voltage window and exhibit ideal electric double-layer capacitive behavior.」** — 作者不仅停留在材料层面，还把该凝胶直接验证到超级电容器中，证明其可以作为低温柔性储能器件电解质的候选材料。

## 方法

- **[原文]「This study developed a polymerizable deep eutectic solvent (PDES) system composed of H2O, choline chloride, and acrylic acid, which exhibited a freezing point as low as -57.2?°C.」** — 先设计由 H2O、choline chloride 和 acrylic acid 组成的 PDES 体系，以获得极低冰点的基础溶剂环境。
- **[原文]「By introducing sodium acetate and carrying out in situ polymerization within a polyacrylamide hydrogel, a high-performance eutectogel with an interpenetrating polymer network (IPN) structure was constructed.」** — 再通过引入 sodium acetate，并在 polyacrylamide hydrogel 内进行原位聚合，形成 IPN 结构的 eutectogel。
- **[原文]「Supercapacitors using this eutectogel operate effectively within a 1.2?V voltage window and exhibit ideal electric double-layer capacitive behavior.」** — 最后将该凝胶用于超级电容器组装，并在 1.2?V 电压窗口下评估其电化学行为。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「This study developed a polymerizable deep eutectic solvent (PDES) system composed of H2O, choline chloride, and acrylic acid, which exhibited a freezing point as low as -57.2?°C.」** → 该 PDES 体系的最低冰点达到 -57.2?°C，直接支撑其低温应用定位。
- **[原文]「The optimized IPN eutectogel demonstrates outstanding overall properties, including high mechanical strength (tensile stress up to 97.2?kPa) and toughness (fracture elongation of 235%), while maintaining high ionic conductivity (21.4?mS?cm-1 at 25°C) across a broad temperature range (-40 to 60°C).」** → 优化后的 IPN 共晶凝胶拉伸应力可达 97.2?kPa，断裂伸长率为 235%，并在 25°C 下保持 21.4?mS?cm-1 的离子电导率，适用温区覆盖 -40 到 60°C。
- **[原文]「Supercapacitors using this eutectogel operate effectively within a 1.2?V voltage window and exhibit ideal electric double-layer capacitive behavior.」** → 该凝胶支持器件在 1.2?V 窗口内稳定工作，且表现出理想双电层电容行为。
- **[原文]「They achieve a maximum energy density of 7.10?Wh?kg-1, retain 80% of the initial capacitance after 10,000 cycles, and show stable performance under bending, damage, and low-temperature (-30?°C) conditions.」** → 器件最大能量密度达到 7.10?Wh?kg-1，10,000 次循环后仍保留 80% 初始电容，并且在弯折、损伤和 -30?°C 条件下保持稳定性能。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要强调了低温适用性、力学性能和循环稳定性，但没有给出倍率性能、功率密度、界面机理、对照组细节和实际封装条件下的长期稳定性，因此其工程可扩展性与性能来源还需要正文数据进一步支撑。
- **[推断]** 摘要只说明器件在 bending、damage 和 low-temperature (-30?°C) 条件下稳定，却未交代自修复机制、损伤恢复方式或定量衰减幅度，后续应关注受损后可逆性与复杂工况耐久性。

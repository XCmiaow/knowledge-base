# Multilayer Antibacterial Hydrogel Wound Dressings Incorporated With Green Synthesized Silver Nanoparticles

**中文标题**: 载有绿色合成银纳米颗粒的多层抗菌水凝胶创伤敷料

## 摘要

> [原文]「Multilayer antibacterial hydrogel wound dressings were fabricated and characterized for wound healing applications.」这篇工作聚焦于面向创面愈合应用的多层抗菌水凝胶敷料制备与表征。  
> [原文]「Dressings are designed to achieve infection control, moisture management in the wound area and to support wound healing.」作者将感染控制、伤口湿度管理与愈合支持作为同一个敷料系统的并行目标。  
> [原文]「The optimal multilayered hydrogel, incorporating AgNPs and loaded with lidocaine HCl, has shown biocompatibility and hemocompatibility, exhibiting 60% degradation by day 14, water vapor permeability of 2022 ± 460 g/m2 over 24 h, a tensile strength of 6.71 ± 0.62 MPa, 36.38% ± 3.62% elongation at break, and 65.72% ± 14.80% drug release within 10 h, making it a promising candidate for facilitating the wound healing process.」最优配方在相容性、力学、透湿、降解和释药之间表现出较完整的综合性能。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Multilayer wound dressings were prepared as three layers by solvent casting method.」** 核心设计不是单层敷料，而是通过溶剂浇铸构建三层结构来分配不同功能。
- **[原文]「The upper layer is composed of kappa carrageenan and green synthesized silver nanoparticles (AgNPs, ~122 nm in size, zeta potential of –35 mV) to provide the moist control, and to form a barrier against microorganism attack.」** 上层把湿度调控与抗菌屏障耦合在一起，并明确给出了AgNPs的粒径与表面电性。
- **[原文]「Lidocaine HCl loaded polyvinyl alcohol and chitosan‐based middle layer was designed to achieve controlled drug release and to add strength to the hydrogel structure.」** 中层同时承担镇痛药物控释与结构增强两项任务。
- **[原文]「The lower layer is composed of hyaluronic acid and ovalbumin to serve a controlling membrane for controlled drug release, and to further support wound healing.」** 下层被定义为控释膜，同时进一步支撑创面修复。
- **[原文]「The incorporation of AgNPs resulted in reduced swelling values and degradation rates of the multilayer wound dressings, enhanced mechanical capabilities, and no significant change in water vapor permeability values.」** AgNPs在这个体系里不只是抗菌填料，也改变了溶胀、降解、力学和透湿的整体平衡。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Multilayer wound dressings were prepared as three layers by solvent casting method.」** 制备路线是三层溶剂浇铸。
- **[原文]「The upper layer is composed of kappa carrageenan and green synthesized silver nanoparticles (AgNPs, ~122 nm in size, zeta potential of –35 mV) to provide the moist control, and to form a barrier against microorganism attack.」** 上层材料选用kappa carrageenan与绿色合成AgNPs，目标是保湿控制和抗微生物屏障。
- **[原文]「Lidocaine HCl loaded polyvinyl alcohol and chitosan‐based middle layer was designed to achieve controlled drug release and to add strength to the hydrogel structure.」** 中层由负载Lidocaine HCl的PVA/壳聚糖体系组成，用于控释与增强。
- **[原文]「The lower layer is composed of hyaluronic acid and ovalbumin to serve a controlling membrane for controlled drug release, and to further support wound healing.」** 下层由透明质酸和卵清蛋白构成，承担控释膜角色。
- **[原文]「Different amounts of AgNPs were used in formulations to evaluate their impact on multilayer wound dressings.」** 实验变量集中在AgNPs添加量，用于评估其对多层敷料性能的影响。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The upper layer is composed of kappa carrageenan and green synthesized silver nanoparticles (AgNPs, ~122 nm in size, zeta potential of –35 mV) to provide the moist control, and to form a barrier against microorganism attack.」** AgNPs的关键表征是约122 nm粒径和–35 mV zeta电位，这说明作者使用的是带负电的纳米银分散体系。
- **[原文]「The incorporation of AgNPs resulted in reduced swelling values and degradation rates of the multilayer wound dressings, enhanced mechanical capabilities, and no significant change in water vapor permeability values.」** AgNPs带来的主要趋势是降低溶胀、减缓降解、增强力学性能，同时不明显牺牲水蒸气透过性。
- **[原文]「They have demonstrated enhanced antibacterial efficacy against Klebsiella pneumoniae, Bacillus subtilis and Candida albicans.」** 抗菌活性覆盖Klebsiella pneumoniae、Bacillus subtilis和Candida albicans，说明其对细菌和真菌均有增强抑制效果。
- **[原文]「The optimal multilayered hydrogel, incorporating AgNPs and loaded with lidocaine HCl, has shown biocompatibility and hemocompatibility, exhibiting 60% degradation by day 14, water vapor permeability of 2022 ± 460 g/m2 over 24 h, a tensile strength of 6.71 ± 0.62 MPa, 36.38% ± 3.62% elongation at break, and 65.72% ± 14.80% drug release within 10 h, making it a promising candidate for facilitating the wound healing process.」** 最优体系在第14天降解60%，24 h水蒸气透过率为2022 ± 460 g/m2，拉伸强度为6.71 ± 0.62 MPa，断裂伸长率为36.38% ± 3.62%，并在10 h内释放65.72% ± 14.80%药物，显示出面向创面敷料的综合适配性。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出AgNPs具体添加量、对照组定量差异和统计检验细节，因此目前只能确认趋势，难以判断效应量大小。
- **[推断]** 摘要强调了生物相容性、血液相容性和体外抗菌性，但没有出现动物创面模型或临床结果，后续需要验证其真实促愈合效果。
- **[推断]** 虽然给出了10 h释药、14天降解和24 h透湿等结果，但未说明这些指标之间的优化权衡过程，后续值得做更系统的处方-性能关联分析。

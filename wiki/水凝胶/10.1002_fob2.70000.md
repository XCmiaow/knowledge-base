## 摘要

> [原文]「Hydrogels of natural, synthetic and hybrid origin are used in various food applications in modern days.」这篇综述先把食品领域中的水凝胶放在天然、合成与杂化三类材料的总体背景下，并聚焦天然聚合物基水凝胶在当代食品应用中的位置。  
> [原文]「natural polymer‐based hydrogels are becoming increasingly popular in the food industry owing to their structural diversity, biocompatibility, affordability, biodegradability and non‐toxicity compared to their synthetic counterparts」文章指出，天然聚合物基水凝胶受关注的核心原因是结构多样、生物相容、价格可负担、可生物降解且低毒。  
> [原文]「This review provides a comprehensive overview of the recent food applications of natural polymer‐based hydrogels (2018–2024), their gelation mechanisms, rheology and future perspectives, with special emphasis on their environmental significance.」综述范围覆盖 2018–2024 年的食品应用、成胶机制、流变学与未来方向，并特别强调环境意义。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「These hydrogels are grouped as polysaccharide‐based, protein‐based and composite hydrogels.」** — 将天然聚合物基水凝胶明确分为多糖基、蛋白基和复合水凝胶三类，提供了清晰的类型框架。
- **[原文]「These natural hydrogels are used to form edible films, encapsulate and control the release of bioactive and flavour compounds, 3D printing applications and as fat substitutes, food additives and stabilisers in the food industry.」** — 系统归纳了其在食品中的主要功能场景，包括可食膜、生物活性物与风味物质包埋及控释、3D 打印，以及作为脂肪替代物、食品添加剂和稳定剂。
- **[原文]「Due to their biocompatible nature, they have shown great promise as an essential ingredient in a range of food products, including dairy dessert gels, yogurt, confectionery and meat products.」** — 将材料功能进一步连接到具体食品品类，说明其在乳制甜品凝胶、酸奶、糖果和肉制品中的应用潜力。

## 方法

- **[原文]「This review provides a comprehensive overview of the recent food applications of natural polymer‐based hydrogels (2018–2024), their gelation mechanisms, rheology and future perspectives, with special emphasis on their environmental significance.」** — 方法上这是一篇更新综述，围绕 2018–2024 年文献，对食品应用、成胶机制、流变学、未来展望与环境意义进行综合梳理。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「This review provides a comprehensive overview of the recent food applications of natural polymer‐based hydrogels (2018–2024)」** → 定量信息上，综述明确覆盖了 **2018–2024** 这一时间窗口，说明其目标是更新近年的研究进展。
- **[原文]「These natural hydrogels are used to form edible films, encapsulate and control the release of bioactive and flavour compounds, 3D printing applications」** → 应用层面已延伸到 **3D** 打印，表明该材料不仅用于传统配方改良，也进入结构化食品制造场景。
- **[原文]「the product‐associated environmental footprint of natural hydrogels is minimal」** → 文章把“环境足迹低”视为天然水凝胶的重要优势，这使其价值不只在功能性，也在可持续性。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要强调的是分类、应用、成胶机制、流变学与环境意义，但没有给出任何性能对比数据、实验条件、百分比指标或温度参数，因此更适合作为领域地图，而不是直接用于配方设计或工艺放大。
- **[推断]** 摘要指出天然材料相较合成材料具有多项优势，但未交代不同天然水凝胶在稳定性、加工窗口或应用边界上的差异，后续阅读正文时应重点补足这些可操作细节。

## 摘要

> [原文]「Recent advancements in biomedical applications have highlighted the need for nontoxic and organic materials with versatile capabilities.」[原文]「Silver nanoparticles (AgNPs) have emerged as a promising antimicrobial agent due to their exceptional physicochemical properties, whereas hydrogels offer potential applications in biomedicine due to their biocompatibility, biodegradability, and hydrophilicity.」本文指出，生物医学材料正在朝低毒、有机且多功能方向发展，而 AgNPs 与水凝胶分别提供抗菌能力和生物相容性基础。

> [原文]「AgNPs‐infused hydrogel can offer synergistic approach for various biomedical applications, specifically in wound healing, drug delivery, and antimicrobial coatings.」[原文]「The incorporation of AgNPs into the hydrogel enhances their antimicrobial properties, making them ideal for reducing infections and promoting tissue regeneration.」作者将 AgNPs 掺入水凝胶界定为一种协同策略，核心价值在于把抗感染、组织修复支持与多场景应用整合到同一材料平台中。

> [原文]「AgNPs‐infused hydrogel can serve as controlled‐release systems for therapeutic agents, which ensures sustained and targeted drug delivery.」[原文]「challenges such as potential cytotoxicity, environmental concerns, and long‐term effects require further investigation.」该体系兼具持续、靶向递药潜力，但其细胞毒性、环境风险和长期效应仍是后续研究必须正面解决的问题。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「AgNPs‐infused hydrogel can offer synergistic approach for various biomedical applications, specifically in wound healing, drug delivery, and antimicrobial coatings.」** — 综述把该体系定位为覆盖伤口愈合、药物递送和抗菌涂层的多用途协同平台。
- **[原文]「The incorporation of AgNPs into the hydrogel enhances their antimicrobial properties, making them ideal for reducing infections and promoting tissue regeneration.」** — 文章强调 AgNPs 的引入不仅增强抗菌性，还把“降低感染”与“促进组织再生”连接为同一应用逻辑。
- **[原文]「AgNPs‐infused hydrogel can serve as controlled‐release systems for therapeutic agents, which ensures sustained and targeted drug delivery.」** — 该材料被进一步提升为治疗载体，而不只是抗菌基质。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Overall, this review comprehensively explores the synthesis methods, properties, applications, and challenges associated with AgNPs‐infused hydrogel.」** — 本文采用综述性梳理路径，围绕合成方法、材料性质、应用场景与现实挑战组织已有研究。
- **[原文]「Recent advancements in biomedical applications have highlighted the need for nontoxic and organic materials with versatile capabilities.」** — 选题出发点是从生物医学应用需求反推材料设计与评价重点。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「Silver nanoparticles (AgNPs) have emerged as a promising antimicrobial agent due to their exceptional physicochemical properties, whereas hydrogels offer potential applications in biomedicine due to their biocompatibility, biodegradability, and hydrophilicity.」** → 关键发现是该体系的协同基础来自 AgNPs 的理化抗菌优势与水凝胶的生物相容性、可降解性、亲水性互补。
- **[原文]「The incorporation of AgNPs into the hydrogel enhances their antimicrobial properties, making them ideal for reducing infections and promoting tissue regeneration.」** → AgNPs 的加入使水凝胶从一般载体转向兼具抗感染与促修复功能的活性材料。
- **[原文]「Most importantly, this type of system offers a potential pathway for overcoming the challenges posed by traditional materials.」** → 作者将其视为应对传统材料局限的一条潜在替代路径。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 由于[原文]「Overall, this review comprehensively explores the synthesis methods, properties, applications, and challenges associated with AgNPs‐infused hydrogel.」且摘要未呈现任何性能数值、百分比或实验条件，该文更适合作为领域地图与研究入口，不足以单独支持配方优选或工艺放大决策。
- **[推断]** 既然[原文]「challenges such as potential cytotoxicity, environmental concerns, and long‐term effects require further investigation」，后续工作应优先补足长期安全性、环境归趋和真实应用条件下的验证证据。

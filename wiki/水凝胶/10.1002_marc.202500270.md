# A Cellulose Ionogel with Mechanical Robustness and Extreme Temperature Tolerance for Electronic Skin.

**中文标题**: 用于电子皮肤的兼具机械鲁棒性与极端温度耐受性的纤维素离子凝胶

## 摘要

> 该研究面向柔性电子中的离子凝胶痛点，提出了一种纤维素离子凝胶，其设计路径是[原文]「triggering the mechano-induced alignment of cellulose molecular chains and incorporating Ca2+ complexation interactions」以同时改善力学性能与低温适应性。  
> 性能上，该材料实现了[原文]「a tensile strength of 6.84 MPa, an elastic modulus surpassing 100 MPa, and an ionic conductivity of 31.7 mS·cm-1」，并且能够[原文]「maintain flexibility even at temperatures as low as -196 °C」。  
> 应用上，作者指出[原文]「the assembled electronic skin displays reliable sensing performance」且该体系有望[原文]「ensuring the stable operation of flexible electronic devices in highly challenging environments」。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「The combination of robust mechanical strength, high ionic conductivity, biocompatibility, and extreme temperature tolerance underscores the potential applications of this cellulose ionogel」** — 文章的核心贡献是把高力学强度、高离子电导、生物相容性与极端温度耐受性整合到同一纤维素离子凝胶体系中，从而支撑其在电子皮肤与柔性器件中的应用价值。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Here, a cellulose ionogel is reported by triggering the mechano-induced alignment of cellulose molecular chains and incorporating Ca2+ complexation interactions.」** — 方法上以机械诱导的纤维素分子链取向配合 Ca2+ 配位作用来构建离子凝胶网络，这是其同时获得强韧性与抗冻性的关键设计。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「This cellulose ionogel presents outstanding mechanical properties and ionic conductivity, featuring a tensile strength of 6.84 MPa, an elastic modulus surpassing 100 MPa, and an ionic conductivity of 31.7 mS·cm-1.」** → 该材料在强度、刚度与离子传导三项指标上同时达到较高水平，定量数据分别为 6.84 MPa、超过 100 MPa、31.7 mS·cm-1。
- **[原文]「Furthermore, the Ca2+ complexation networks provide the cellulose ionogel with remarkable freezing resistance, allowing it to maintain flexibility even at temperatures as low as -196 °C, while the assembled electronic skin displays reliable sensing performance.」** → Ca2+ 配位网络不仅赋予材料显著抗冻性，使其在 -196 °C 仍保持柔性，还支持组装后的电子皮肤实现可靠传感。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出循环拉伸稳定性、长期环境老化、传感灵敏度/响应时间等器件级指标，因此其工程化可靠性与性能边界仍需结合正文和补充实验进一步确认。

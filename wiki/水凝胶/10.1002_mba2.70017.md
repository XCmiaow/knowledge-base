## 摘要

> [原文]「Hydrogels, as three-dimensional hydrophilic polymer networks, have been widely utilized in biomedical applications due to their excellent biocompatibility, high water content, and tunable physicochemical properties.」水凝胶因其三维亲水聚合物网络结构、优异生物相容性、高含水量与可调理化性质，已被广泛用于生物医学应用。  
> [原文]「However, traditional bulk hydrogels often suffer from limitations such as inadequate mechanical strength, slow response to external stimuli, and restricted diffusion efficiency」与 [原文]「hydrogel microspheres (HMs) have emerged as a promising alternative, which offers advantages such as injectability, high surface-area-to-volume ratio, and tunable functionality.」传统块体水凝胶在机械强度、外界刺激响应速度和扩散效率方面存在局限，因此具备可注射性、高表面积体积比和可调功能性的水凝胶微球成为有前景的替代方案。  
> [原文]「In recent years, stimuli-responsive HMs have attracted significant attention for their ability to respond intelligently to environmental cues such as pH, reactive oxygen species (ROS), enzymes, and temperature.」与 [原文]「This enables controlled drug release, enhanced therapeutic precision, and spatiotemporal regulation in biomedical applications.」近年来，刺激响应型水凝胶微球因能够对 pH、ROS、酶和温度等环境信号作出智能响应，而被看作实现控释、提升治疗精度和时空调控的重要方向。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「This review systematically summarizes the materials, fabrication strategies, and functional mechanisms of stimuli-responsive HMs」** — 核心贡献是把刺激响应型水凝胶微球的材料体系、制备策略与功能机制纳入同一综述框架。
- **[原文]「highlighting their applications in drug delivery, disease treatment, and tissue engineering」** — 文章将应用场景明确聚焦于药物递送、疾病治疗和组织工程。
- **[原文]「key challenges and future perspectives are discussed, which provides insights into how these intelligent HMs can advance personalized medicine and clinical translation」** — 综述不仅总结现状，也把关键挑战、未来方向、个体化医疗与临床转化联系起来。

## 方法

- **[原文]「By integrating natural and synthetic materials with advanced fabrication techniques, including microfluidics and emulsification, researchers have achieved precise control over the morphology, size, and bioactivity of HMs.」** — 方法层面强调将天然与合成材料结合，并通过微流控和乳化等先进制备技术精确调控微球的形貌、尺寸和生物活性。
- **[原文]「This review systematically summarizes the materials, fabrication strategies, and functional mechanisms of stimuli-responsive HMs」** — 文章本身采用系统性综述方式组织证据，而不是报告单一实验结果。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「Hydrogels, as three-dimensional hydrophilic polymer networks」** → 关键材料前提是水凝胶具有“三维”亲水聚合物网络结构，这是全文唯一直接出现的数量性描述。
- **[原文]「high surface-area-to-volume ratio」** → 与传统块体水凝胶相比，微球的重要结构优势在于更高的表面积体积比。
- **[原文]「environmental cues such as pH, reactive oxygen species (ROS), enzymes, and temperature」** → 刺激响应型微球的触发信号覆盖 pH、活性氧、酶和温度等多类生物环境线索。
- **[原文]「This enables controlled drug release, enhanced therapeutic precision, and spatiotemporal regulation in biomedical applications.」** → 这些响应能力直接指向控释、精准治疗和时空调控三类生物医学效应。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要强调「systematically summarizes」与「key challenges and future perspectives are discussed」，但未给出不同材料体系、刺激类型或制备策略之间的定量比较标准，因此当前更像领域全景梳理而非可直接指导方案优选的比较性证据总结。
- **[推断]** 摘要将前景落在「personalized medicine and clinical translation」，这意味着从实验室级智能响应到临床转化之间，仍可能存在规模化制备、一致性控制与体内复杂环境适配等尚待解决的问题。

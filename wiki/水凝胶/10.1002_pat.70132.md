## 摘要

> 这是一篇综述，核心任务是梳理Bio-HyGs在“design and utilization”上的最新进展，重点聚焦药物递送与伤口愈合两类应用。[原文] "This review on bioactive Hydrogels (Bio-HyGs) synthesizes current advancements in their design and utilization, particularly emphasizing their roles in drug delivery and wound healing."
>
> 文中将GM、PEG、PVA等材料体系与慢性创面治疗联系起来，并强调其保湿、促组织再生、递送VEGF/PDGF以及整合抗菌组分的能力。[原文] "Bio-HyGs, including gelatin methacrylate (GM), polyethylene glycol (PEG), and poly(vinyl alcohol) (PVA), are highlighted for their effectiveness in treating chronic wounds like diabetic and pressure ulcers, leveraging their moisture retention and tissue regeneration capabilities." [原文] "These hydrogels are designed for the controlled release of bioactive compounds such as vascular endothelial growth factor (VEGF) and platelet-derived growth factor (PDGF), thereby facilitating healing without the need for initial cell seeding." [原文] "The review also covers hydrogels embedded with antimicrobial agents like silver nanoparticles and quaternized chitosan, which are crucial for managing infected wounds."
>
> 文章还把温敏响应、自组装肽和3D打印视为进一步逼近生物组织行为、增强递药与创面修复效果的重要方向。[原文] "Additionally, advancements in thermoresponsive hydrogels that respond to temperature changes and the application of self-assembling peptides and 3D printing are discussed for their contributions to mimicking biological tissues, which enhance both drug delivery and wound healing."

## 核心贡献

- **[原文] "This review on bioactive Hydrogels (Bio-HyGs) synthesizes current advancements in their design and utilization, particularly emphasizing their roles in drug delivery and wound healing."** - 文章的主要贡献不是提出单一新材料，而是对Bio-HyGs的设计改造路径和应用场景做综述性整合，明确将药物递送与伤口愈合作为主线。
- **[原文] "The review aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of structural and functional modifications in Bio-HyGs, exploring their potential in transforming clinical outcomes in wound treatment and drug delivery systems."** - 文章试图把“结构改造”和“功能改造”放到同一分析框架下，强调这类材料对临床伤口治疗和递药系统的转化潜力。

## 方法

- **[原文] "This review on bioactive Hydrogels (Bio-HyGs) synthesizes current advancements in their design and utilization"** - 方法本质上是文献综述与归纳，不是实验研究；作者通过汇总当前设计与应用进展来搭建主题地图。
- **[原文] "The review also covers hydrogels embedded with antimicrobial agents like silver nanoparticles and quaternized chitosan"** - 综述组织方式偏“功能模块”导向，按抗菌、递药、创面修复等功能组件展开。
- **[原文] "Additionally, advancements in thermoresponsive hydrogels that respond to temperature changes and the application of self-assembling peptides and 3D printing are discussed"** - 在技术路线上，文章还纳入温敏响应、自组装肽和3D打印等新兴构建策略，说明其覆盖传统材料到先进制造的连续谱。

## 关键发现

- **[原文] "Bio-HyGs, including gelatin methacrylate (GM), polyethylene glycol (PEG), and poly(vinyl alcohol) (PVA), are highlighted for their effectiveness in treating chronic wounds like diabetic and pressure ulcers, leveraging their moisture retention and tissue regeneration capabilities."** - 作者认为GM、PEG、PVA这几类代表性Bio-HyGs已经显示出处理糖尿病溃疡、压疮等慢性伤口的应用价值，关键机制是保湿与促组织再生。
- **[原文] "These hydrogels are designed for the controlled release of bioactive compounds such as vascular endothelial growth factor (VEGF) and platelet-derived growth factor (PDGF), thereby facilitating healing without the need for initial cell seeding."** - 文章强调Bio-HyGs不仅是覆盖材料，还是递药载体；通过控制释放VEGF、PDGF，可在“不需要初始细胞接种”的前提下促进愈合。
- **[原文] "The review also covers hydrogels embedded with antimicrobial agents like silver nanoparticles and quaternized chitosan, which are crucial for managing infected wounds."** - 对感染创面的处理被视为Bio-HyGs的重要应用分支，其中银纳米颗粒和季铵化壳聚糖是代表性的抗菌功能组分。
- **[原文] "Additionally, advancements in thermoresponsive hydrogels that respond to temperature changes and the application of self-assembling peptides and 3D printing are discussed for their contributions to mimicking biological tissues, which enhance both drug delivery and wound healing."** - 文章把温敏水凝胶、自组装肽和3D打印视为提升仿生能力的关键技术，其中“3D”是摘要中唯一明确出现的数字信息，也提示先进制造已进入该领域的主流讨论。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要突出“potential”和“transforming clinical outcomes”，但没有给出具体疗效指标、对照结果或安全性数据，说明本文更适合作为方向性综述，而不是直接支持某一材料优效性的证据。
- **[推断]** 文中并列讨论GM、PEG、PVA、抗菌组分、温敏响应、自组装肽和3D打印，覆盖面很广，但摘要未显示统一评价框架；后续阅读时需要重点检查正文是否比较了不同路线的适用边界与取舍。
- **[推断]** 摘要强调“without the need for initial cell seeding”，这说明作者偏向无细胞、材料主导的修复策略；后续可继续追问这一路线在复杂慢性伤口中是否足以替代细胞治疗或联合治疗。

# Preparation of spherical nanocellulose from <scp>Gai</scp> bamboo and mechanical properties of chitosan/nanocellulose composite

**中文标题**: 盖竹球形纳米纤维素的制备及壳聚糖/纳米纤维素复合材料的力学性能

## 摘要

> [原文]「spherical cellulose nanocrystals have been extracted from Gai bamboo (Bambusa blumeana) fibers by sulfuric acid hydrolysis methods」本文以盖竹纤维为原料，通过硫酸水解制备球形纤维素纳米晶。  
> [原文]「the nanocellulose has a spherical shape with a diameter of 2 to 10 nm and the crystallization index of 70.30% and crystallization degree of 0.77」所得纳米纤维素呈 2 to 10 nm 球形颗粒，结晶指数为 70.30%，结晶度为 0.77。  
> [原文]「The highest value achieved with chitosan film containing nanocellulose of 2 phr. The increase values of tensile strength, modulus and elongation at break were of 63%, 207 MPa, and 30%, respectively. In addition, the moisture absorption of the chitosan film was decreased to 20%」当添加量为 2 phr 时，壳聚糖复合膜综合性能最佳，拉伸强度、模量、断裂伸长率分别提升 63%、207 MPa、30%，吸湿率降至 20%。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「spherical cellulose nanocrystals have been extracted from Gai bamboo (Bambusa blumeana) fibers by sulfuric acid hydrolysis methods」** — 贡献在于从盖竹纤维中制备出球形纤维素纳米晶，说明该竹材可以作为纳米纤维素来源。
- **[原文]「The improvement of thermal and mechanical properties of the chitosan films loaded with spherical nanocellulose were evaluated」** — 贡献在于将球形纳米纤维素引入壳聚糖膜，并系统评估其热学与力学性能改进。
- **[原文]「These improvements illustrated the advancement of nano sized of spherical nanocellulose and the chemical compatibility between nanocellulose and chitosan that resulted in well‐dispersion of nanocellulose in chitosan matrix, developed high performance bionanocomposite」** — 文章将性能提升归因于球形纳米尺寸效应、壳聚糖与纳米纤维素的化学相容性，以及在基体中的良好分散。

## 方法

- **[原文]「spherical cellulose nanocrystals have been extracted from Gai bamboo (Bambusa blumeana) fibers by sulfuric acid hydrolysis methods」** — 核心制备方法是以盖竹纤维为前体进行硫酸水解。
- **[原文]「The removal of amorphous components, lignin, and hemicellulose in raw bamboo fiber and the incorporation of sulfate groups were explored via the changes of bond's vibration in Fourier‐infrared spectra and the decrease of onset temperature of thermogravimetric diagram」** — 通过傅里叶红外光谱中的键振动变化，以及热重图起始温度下降来分析无定形组分、木质素、半纤维素去除和硫酸酯基引入。
- **[原文]「The results from transmission electron microscopy and X‐ray diffraction revealed that the nanocellulose has a spherical shape with a diameter of 2 to 10 nm and the crystallization index of 70.30% and crystallization degree of 0.77」** — 使用透射电镜和 X 射线衍射表征形貌与结晶特征。
- **[原文]「The improvement of thermal and mechanical properties of the chitosan films loaded with spherical nanocellulose were evaluated」** — 随后将球形纳米纤维素加载到壳聚糖膜中，评估热学与力学性能变化。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「the nanocellulose has a spherical shape with a diameter of 2 to 10 nm and the crystallization index of 70.30% and crystallization degree of 0.77」** → 纳米纤维素成功获得球形形貌，粒径范围为 2 to 10 nm，且具有较高结晶水平。
- **[原文]「The highest value achieved with chitosan film containing nanocellulose of 2 phr」** → 最优复合效果出现在纳米纤维素添加量为 2 phr 时。
- **[原文]「The increase values of tensile strength, modulus and elongation at break were of 63%, 207 MPa, and 30%, respectively」** → 在最优添加量下，壳聚糖复合膜的拉伸强度、模量和断裂伸长率均明显提高，其中模量增量明确给出为 207 MPa。
- **[原文]「the moisture absorption of the chitosan film was decreased to 20% indicating that the barrier properties have improved」** → 吸湿率降至 20%，表明复合膜阻隔性能得到改善。
- **[原文]「the decrease of onset temperature of thermogravimetric diagram」** → 热重起始温度出现下降，提示硫酸酯基引入后材料热稳定性存在变化，但摘要未给出具体温度数值。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要只报告了最佳添加量 2 phr 及其性能结果，没有给出其他添加量的完整比较数据，因此难以判断性能提升的剂量-效应关系。
- **[推断]** 虽然提到热重起始温度下降，但未提供具体温度值与完整热分析参数，限制了对热稳定性代价的定量评估。
- **[推断]** 机理解释主要基于表征与性能结果推断，尚缺少更直接的界面作用证据来验证“chemical compatibility”和“well‐dispersion”的具体来源。

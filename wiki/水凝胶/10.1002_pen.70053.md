# Development and Characterization of Novel Biodegradable Hydrogels With Controlled Moisture Release for Smart Packaging Materials

**中文标题**: 用于智能包装材料的具有可控湿度释放功能的新型可生物降解水凝胶的开发与表征

## 摘要

> **[原文]** "The primary objective of this study was to develop novel biodegradable hydrogels based on chitosan and alginate with controlled moisture release for smart packaging applications." 这篇论文的核心目标是开发基于壳聚糖与海藻酸盐、可用于智能包装的可生物降解控湿水凝胶。  
> **[原文]** "Key findings demonstrated that chitosan-based and alginate-based hydrogels achieved a swelling degree of up to 1500% in distilled water, a tensile strength of 1.2 MPa, an elongation at break of 180%, and 70% biodegradability over 28 days." 结果显示该材料同时具备高溶胀能力、一定机械强度、延展性与28天内70%的生物降解表现。  
> **[原文]** "The application of these hydrogels maintained carrot moisture content at 68% after 28 days, reduced color change (螖E) to 14.2, and decreased microbial contamination to 105 CFU/g." 在胡萝卜模型产品中，这种水凝胶还能维持含水率、降低变色并抑制微生物污染。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]** "The primary objective of this study was to develop novel biodegradable hydrogels based on chitosan and alginate with controlled moisture release for smart packaging applications." 贡献在于把壳聚糖/海藻酸盐水凝胶明确推进到“可控湿度释放”的智能包装应用场景。
- **[原文]** "The practical value of this research lies in the development of eco-friendly packaging materials capable of extending product shelf life and reducing microbial contamination. These developments hold considerable importance for the food and pharmaceutical industries." 贡献不仅是材料制备本身，也在于其被定位为可延长货架期、降低污染的绿色包装方案，并面向食品与医药行业。

## 方法

- **[原文]** "The study utilized chitosan, sodium alginate, glutaraldehyde, and calcium chloride, while the methods included determining the swelling degree, mechanical properties, biodegradability, and the hydrogels' effects on moisture content, color, and microbial contamination in model products." 方法上使用壳聚糖、海藻酸钠、戊二醛和氯化钙构建水凝胶，并从溶胀、力学性能、生物降解性以及模型产品中的含水率、颜色和微生物污染多个维度进行评估。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]** "Key findings demonstrated that chitosan-based and alginate-based hydrogels achieved a swelling degree of up to 1500% in distilled water, a tensile strength of 1.2 MPa, an elongation at break of 180%, and 70% biodegradability over 28 days." 关键材料性能数据包括蒸馏水中最高1500%的溶胀度、1.2 MPa的拉伸强度、180%的断裂伸长率，以及28天内70%的生物降解率。
- **[原文]** "The application of these hydrogels maintained carrot moisture content at 68% after 28 days, reduced color change (螖E) to 14.2, and decreased microbial contamination to 105 CFU/g." 应用层面上，水凝胶在28天后仍将胡萝卜含水率维持在68%，将色差控制到14.2，并把微生物污染降到105 CFU/g。
- **[原文]** "The relevance of the study is driven by the need to develop environmentally friendly materials for smart packaging capable of regulating moisture and extending the shelf life of products." 这些结果说明该体系不仅是可降解材料研究，也直接对应“调控湿度并延长货架期”的包装功能目标。

## 不足与展望

- **[原文]** "Limitations include the laboratory-scale experimental setup, which may differ from real-world storage conditions, as well as the need for further testing on various products." 已知不足是实验仍停留在实验室尺度，且真实储藏条件与更多产品类型上的有效性尚待验证。
- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出湿度释放动力学、长期储存波动下的稳定性或与实际包装结构集成后的表现，因此距离工程化应用还需要更完整的过程数据。

## 摘要

> [原文]「By integrating computer simulations with 4D materials research, we have improved the precision of our studies and reduced research time.」本文的核心思路是把计算机模拟引入4D材料研究，以提升研究精度并缩短研究时间。  
> [原文]「In this study, we developed and analyzed bilayer‐designed hydrogels using acrylamide (AM), N‐isopropylacrylamide (NIPAm), and 3‐[[2‐(methacryloyloxy)ethyl]dimethylammonio]propane‐1‐sulfonate (SBMA) monomers.」作者构建并分析了由AM、NIPAm与SBMA单体组成的双层水凝胶体系。  
> [原文]「The bilayer hydrogel samples, composed of LCST and UCST polymer materials (NIPAm and SBMA), demonstrated curvature and inverse‐curvature 4D movements across a temperature range of 4°C70°C.」研究进一步表明，这类由LCST/UCST聚合物构成的双层样品能够在热刺激下产生曲率与反曲率两类4D运动。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「By integrating computer simulations with 4D materials research, we have improved the precision of our studies and reduced research time.」** — 贡献在于把计算机模拟明确嵌入4D水凝胶研究流程，目标不是单纯观察现象，而是同时提高研究精度并压缩研发周期。
- **[原文]「By employing computer simulations to predict 4D movements in bilayer‐designed samples, we achieved more precise insights into the properties of thermo‐responsive hydrogel materials.」** — 贡献还在于用模拟预测双层样品的4D运动，从而更精确地理解热响应性水凝胶的材料性质。
- **[原文]「This study highlights the potential of combining computer simulations with experimental approaches to design more complex and sophisticated 4D materials in the future.」** — 文章把“模拟+实验”定位为未来设计更复杂4D材料的可扩展路线。

## 方法

- **[原文]「In this study, we developed and analyzed bilayer‐designed hydrogels using acrylamide (AM), N‐isopropylacrylamide (NIPAm), and 3‐[[2‐(methacryloyloxy)ethyl]dimethylammonio]propane‐1‐sulfonate (SBMA) monomers.」** — 材料设计上采用AM、NIPAm、SBMA三类单体构建双层水凝胶，用于比较不同热响应机制下的形变行为。
- **[原文]「These hydrogels were based on LCST, UCST, and standard hydrogel formulations and were characterized using FT‐IR spectroscopy, rheological measurements, and 4D motion analysis.」** — 方法上同时覆盖LCST、UCST与标准水凝胶配方，并通过FT‐IR光谱、流变测试和4D运动分析完成表征。
- **[原文]「By employing computer simulations to predict 4D movements in bilayer‐designed samples」** — 分析框架包含对双层样品4D运动的计算机预测，再与实验观察结合解释材料行为。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The bilayer hydrogel samples, composed of LCST and UCST polymer materials (NIPAm and SBMA), demonstrated curvature and inverse‐curvature 4D movements across a temperature range of 4°C70°C.」** → 关键结果是由NIPAm与SBMA组成的LCST/UCST双层样品出现了曲率与反曲率两种4D运动；[推断] 原文写作 `4°C70°C`，应表示覆盖 4°C 到 70°C 的温度区间。
- **[原文]「These hydrogels were based on LCST, UCST, and standard hydrogel formulations」** → 研究不是只考察单一配方，而是并列比较了LCST、UCST和标准水凝胶三类体系。
- **[原文]「The fabrication of bilayer‐designed stimuli‐responsive hydrogels has been extensively investigated.」** → 本文工作建立在“双层刺激响应性水凝胶已被广泛研究”的背景上，因此其新意集中在热响应体系组合与模拟辅助分析，而非双层结构本身。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出曲率大小、响应速率、可逆循环次数或模拟模型参数，因此当前只能确认其展示了4D运动与模拟辅助设计潜力，尚不足以从摘要判断性能上限、预测误差和工程可放大性。

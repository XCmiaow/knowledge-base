# A Cellulose Reinforced Polyacrylamide/Polyvinyl Alcohol Dual Network Hydrogel for Flexible Sensors

**中文标题**: 一种用于柔性传感器的纤维素增强聚丙烯酰胺/聚乙烯醇双网络水凝胶

## 摘要

> [原文]「polyacrylamide (PAM) was used as the main matrix material to form the first layer network structure, and polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) was used as the secondary matrix material to form the second layer network structure.」本文以 PAM 作为第一层网络基体、PVA 作为第二层网络基体，构建双网络水凝胶体系。  
> [原文]「The hydrogels were prepared by free radical in‐situ polymerization and physical cross‐linking.」同时结合 [原文]「cellulose nanocrystals (CNC) penetrate through the polyacrylamide network」说明作者将 CNC 引入 PAM 网络，形成半互穿增强结构。  
> [原文]「the hydrogel also has good sensing and electrical properties」且 [原文]「has potential application value in the field of flexible sensors」表明该材料被定位为兼具力学、电学与环境适应性的柔性传感候选水凝胶。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「This hydrogel was characterized not only by its semi‐interpenetrating network structure, in which cellulose nanocrystals (CNC) penetrate through the polyacrylamide network but also by the physically and chemically cross‐linked hybrid PAM/PVA double network.」** — 核心创新是把 CNC 半互穿增强与 PAM/PVA 物理-化学双重交联网络耦合在同一体系中。
- **[原文]「The addition of organic solvents makes the hydrogels have excellent antifreeze and water retention properties and also have excellent swelling properties.」** — 除了常见的力学与传感目标，作者还把抗冻、保水和溶胀性能纳入设计目标，拓宽了应用场景。
- **[原文]「Therefore, the PAM/PVA/CNC dual network interpenetrating composite hydrogel has potential application value in the field of flexible sensors.」** — 研究的落点明确指向柔性传感器应用，而不是仅停留在材料制备层面。

## 方法

- **[原文]「The hydrogels were prepared by free radical in‐situ polymerization and physical cross‐linking.」** — 制备方法是自由基原位聚合配合物理交联，分别对应化学网络与次级网络的形成。
- **[原文]「polyacrylamide (PAM) was used as the main matrix material to form the first layer network structure, and polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) was used as the secondary matrix material to form the second layer network structure.」** — 材料设计上采用 PAM/PVA 主次双基体分层构网。
- **[原文]「The structure and appearance of the hydrogels were characterized by scanning electron microscope and Fourier transform infrared spectrometer, and a series of tests were carried out to detect their mechanical properties, electrical properties and sensing properties.」** — 表征路径包括 SEM、FTIR 以及力学、电学、传感性能测试，覆盖了结构到功能的主要验证链条。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「the elongation at break can reach 600?% and the stress at break can reach 0.16?MPa」** → 该水凝胶的断裂伸长率可达 600?%，断裂应力可达 0.16?MPa，体现出较强延展性与一定承载能力。
- **[原文]「the hydrogel also has good sensing and electrical properties」** → 除机械性能外，材料同时表现出良好的电学与传感性能，说明其具备功能化输出基础。
- **[原文]「The addition of organic solvents makes the hydrogels have excellent antifreeze and water retention properties and also have excellent swelling properties.」** → 有机溶剂的加入显著扩展了材料在低温保持、失水抑制和溶胀行为上的适用性。
- **[原文]「Therefore, the PAM/PVA/CNC dual network interpenetrating composite hydrogel has potential application value in the field of flexible sensors.」** → 作者据此判断该复合双网络水凝胶具备柔性传感器应用潜力。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有提供灵敏度、响应/恢复时间、循环稳定性或应变范围等传感器关键定量指标，因此其器件级表现仍需依赖正文验证。
- **[推断]** 抗冻与保水性能仅作定性描述，未在摘要中给出有机溶剂种类、配比、测试温度或保持时长，当前还无法与同类抗冻水凝胶直接比较。
- **[推断]** 力学数据只给出 600?% 与 0.16?MPa 两个终点指标，缺少模量、韧性和循环加载信息，对双网络协同增强机制的判断仍然有限。

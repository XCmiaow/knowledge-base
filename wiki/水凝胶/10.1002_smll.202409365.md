# Self-Healing and Antifreezing/Antidrying Conductive Eutectohydrogel-Based Biosignal Monitoring Multisensors with Integrated Supercapacitor.

**中文标题**: 自愈与抗冻/抗干燥导电低共熔水凝胶基生物信号监测多传感器及集成超级电容器

## 摘要

> [原文]「A novel self-healing and antifreezing/antidrying conductive eutectohydrogel, ideal for wearable multifunctional sensors and supercapacitors, is reported.」本文报道了一种面向可穿戴多功能传感器与超级电容器的自愈、抗冻、抗干燥导电低共熔水凝胶。  
> [原文]「Conductive eutectohydrogel with self-healing and facilely tunable mechanical performance is obtained by incorporation of trehalose and phytic acid as reversible cross-linkers into a polyacrylamide network, forming the dynamic hydrogen bonding and electrostatic interactions.」与[原文]「combined use of deep eutectic solvent with water ensures the air stability as well as the antifreezing/antidrying characteristics.」说明该体系通过海藻糖和植酸引入动态氢键/静电作用，并借助深共熔溶剂与水的组合实现空气稳定性及抗冻抗干燥特性。  
> [原文]「With the eutectohydrogel as a versatile platform, self-healing strain and temperature sensors, electrocardiogram electrodes, and supercapacitor are fabricated」与[原文]「This work demonstrates high potential application of the synthesized eutectohydrogel to flexible wearable devices featuring durability and longevity.」表明该材料被验证为应变传感、温度传感、心电电极和储能器件的一体化平台，目标是提升柔性可穿戴器件的耐久性与寿命。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「A novel self-healing and antifreezing/antidrying conductive eutectohydrogel, ideal for wearable multifunctional sensors and supercapacitors, is reported.」** — 核心贡献在于把“自愈 + 抗冻 + 抗干燥 + 导电”这些通常难以兼顾的属性统一到同一低共熔水凝胶平台上，并直接面向多传感与储能一体化可穿戴应用。
- **[原文]「With the eutectohydrogel as a versatile platform, self-healing strain and temperature sensors, electrocardiogram electrodes, and supercapacitor are fabricated」** — 贡献不止于材料本体，而是进一步完成了应变传感器、温度传感器、心电电极和超级电容器的器件级验证，体现出平台化价值。
- **[原文]「With a vertically integrated patch device of supercapacitor and strain sensor attached onto skin, various body movements are successfully detected using the energy stored in the supercapacitor」** — 论文进一步展示了储能器件与传感器的垂直集成贴片，强调其在自供能式贴肤监测中的应用潜力。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Conductive eutectohydrogel with self-healing and facilely tunable mechanical performance is obtained by incorporation of trehalose and phytic acid as reversible cross-linkers into a polyacrylamide network, forming the dynamic hydrogen bonding and electrostatic interactions.」** — 方法上以聚丙烯酰胺网络为骨架，引入海藻糖与植酸作为可逆交联剂，通过动态氢键和静电相互作用实现自愈与力学可调。
- **[原文]「Furthermore, combined use of deep eutectic solvent with water ensures the air stability as well as the antifreezing/antidrying characteristics.」** — 体系设计的另一关键是深共熔溶剂与水的协同使用，用于同时保证空气稳定性、抗冻性和抗干燥性。
- **[原文]「With the eutectohydrogel as a versatile platform, self-healing strain and temperature sensors, electrocardiogram electrodes, and supercapacitor are fabricated」** — 材料制备完成后，作者将其直接拓展为多类器件，用器件恢复与温域稳定性来反证材料设计的有效性。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The synthesized eutectohydrogel exhibits a self-healing efficiency of 90.7% after 24 h at room temperature, Young's modulus of 140.9 kPa, and strain at break of 352.8%.」** → 该材料在室温放置 24 h 后自愈效率达到 90.7%，同时具备 140.9 kPa 的杨氏模量和 352.8% 的断裂应变，说明其在自愈之外仍保持了可观的力学强度与延展性。
- **[原文]「recovering the device performance after self-healing from complete bisection and exhibiting stable performance over a wide temperature range from -20 to 50 °C.」** → 器件在“完全切断后自愈”条件下仍能恢复性能，并且可在 -20 到 50 °C 的宽温区稳定工作，这对应可穿戴场景中低温与常温波动下的可靠性需求。
- **[原文]「With a vertically integrated patch device of supercapacitor and strain sensor attached onto skin, various body movements are successfully detected using the energy stored in the supercapacitor, without performance degradation even after self-healing from complete bisection of the full patch device.」** → 集成贴片不仅能依靠超级电容器储能完成体动检测，而且在整块贴片完全切断后自愈，性能仍无明显下降，说明“材料自愈”被真正传递到了“系统级功能保持”。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要只给出了 90.7%、24 h、140.9 kPa、352.8% 和 -20 至 50 °C 等代表性指标，但没有提供电导率、比电容、循环寿命、传感灵敏度或 ECG 信号质量等关键量化数据，因此目前更像是“多功能可行性验证”，距离严格的器件性能对标还差完整数据支撑。
- **[推断]** 自愈性能是在「after 24 h at room temperature」条件下报告的，说明恢复速度可能并不快；若面向日常佩戴与反复损伤场景，后续需要验证更短时间尺度下的恢复效率与长期循环自愈稳定性。
- **[推断]** 虽然展示了「without performance degradation even after self-healing from complete bisection of the full patch device」，但摘要未说明重复切割-愈合次数、复杂形变疲劳和长期皮肤贴附工况，后续应补足耐久寿命与真实使用边界。

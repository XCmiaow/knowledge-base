# N-Doped Holey Graphene/Porous Carbon/Cellulose Nanofibers Electrode and Hydrogel Electrolyte for Low-temperature Zinc-ion Hybrid Supercapacitors.

## 摘要

> [原文]「The susceptibility to freezing of the electrolyte and mismatched cathode make the aqueous zinc-ion hybrid supercapacitors (ZHSCs) have inferior electrochemical performance at low temperature.」这项工作首先界定了低温水系锌离子混合超级电容器的核心痛点：电解液易冻结，同时正极与体系不匹配，导致低温电化学性能下降。  
> [原文]「Herein, a novel freeze-tolerant hydrogel electrolyte (CEEZ) and matched graphene/porous carbon/cellulose nanofibers cathode (GPCN) are respectively fabricated via chemical cross-linking and a two-step process to assemble ZHSCs.」作者提出同时优化电解液与正极的协同方案，分别构建抗冻水凝胶电解质 CEEZ 和匹配的 GPCN 正极来组装 ZHSCs。  
> [原文]「Consequently, the ZHSCs assembled from GPCN, CEEZ, and Zn anode exhibit excellent specific capacitances of 1.11 F cm⁻2 (21.35 F cm⁻3) at 20 °C and 0.74 F cm⁻2 (14.23 F cm⁻3) at -20 °C.」结果表明该器件在 20 °C 与 -20 °C 下都保持了可观比电容，说明其面向寒冷环境储能应用具有现实潜力。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Herein, a novel freeze-tolerant hydrogel electrolyte (CEEZ) and matched graphene/porous carbon/cellulose nanofibers cathode (GPCN) are respectively fabricated via chemical cross-linking and a two-step process to assemble ZHSCs.」** — 论文的核心贡献不是单点改性，而是把抗冻电解质与匹配正极作为一体化设计对象，直接针对低温 ZHSC 的“电解液冻结 + 正极失配”双重瓶颈。
- **[原文]「This work provides valuable insights and a robust strategy for the design of high-performance low-temperature ZHSCs, enhancing their practical applicability in renewable energy storage systems.」** — 作者将工作定位为一种可迁移的低温 ZHSC 设计策略，而不只是单个材料组合的性能展示。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Herein, a novel freeze-tolerant hydrogel electrolyte (CEEZ) and matched graphene/porous carbon/cellulose nanofibers cathode (GPCN) are respectively fabricated via chemical cross-linking and a two-step process to assemble ZHSCs.」** — 方法上采用双路径制备：CEEZ 通过化学交联获得，GPCN 通过 two-step process 制备，再与 Zn 负极配对组装器件。
- **[原文]「The prepared electrode has a highly porous structure, abundant edge active sites, and increased interlayer spacing, which collectively reduces ion transport complexity and enhances the contact area with the electrolyte, promoting rapid ionic conduction pathways.」** — 正极设计强调高孔隙结构、丰富边缘活性位点与增大的层间距，其目标是降低离子传输复杂度并扩大电极/电解液接触面积。
- **[原文]「For the CEEZ, the use of ethylene glycol reduces the saturated vapor pressure of water, thereby enhancing the frost resistance of the hydrogel electrolyte.」** — 电解质侧的关键操作是引入乙二醇，通过降低水的饱和蒸气压来提升水凝胶的抗冻性。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「Consequently, the ZHSCs assembled from GPCN, CEEZ, and Zn anode exhibit excellent specific capacitances of 1.11 F cm⁻2 (21.35 F cm⁻3) at 20 °C and 0.74 F cm⁻2 (14.23 F cm⁻3) at -20 °C.」** → 最关键的定量结果是器件在常温 20 °C 下达到 1.11 F cm⁻2 与 21.35 F cm⁻3，在低温 -20 °C 下仍有 0.74 F cm⁻2 与 14.23 F cm⁻3，说明低温性能保留度较高。
- **[原文]「These results demonstrate the promising application potential of these ZHSCs in cold environments while maintaining impressive energy storage capabilities.」** → 作者据此认为该体系兼顾寒冷环境适用性与储能能力，应用导向明确。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要给出了 20 °C 和 -20 °C 下的比电容，但未提供循环寿命、倍率演化、能量/功率密度转换关系以及更低温区间的数据，因此该体系的长期稳定性与极端低温边界仍需阅读全文和补充实验来确认。
- **[推断]** 目前机制论证主要停留在结构特征与乙二醇抗冻作用的功能描述层面，若要进一步增强说服力，后续应结合原位/低温表征去分离“离子传输改善”与“界面反应动力学改善”的相对贡献。

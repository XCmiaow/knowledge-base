# A robust friendly nano-encapsulated plant extract in hydrogel Tragacanth gum on cotton fabric through one single step in-situ synthesis and fabrication

**中文标题**: 基于黄蓍胶水凝胶的植物提取物纳米包封在棉织物上的单步原位合成与整理

## 摘要

> [原文]「Here, a robust cotton fabric was prepared through novel simultaneous treatment and nano-encapsulation of Chamomile extract using Tragacanth gum (TG) as the wall material and an environmentally friendly binder with hydrogel properties.」论文提出把洋甘菊提取物纳米包封、TG 水凝胶壁材/黏结剂、棉织物整理三者合并为单步原位过程。  
> [原文]「The prepared NE&TC fabric indicated a relatively good washing and rubbing durability with reasonable release behavior.」并且[原文]「NE&TC fabric indicated relatively good antimicrobial activity with microbe reduction of 80 ± 0.1 % against E. coli, 90 ± 0.1 % against S. aureus and 92 ± 0.1 % against C. albicans.」说明该体系不仅能固定活性物，还兼顾耐久、控释和抗菌。  
> [原文]「NE&TC fabric indicated 13.5 % more water retention than the raw cotton fabric」且[原文]「the required time for water absorption into the raw cotton fabric was 8.22 s, which decreased to 0 s for NE&TC fabric because of the hydrogel properties of TG on the treated fabric」表明 TG 在这里不是单纯包封壁材，而是直接把水凝胶吸水特性引入了织物。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「This article proposes a novel method for the application of TG as a natural polymer on cotton fabric for obtaining multifunction purposes including in-situ synthesis of encapsulated Chamomile extract, stabilization of nanocapsules and introduction of hydrogel properties.」** — 核心创新是把天然多糖 TG 同时当作壁材、稳定剂和水凝胶功能来源，在棉织物上实现“包封 + 固着 + 功能化”一体化。
- **[原文]「Application of the encapsulated materials on the textile substrates is usually carried out in several separate steps including encapsulation, centrifuging, drying and ﬁnally ﬁxing with a binder」** 与 **[原文]「The present work introduces a single-step process through simultaneous nano-encapsulation of plant extracts and stabilization on the cotton fabric.」** — 相比传统多步骤工艺，本文把流程压缩成单步处理，目标是降低时间成本并减少额外黏结剂带来的副作用。
- **[原文]「The hydrophilic structure of TG leads to hydrogel properties by holding large amounts of water in the three-dimensional network.」** — 这篇文章的价值不只在纺织整理，还在于给出了天然多糖水凝胶如何在固体基底上承担载药和控释双重角色的具体案例。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Chamomile ﬂower (0.25 g) was added to a mixture of 30 ml distilled water and 10 ml ethanol in a glass beaker and sonicated with a 400 W and 24 kHz Hielscher ultrasonic homogenizer (UP400S, Germany) equipped with a titanium sonotrode with diameter of 3 mm. The extraction was carried out in an amplitude of 50 % and cycle of 0.5 for 5 min. The sample was then ﬁltered by a centrifuge device (RST 24&16, Iran) for 10 min」** — 洋甘菊提取步骤参数给得很完整，说明作者把超声提取条件纳入了整个单步整理工艺的前处理设计。
- **[原文]「Triton X-100 (0.2 ml) was added to a 3 ml mixture of Chamomile extract and commercial almond oil with the ratio of 1:3 and sonicated for 5 min. The prepared microemulsion was transferred to 100 ml solution containing TG 1 % and Triton X-100 and then sonicated to obtain a steady white microemulsion. The cotton fabric was immersed into the prepared microemulsion for 5 min, and then nano-encapsulation was carried out by adding aluminum chloride 2 %.」** — 单步工艺的关键是“微乳液 + 浸轧式浸渍 + Al³⁺交联”，其中 TG 1 % 和 aluminum chloride 2 % 是形成包封壁层并把其固定到棉纤维上的关键条件。
- **[原文]「The washing test was carried out for 45 min at 30 °C according to the ISO 105-C10 standard test method, and the rubbing test was carried out by a crockmeter/rubbing fastness tester (SDL Atlas, M238AA, USA) according to the ISO 105-X12 standard test method.」** 与 **[原文]「the sample was cut into 5 × 5-cm pieces and stirred with 5 ml phosphate buffer for 2, 4, 8, 12, 18 and 24 h」** — 评价体系覆盖了耐洗、耐摩擦和 24 h 释放行为，比较贴近纺织品实际使用情境。
- **[原文]「A DSC system (20/10 TA Instruments, USA) was used to investigate the thermal behavior of NE&TC fabric by heating to 250 °C with an increasing rate of 10°C min-1 under N2 steady ﬂow.」** 与 **[原文]「Samples were cut into pieces with 48 ± 0.1 mm in diameter and sterilized in an autoclave at 121 °C for 15 min. 0.2 ml of the prepared inoculator was applied to the samples. They were cultivated at 37 °C for 18 h.」** — 热分析与抗菌测试的实验窗口也写得明确，便于后续复现和横向比较。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「the prepared nanocapsules have a spherical shape with an average size of 55–70 nm」** → 作者确实做到了纳米尺度包封，而不是普通微胶囊或表面成膜。
- **[原文]「In the FT-IR spectrum of NE&TC fabric, a higher intensity of the hydroxyl peak at 3346 cm-1, bifurcation of the C-H peak at 2900 and 2920 cm-1, appearance of a peak related to cyclic ester carbonyl at 1777 cm-1 and a peak at 1736 cm-1」** 与 **[原文]「Encapsulation of Chamomile extract into TG was conﬁrmed with a peak at 1111 cm-1 related to the Al–O–C bond」** → 1736、1777、1111 cm-1 这三个位点是“TG 存在、洋甘菊存在、Al³⁺交联存在”的关键证据链。
- **[原文]「The DSC plot of NE&TC fabric indicated two peaks merged and formed one single peak at 78 °C with the onset of 36 °C and the end temperature of 128 °C with latent heat of 73 J g-1. The second peak at 162 °C could be attributed to the evaporation of Chamomile extract and almond oil from the nanocapsules.」** 与 **[原文]「The leakage and evaporation of Chamomile extract and almond oil from the nanocapsules occurred within 143–200 °C with latent heat of 15 J g-1.」** → DSC 把纳米胶囊中的客体存在状态和热释放窗口都标了出来，162 °C 是全文最关键的热学标志温度。
- **[原文]「the absorbance at 310 nm increased from 0.35 to 1.29 after 24 h」** 与 **[原文]「the blank sample showed a very poor release behavior as the UV-Vis absorbance at 310 nm sharply increased to 0.69 after just 2 h and to 0.92 after 4 h」** → 包封样有明显慢释放，空白样则是快速泄放，说明 TG/Al³⁺ 形成的壳层确实在控制释放动力学。
- **[原文]「NE&TC fabric indicated relatively good antimicrobial activity with microbe reduction of 80 ± 0.1 % against E. coli, 90 ± 0.1 % against S. aureus and 92 ± 0.1 % against C. albicans.」** → 对革兰阴性菌、革兰阳性菌和真菌都有效，其中对白色念珠菌的抑制最强。
- **[原文]「NE&TC fabric indicated 13.5 % more water retention than the raw cotton fabric」**、**[原文]「the required time for water absorption into the raw cotton fabric was 8.22 s, which decreased to 0 s for NE&TC fabric」**、**[原文]「The weight of NE&TC fabric increased 3.6 %」**、**[原文]「increasing from 0.45 mm for raw fabric to 0.46 mm for NE&TC fabric」**、**[原文]「the stiffness of NE&TC fabric (187.66 N m-1) was similar to that of the raw fabric (182.97 N m-1)」** → TG 带来的水凝胶增益是明显的，但对厚度和手感相关刚度的副作用较小。

## 图表要点

- **[原文]「Figure 1 indicates the suggested mechanism for simultaneous nano-encapsulation and treatment of Chamomile extract on the cotton fabric through the microemulsion method.」** 与 **[原文]「The encapsulation was carried out through cross-linking between the aluminum ions and carboxylic groups of TG.」** — 图 1 的关键不是画工艺流程，而是说明了“微乳液双层胶束 + Al³⁺-TG 交联 + TG/纤维素氢键”的三段式稳定机制。
- **[原文]「The FESEM images of NE&TC fabric are indicated in Fig. 2a, b, conﬁrming the nanocapsules containing Chamomile extract were successfully formed and treated on the cotton fabric surface.」** 与 **[原文]「the blank fabric treated without aluminum chloride indicated the formation of a ﬁlm layer of TG on the cotton fabric without nano-encapsulation」** — 图 2 的对照意义很强，直接证明没有 aluminum chloride 时更像表面成膜，而不是有效纳米包封。
- **[原文]「Figure 4d shows the UV-Vis spectra of the remaining solutions of NE&TC fabric before and after washing. The lower absorbance peak appearing within 270 and 400 nm in the spectrum of the washed NE&TC fabric compared with the unwashed one proved the Chamomile extract remained on the NE&TC fabric after washing.」** — 图 4 把“洗后仍保留活性物”这件事从形貌、化学键和 UV 吸收三个层面闭环起来了。
- **[原文]「The Chamomile extract on the rubbed NE&TC fabric led to an absorbance peak with the value of 0.6 in the range of 270–400 nm.」** — 图 5 给出了摩擦后的残留信号，不是只说“耐摩擦”，而是保留了可观的紫外特征峰。
- **[原文]「Figure 7a shows a slow increase in the absorbance peak of NE&TC fabric along with prolonged stirring.」** 与 **[原文]「there was no control on the release behavior of the blank sample as nearly the entire Chamomile extracts were released from the fabric during the ﬁrst hour of stirring.」** — 图 7 是全文最能体现“水凝胶控释”价值的一张图，因为它把包封样和空白样的释放曲线拉开了。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 文中虽然报告了「a relatively good washing and rubbing durability」和 24 h 释放曲线，但没有给出多次洗涤循环后的保留率、载药量、包封效率或长期储存稳定性，因此现阶段更像“概念验证”而不是已完成工艺放大的成熟方案。
- **[推断]** 体系使用了 commercial almond oil、Triton X-100 和 aluminum chloride，后续如果面向可穿戴生物医用或高频皮肤接触场景，仍需要补充皮肤相容性、残留组分和长期迁移行为评估。
- **[推断]** 文章把 TG 的水凝胶优势主要体现为吸水、保水和控释，但还没有把交联密度、含水网络结构与释放动力学模型对应起来，后续可以把材料结构表征和释药模型进一步做实。

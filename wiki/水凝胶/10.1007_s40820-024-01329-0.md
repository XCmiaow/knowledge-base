## 摘要

> 该工作面向水系可充锌金属电池的关键失效问题，提出了一种可持续的双重交联纤维素水凝胶电解质。[原文]「their widespread commercial application is largely plagued by three major challenges: The uncontrollable Zn dendrites, notorious parasitic side reactions, and sluggish Zn2+ ion transfer. To address these issues, we design a sustainable dual cross-linked cellulose hydrogel electrolyte」
>
> 作者将其作用机理概括为力学抑枝晶、配位抑副反应与多孔促传输三者协同。[原文]「which has excellent mechanical strength to inhibit dendrite formation, high Zn2+ ions binding capacity to suppress side reaction, and abundant porous structure to facilitate Zn2+ ions migration」
>
> 性能上，该电解质同时支撑 Zn||Zn 对称电池长时稳定运行与 Zn||polyaniline 电池高倍率长循环，并强调材料“易获取、可生物降解”的可持续优势。[原文]「the Zn||Zn cell with the hydrogel electrolyte can cycle stably for more than 400 h under a high current density of 10 mA cm−2」 [原文]「the hydrogel electrolyte also enables the Zn||polyaniline cell to achieve high-rate and long-term cycling performance (> 2000 cycles at 2000 mA g−1)」 [原文]「the hydrogel electrolyte is easily accessible and biodegradable」

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「The hydrogel electrolyte effectively suppresses dendrites growth and side reactions to achieve a stable Zn anode (over 2000 h for Zn||Zn cell), which are proved by a multi-perspective and in-depth mechanism investigation.」** — 核心贡献不只是“做出一种水凝胶”，而是把抑制枝晶、生长副反应控制与机理验证绑在一起，直接指向 Zn 负极稳定性这个中心问题。
- **[原文]「To address these issues, we design a sustainable dual cross-linked cellulose hydrogel electrolyte, which has excellent mechanical strength to inhibit dendrite formation, high Zn2+ ions binding capacity to suppress side reaction, and abundant porous structure to facilitate Zn2+ ions migration.」** — 贡献在于把结构设计与失效机理一一对应：力学强度对应枝晶抑制，Zn2+ 结合能力对应副反应抑制，多孔结构对应离子迁移优化。
- **[原文]「Remarkably, the hydrogel electrolyte is easily accessible and biodegradable, making the ARZBs attractive in terms of scalability and sustainability.」** — 该工作把“可持续性”前置为设计目标，而不是仅追求电化学性能，这对大规模储能场景更有现实意义。

## 方法

- **[原文]「A sustainable dual cross-linked cellulose hydrogel with excellent mechanical strength was fabricated from aqueous alkali hydroxide/urea solution using a sequential chemical and physical cross-linking strategy.」** — 材料制备路线是从水系 alkali hydroxide/urea 溶液出发，采用“先化学后物理”的顺序双重交联策略构筑纤维素水凝胶。
- **[原文]「which are proved by a multi-perspective and in-depth mechanism investigation.」** — 方法层面不仅停留在器件测试，还辅以多视角、深入的机理研究来支撑其对枝晶与副反应的抑制结论。
- **[原文]「which has excellent mechanical strength to inhibit dendrite formation, high Zn2+ ions binding capacity to suppress side reaction, and abundant porous structure to facilitate Zn2+ ions migration.」** — 设计方法本质上是性能导向的结构功能协同：机械性质、界面化学与传输通道同时被纳入材料设计。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The hydrogel electrolyte effectively suppresses dendrites growth and side reactions to achieve a stable Zn anode (over 2000 h for Zn||Zn cell)」** → 在 Zn||Zn 对称电池中，作者首先给出“over 2000 h”的长时稳定负极表现，说明其对 Zn 负极失稳问题具有显著缓解作用。
- **[原文]「the Zn||Zn cell with the hydrogel electrolyte can cycle stably for more than 400 h under a high current density of 10 mA cm−2.」** → 在更苛刻的高电流密度 10 mA cm−2 条件下，Zn||Zn 电池仍可稳定循环“more than 400 h”，说明该电解质不是只在温和工况下有效。
- **[原文]「the hydrogel electrolyte also enables the Zn||polyaniline cell to achieve high-rate and long-term cycling performance (> 2000 cycles at 2000 mA g−1).」** → 在 Zn||polyaniline 全电池中，作者报告“> 2000 cycles at 2000 mA g−1”，表明该体系兼顾高倍率与长寿命，而不是单一维度优化。
- **[原文]「The hydrogel electrolyte is easily accessible and biodegradable, making the zinc batteries attractive in terms of scalability and sustainability.」** → 关键发现还包括材料可获得性与可降解性，这使其价值不仅体现在实验室性能，也体现在潜在规模化应用逻辑上。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要强调了稳定循环时间、倍率工况与可持续性，但没有给出离子电导率、库仑效率、界面阻抗或与其他水凝胶/液态电解质的定量对比，因此其优势边界仍需阅读全文确认。
- **[推断]** 摘要中的验证对象主要是 Zn||Zn 与 Zn||polyaniline 两类体系，材料对其他正极体系、不同温度窗口以及长期实际降解环境中的适用性还有待进一步验证。
- **[推断]** “multi-perspective and in-depth mechanism investigation”说明作者做了机理研究，但摘要未展开具体表征手段与因果链条，后续阅读应重点核查其证据是否足以区分“抑枝晶”“抑副反应”“促传输”三种贡献的相对权重。

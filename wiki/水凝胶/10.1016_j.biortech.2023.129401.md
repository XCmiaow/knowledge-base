# Exploration of the interaction mechanism of lignocellulosic hybrid systems based on deep eutectic solvents.

**中文标题**: 深共熔溶剂作用下木质纤维素杂化体系相互作用机制探索

## 摘要

> [原文]「The interactions of three deep eutectic solvents (DES) choline chloride-glycerol (ChCl-GLY), ChCl-lactic acid (ChCl-LA) and ChCl-urea (ChCl-U) with cellulose-hemicellulose and cellulose-lignin hybrid systems were investigated using the simulated computational approach.」本文用模拟计算方法研究了 3 种深共熔溶剂与纤维素-半纤维素、纤维素-木质素杂化体系之间的相互作用。
>
> [原文]「DES pretreatment could disrupt the original hydrogen bonding network structure among the lignocellulosic components and reconstruct the new DES-lignocellulosic hydrogen bonding network structure.」摘要指出，DES 预处理的核心机制是打破原有氢键网络，并重建新的 DES-木质纤维素氢键网络。
>
> [原文]「ChCl-U had the highest intensity of action on the hybrid systems, removing 78.3% of the hydrogen bonds between cellulose-4-O-methyl Gluconic acid xylan (cellulose-Gxyl) and 68.4% of the hydrogen bonds between cellulose-Veratrylglycerol-b-guaiacyl ether (cellulose-VG), respectively.」[原文]「The increase of urea content facilitated the interaction between DES and lignocellulosic blend system.」[原文]「Finally, the addition of appropriate water (DES:H2O = 1:5) and DES formed the new DES-water hydrogen bonding network structure more favorable for the interaction of DES with lignocellulose.」结果上，ChCl-U 作用最强；提高尿素含量，以及加入适量水（DES:H2O = 1:5），都更有利于 DES 与木质纤维素发生作用。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「DES pretreatment could disrupt the original hydrogen bonding network structure among the lignocellulosic components and reconstruct the new DES-lignocellulosic hydrogen bonding network structure.」** — 给出了摘要层面的机制结论：DES 的作用不只是“溶解”或“接触”，而是通过重构氢键网络改变木质纤维素复合体系的相互作用关系。
- **[原文]「ChCl-U had the highest intensity of action on the hybrid systems, removing 78.3% of the hydrogen bonds between cellulose-4-O-methyl Gluconic acid xylan (cellulose-Gxyl) and 68.4% of the hydrogen bonds between cellulose-Veratrylglycerol-b-guaiacyl ether (cellulose-VG), respectively.」** — 比较了不同 DES 的作用强弱，并明确指出 ChCl-U 在两类杂化体系中表现最强。

## 方法

- **[原文]「The interactions of three deep eutectic solvents (DES) choline chloride-glycerol (ChCl-GLY), ChCl-lactic acid (ChCl-LA) and ChCl-urea (ChCl-U) with cellulose-hemicellulose and cellulose-lignin hybrid systems were investigated using the simulated computational approach.」** — 方法主体是模拟计算，比较 3 种 DES 对两类木质纤维素杂化体系的相互作用。
- **[原文]「Aiming to simulate DES pretreatment of real lignocellulosic biomass in nature.」** — 研究设计目标是用模型体系逼近天然木质纤维素生物质的 DES 预处理过程。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「ChCl-U had the highest intensity of action on the hybrid systems, removing 78.3% of the hydrogen bonds between cellulose-4-O-methyl Gluconic acid xylan (cellulose-Gxyl) and 68.4% of the hydrogen bonds between cellulose-Veratrylglycerol-b-guaiacyl ether (cellulose-VG), respectively.」** → 定量结果表明，ChCl-U 对 cellulose-Gxyl 和 cellulose-VG 两类体系的氢键破坏最强，分别达到 78.3% 和 68.4%。
- **[原文]「The increase of urea content facilitated the interaction between DES and lignocellulosic blend system.」** → 提高尿素含量会增强 DES 与木质纤维素混合体系的相互作用，说明 ChCl-U 的配比可能是重要调控因素。
- **[原文]「Finally, the addition of appropriate water (DES:H2O = 1:5) and DES formed the new DES-water hydrogen bonding network structure more favorable for the interaction of DES with lignocellulose.」** → 适量加水并不是简单稀释；在 DES:H2O = 1:5 条件下，会形成新的 DES-水氢键网络，从而进一步促进 DES 与木质纤维素的相互作用。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要只说明采用了 simulated computational approach，尚未给出真实预处理实验、产物分离效果或放大条件，因此当前结论更适合作为机理假设与配方筛选依据，后续需要结合实验体系验证其工程适用性。

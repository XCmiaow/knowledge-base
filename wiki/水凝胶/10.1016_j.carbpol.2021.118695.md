# Structure reorganization of cellulose hydrogel by green solvent exchange for potential plastic replacement.

**中文标题**: 通过绿色溶剂交换重组纤维素水凝胶结构以实现潜在塑料替代

## 摘要

> 研究从石油基塑料在生产到生命周期终点的环境问题出发，提出开发兼具一定塑料性能的可持续绿色材料。[原文]“Petroleum-based plastics have raised great environmental concerns from the beginning of their production to the end-of-life cycle. It is urgently needed to develop sustainable and green materials with certain plastic properties.”
>
> 作者以低品质棉纤维素为原料，通过绿色溶剂调控氢键网络制备生物基纤维素薄膜：先在无机盐中形成离子水凝胶，再经乙醇置换和空气干燥得到坚韧柔性薄膜，并将其指向可降解包装中的塑料替代应用。[原文]“Herein, biobased cellulose films are fabricated from low quality cotton cellulose by manipulating its hydrogen bonding network with green solvents. The cellulose is dispersed in inorganic salts (ZnCl2/CaCl2) to form ionic hydrogels, and then transformed into tough and flexible films through ethanol exchange and air drying.” [原文]“Such cellulose materials hold a potential for plastic replacement in the field of biodegradable packing.”

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]“Herein, biobased cellulose films are fabricated from low quality cotton cellulose by manipulating its hydrogen bonding network with green solvents.”** 贡献在于将低品质棉纤维素、绿色溶剂和氢键网络调控结合为一条生物基薄膜制备路线，面向塑料替代这一明确应用目标。

## 方法

- **[原文]“The cellulose is dispersed in inorganic salts (ZnCl2/CaCl2) to form ionic hydrogels, and then transformed into tough and flexible films through ethanol exchange and air drying.”** 方法路径是“ZnCl2/CaCl2 分散成离子水凝胶 → 乙醇置换 → 空气干燥成膜”，核心不是额外加工增强，而是利用溶剂交换驱动结构演化。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]“Without extra hot-pressing treatment, the aggregate structure of cellulose is re-organized with the disruption and re-construction of hydrogen bonds.”** 关键发现是即使“不经过额外热压”，纤维素聚集结构也能依靠氢键的破坏与重建完成重组，说明结构优化来自分子间作用网络的再组织而非后续强加工。
- **[原文]“Benefiting from the densely packed structure and highly in-plane orientation, the cellulose film presents outstanding optical, thermal and mechanical properties.”** 结果表明“致密堆积结构”和“高度面内取向”是光学、热学和力学性能提升的直接结构基础；**[推断]** 摘要未给出具体数值，因此目前只能确认性能方向性提升，无法比较提升幅度。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有报告任何量化性能数据、工艺参数窗口、对照样品结果或实际包装测试，因此该工作当前更像是“可替代塑料的概念验证”，后续需要全文中的定量证据来判断其工业可行性。

# Nanocellulose-enhanced organohydrogel with high-strength, conductivity, and anti-freezing properties for wearable strain sensors.

**中文标题**: 纳米纤维素增强型有机水凝胶：兼具高强度、导电性和抗冻性，用于可穿戴应变传感器

## 摘要

> 该研究聚焦离子导电水凝胶在应变传感中难以同时兼顾高力学性能、导电性与抗冻性的难题，[原文]「The use of ion-conductive hydrogels in strain sensors with high mechanical properties, conductivity, and anti-freezing properties is challenging.」作者通过 PAM/SA/TOCNs 体系在 DMSO/water 中自由基聚合并经 CaCl2 浸泡，制备出透明、导电且抗冻的有机水凝胶，[原文]「Here, high-strength, transparent, conductive, and anti-freezing organohydrogels were fabricated through the radical polymerization of polyacrylamide (PAM)/sodium alginate (SA)/TEMPO-oxidized cellulose nanofibrils (TOCNs) in a dimethyl sulfoxide (DMSO)/water solution, followed by soaking in a CaCl2 solution.」
>
> 材料表现出 1.04 MPa 拉伸强度、681% 伸长率、>84% 透过率、1.25 S m-1 离子电导率，并使传感器达到 GF = 2.1；在 DMSO/H2O 与 CaCl2 协同作用下，样品在 -20 °C 仍可 180° 弯折并保持 1.01 S m-1 导电性，[原文]「The resulting organohydrogels demonstrated a high strength (tensile strength of 1.04 MPa), stretchability (681%), transparency (>84% transmittance), and ionic conductivity (1.25 S m-1).」[原文]「The organohydrogel-based strain sensor showed a high strain sensitivity (GF = 2.1).」[原文]「In addition, due to a synergistic effect between the DMSO/H2O binary solvent and CaCl2, the organohydrogel remained flexible (could bend 180°) and conductive (1.01 S m-1) at -20 °C.」

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「This research provides a novel strategy to prepare ion-conductive organohydrogels with good mechanical properties, conductivity, and anti-freezing properties for use as flexible electronic materials.」** — 论文的核心贡献是提出了一种面向柔性电子材料的离子导电有机水凝胶制备策略。
- **[原文]「Interestingly, the TOCNs exerted a reinforcing effect on both the mechanical properties and ionic conductivity.」** — TOCNs 不仅承担增强相作用，还同时提升了力学性能与离子导电性。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Here, high-strength, transparent, conductive, and anti-freezing organohydrogels were fabricated through the radical polymerization of polyacrylamide (PAM)/sodium alginate (SA)/TEMPO-oxidized cellulose nanofibrils (TOCNs) in a dimethyl sulfoxide (DMSO)/water solution, followed by soaking in a CaCl2 solution.」** — 方法路径很清晰：先在 DMSO/water 溶液中对 PAM/SA/TOCNs 体系进行自由基聚合，再通过 CaCl2 浸泡完成后处理与性能构建。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The resulting organohydrogels demonstrated a high strength (tensile strength of 1.04 MPa), stretchability (681%), transparency (>84% transmittance), and ionic conductivity (1.25 S m-1).」** → 材料同时实现了高强度、超高延展性、高透光率与较高离子电导率，四项核心性能被一并拉起来。
- **[原文]「The organohydrogel-based strain sensor showed a high strain sensitivity (GF = 2.1).」** → 该有机水凝胶不仅是材料本体性能好，还能直接支撑应变传感器获得 GF = 2.1 的响应灵敏度。
- **[原文]「In addition, due to a synergistic effect between the DMSO/H2O binary solvent and CaCl2, the organohydrogel remained flexible (could bend 180°) and conductive (1.01 S m-1) at -20 °C.」** → 抗冻性能不是“冻而不裂”这种弱指标，而是在 -20 °C 下仍能 180° 弯折并保持 1.01 S m-1 导电性，说明其低温可用性较强。
- **[原文]「Interestingly, the TOCNs exerted a reinforcing effect on both the mechanical properties and ionic conductivity.」** → TOCNs 是该体系性能协同提升的重要组分，而不是单纯填料。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出循环稳定性、长期传感耐久性、响应/恢复时间与生物相容性数据，因此对可穿戴场景下的长期使用可靠性仍需进一步验证。
- **[推断]** 摘要只报告了 -20 °C 的低温表现，尚不能判断更低温区间、反复冻融条件或复杂环境湿度变化下的性能边界。

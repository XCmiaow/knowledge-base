# Stretchable and conductive cellulose hydrogel electrolytes for flexible and foldable solid-state supercapacitors.

## 摘要

> [原文]「the anti-freezing conductive hydrogel electrolytes with outstanding mechanical properties were synthesized by a facile and feasible method」说明本文的核心是制备兼具抗冻性、导电性与力学性能的水凝胶电解质。  
> [原文]「The mechanical and anti-freezing properties of the synthesized polyacrylamide/lithium lhloride/water soluble cellulose acetate (PAM/LiCl/WSCA) hydrogels are significantly enhanced with the addition of WSCA and LiCl」表明性能提升来自 WSCA 与 LiCl 的协同引入。  
> [原文]「The hydrogel electrolyte can remain soft and flexible at -80 °C」以及[原文]「The capacitor still maintains 64.64 % of its capacity at -40 °C」说明该体系不仅材料本身能在极寒下保持柔韧，还能支撑器件在低温环境中维持可用储能性能。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「The mechanical and anti-freezing properties of the synthesized polyacrylamide/lithium lhloride/water soluble cellulose acetate (PAM/LiCl/WSCA) hydrogels are significantly enhanced with the addition of WSCA and LiCl」** — 贡献在于明确提出通过 WSCA 与 LiCl 共引入，同时提升 PAM 基水凝胶的力学性能与抗冻性能，而不是只优化单一指标。
- **[原文]「the super-capacitor assembled with PAM/LiCl/WSCA hydrogel as electrolyte showed excellent stability in capacitance retention after 500 times of folding cycles and 10,000 times of charge and discharge tests」** — 贡献不止停留在材料表征，还把水凝胶真正组装进柔性超级电容器并验证折叠稳定性与循环稳定性。
- **[原文]「This facile strategy to fabricate anti-freezing conductive hydrogel electrolyte provides a new idea and way to the application of hydrogels as electrolytes in extreme cold environments」** — 论文把研究意义指向极寒环境电解质应用，强调方法简单且具有应用导向。

## 方法

- **[原文]「the anti-freezing conductive hydrogel electrolytes with outstanding mechanical properties were synthesized by a facile and feasible method」** — 方法层面强调合成路线简便可行，目标是同时获得抗冻、导电和力学强化的水凝胶电解质。
- **[原文]「the synthesized polyacrylamide/lithium lhloride/water soluble cellulose acetate (PAM/LiCl/WSCA) hydrogels」** — 材料体系由聚丙烯酰胺、氯化锂与水溶性醋酸纤维素构成，论文围绕 PAM/LiCl/WSCA 复合水凝胶展开。
- **[原文]「with the addition of WSCA and LiCl」** — 关键操作是向体系中引入 WSCA 与 LiCl，以实现性能增强与抗冻能力提升。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The tensile strength and toughness of the gels were gradually increased to 341 KPa and 1.2 MJ/m3, respectively」** → 凝胶拉伸强度提升到 341 KPa、韧性提升到 1.2 MJ/m3，说明材料在承载与耗能两个维度都得到增强。
- **[原文]「The hydrogel electrolyte can remain soft and flexible at -80 °C, displaying certain elasticity and electrical conductivity」** → 材料在 -80 °C 仍保持柔软、可弯曲，并保留一定弹性与导电性，这是其极寒适用性的直接证据。
- **[原文]「the super-capacitor assembled with PAM/LiCl/WSCA hydrogel as electrolyte showed excellent stability in capacitance retention after 500 times of folding cycles and 10,000 times of charge and discharge tests」** → 器件级验证覆盖 500 次折叠循环与 10,000 次充放电测试，说明其机械形变耐受性与电化学循环稳定性较强。
- **[原文]「The capacitor still maintains 64.64 % of its capacity at -40 °C」** → 即使在 -40 °C，器件仍保留 64.64 % 容量，说明低温下虽有性能衰减，但仍具备可工作的储能能力。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出电导率、比电容、能量密度或功率密度等关键电化学参数，因此目前更能确认其低温可用性与稳定性，尚不足以完整判断其综合储能性能上限。
- **[推断]** 摘要只给出 500 次折叠、10,000 次充放电和 -40 °C、-80 °C 等代表性结果，但没有说明不同配比之间的系统对比和失效机制，后续需要结合全文确认 WSCA 与 LiCl 的具体作用边界。
- **[推断]** “displaying certain elasticity and electrical conductivity”表述偏定性，后续若用于工程化设计，需要补充更细的温度区间性能曲线与长期服役数据。

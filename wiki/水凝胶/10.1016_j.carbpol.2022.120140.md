## 摘要

> [原文]「Superabsorbent hydrogels (SAH) are crosslinked three-dimensional networks distinguished by their super capacity to stabilize a large quantity of water without dissolving.」本文首先将超吸水水凝胶界定为一种在不溶解前提下可稳定大量水分的三维交联网络。
>
> [原文]「Cellulose and its derived nanocellulose can become SAHs as an appealing, versatile, and sustainable platform because of abundance, biodegradability, and renewability compared to petroleum-based materials.」作者据此强调，纤维素及其纳米纤维素衍生物相较石油基材料更具丰富性、可生物降解性和可再生性，因此适合作为可持续的 SAH 平台。
>
> [原文]「Finally, various applications of cellulose and nanocellulose SAH, challenges and existing problems, and proposed future research pathways were listed.」综述最终把应用场景、现存挑战与未来研究路径并列呈现，说明其目标不仅是材料介绍，也是在为后续研究整理议程。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Cellulose and its derived nanocellulose can become SAHs as an appealing, versatile, and sustainable platform because of abundance, biodegradability, and renewability compared to petroleum-based materials.」** — 核心贡献是把纤维素/纳米纤维素 SAH 明确定位为相较石油基材料更可持续的材料平台，并点明其可持续性的来源。
- **[原文]「Representative examples of cellulose and nanocellulose SAH and an in-depth discussion of structure-absorption relationships were listed.」** — 文章不仅罗列代表性体系，还把“结构-吸收关系”作为理解材料性能的中心问题。

## 方法

- **[原文]「In this review, a synthetic strategy that reflects starting cellulosic resources to their associated synthons, crosslinking types, and synthetic controlling factors was highlighted.」** — 方法上采用综述式梳理框架，从起始纤维素资源、对应合成单元、交联类型到合成控制因素逐层组织已有研究。
- **[原文]「Representative examples of cellulose and nanocellulose SAH and an in-depth discussion of structure-absorption relationships were listed.」** — 在组织材料时，作者同时结合代表性案例与结构-性能关系讨论，而不是单纯按材料名称堆叠文献。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「Such behavior enables them to engage in various applications.」** → 关键发现之一是，SAH 的高持水能力直接构成其多场景应用潜力的功能基础。
- **[原文]「Finally, various applications of cellulose and nanocellulose SAH, challenges and existing problems, and proposed future research pathways were listed.」** → 关键发现之二是，该领域的讨论已经从材料制备延伸到应用、问题诊断与未来路线设计。
- **[推断]** 摘要未提供具体吸水倍率、百分比、温度或其他定量指标，因此无法仅凭摘要比较不同纤维素/纳米纤维素 SAH 体系的性能高低。

## 不足与展望

- **[原文]「Finally, various applications of cellulose and nanocellulose SAH, challenges and existing problems, and proposed future research pathways were listed.」** — 文章明确把未来研究路径纳入综述收束部分。
- **[推断]** 作为 mini-review 的摘要，这里只说明“列出”了应用、挑战和未来路径，但没有展示筛选标准、优先级或证据强弱；深读时应重点回看正文中关于结构-吸收关系和问题清单的展开部分。

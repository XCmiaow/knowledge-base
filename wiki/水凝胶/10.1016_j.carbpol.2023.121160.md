# Tough and strong sustainable thermoplastic elastomers nanocomposite with self-assembly of SI-ATRP modified cellulose nanofibers.

**中文标题**: 具有 SI-ATRP 改性纤维素纳米纤维自组装结构的高韧高强可持续热塑性弹性体纳米复合材料

## 摘要

> [原文]「The ingenious design of sustainable thermoplastic elastomers (STPEs) is of great significance for the goal of the sustainable development.」[原文]「However, the preparation of STPEs with good mechanical performance is still complicated and challenging.」文章首先界定了问题场景：STPEs 对可持续发展目标重要，但高机械性能体系的制备仍复杂且具有挑战性。
>
> [原文]「Herein, to achieve a simple preparation of STPEs with strong mechanical properties, two biobased monomers (tetrahydrofurfuryl methacrylate (THFMA) and lauryl methacrylate (LMA)) were copolymerized into poly (THFMA-co-LMA) (PTL) and grafted onto TEMPO oxidized cellulose nanofiber (TOCN) via one-pot surface-initiated atom transfer radical polymerization (SI ATRP).」[原文]「The grafting modified TOCN could be self-assembled into nano-enhanced phases in STPEs, which are conducive to the double enhancement of the strength and toughness of the STPEs, and the size of nano-enhanced phases is mainly affected by TOCN fiber length and molecular weight of grafting chains.」作者用 THFMA 与 LMA 共聚得到 PTL，并通过一锅法 SI-ATRP 将其接枝到 TOCN 上，使改性 TOCN 在 STPEs 中自组装为纳米增强相，从而服务于强度与韧性的同步提升。
>
> [原文]「Especially, with the addition of 7 wt% TOCN, tensile strength, tensile strain, toughness, and glass transition temperature (Tg) of TOCN based STPEs (TOCN@PTL) exhibited 140 %, 36 %, 215 %, and 6.8 °C increase respectively, which confirmed the leading level in the field of bio-based elastomers.」摘要中最关键的结果是：在 7 wt% TOCN 下，TOCN@PTL 的拉伸强度、拉伸应变、韧性和 Tg 分别提升 140 %、36 %、215 % 和 6.8 °C。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Herein, to achieve a simple preparation of STPEs with strong mechanical properties, two biobased monomers (tetrahydrofurfuryl methacrylate (THFMA) and lauryl methacrylate (LMA)) were copolymerized into poly (THFMA-co-LMA) (PTL) and grafted onto TEMPO oxidized cellulose nanofiber (TOCN) via one-pot surface-initiated atom transfer radical polymerization (SI ATRP).」** — 核心创新在于把两种生物基单体 THFMA 与 LMA、聚合物 PTL、TOCN 载体以及一锅法 SI-ATRP 接枝流程整合成一条相对简洁的制备路线。
- **[原文]「The grafting modified TOCN could be self-assembled into nano-enhanced phases in STPEs, which are conducive to the double enhancement of the strength and toughness of the STPEs, and the size of nano-enhanced phases is mainly affected by TOCN fiber length and molecular weight of grafting chains.」** — 文章把“自组装纳米增强相”作为结构设计抓手，并指出其尺寸受 TOCN 纤维长度与接枝链分子量调控。
- **[原文]「In general, this work constitutes a proof for the chemical modification and self-assembly behavior of TOCN by one-pot SI ATRP, and provides an alternative strategy for the preparation of high-performance STPEs.」** — 作者将该工作定位为 TOCN 化学改性与自组装行为的概念验证，并强调其对高性能 STPEs 制备的策略意义。

## 方法

- **[原文]「two biobased monomers (tetrahydrofurfuryl methacrylate (THFMA) and lauryl methacrylate (LMA)) were copolymerized into poly (THFMA-co-LMA) (PTL) and grafted onto TEMPO oxidized cellulose nanofiber (TOCN) via one-pot surface-initiated atom transfer radical polymerization (SI ATRP).」** — 方法主线是先将 THFMA 和 LMA 共聚为 PTL，再借助一锅法表面引发 ATRP 把 PTL 接枝到 TOCN 上。
- **[原文]「The grafting modified TOCN could be self-assembled into nano-enhanced phases in STPEs」** — 材料设计的关键步骤不是单纯填充，而是让接枝后的 TOCN 在 STPEs 内部自组装形成纳米增强相。
- **[原文]「the size of nano-enhanced phases is mainly affected by TOCN fiber length and molecular weight of grafting chains.」** — 方法变量主要落在 TOCN 纤维长度和接枝链分子量两个维度上，用于调节纳米增强相尺寸。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「Especially, with the addition of 7 wt% TOCN, tensile strength, tensile strain, toughness, and glass transition temperature (Tg) of TOCN based STPEs (TOCN@PTL) exhibited 140 %, 36 %, 215 %, and 6.8 °C increase respectively, which confirmed the leading level in the field of bio-based elastomers.」** → 在 7 wt% TOCN 条件下，TOCN@PTL 同时获得 140 % 强度提升、36 % 拉伸应变提升、215 % 韧性提升和 6.8 °C 的 Tg 提升，这构成了摘要中最完整的定量证据。
- **[原文]「which confirmed the leading level in the field of bio-based elastomers.」** → 作者将上述性能增益解释为该体系达到生物基弹性体领域的领先水平。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要给出了 7 wt% TOCN 下的相对提升幅度，但没有提供绝对力学数值、测试条件、误差范围或与更多对照体系的横向比较，因此当前还难判断该体系在不同加工窗口和应用场景中的可复现性。
- **[推断]** 摘要强调纳米增强相尺寸主要受 TOCN 纤维长度和接枝链分子量影响，后续工作可以围绕这两个变量建立更清晰的构效关系，并验证其对长期热机械稳定性的影响。

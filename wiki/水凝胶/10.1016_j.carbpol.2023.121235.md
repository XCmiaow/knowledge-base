# Adhesive injectable cellulose-based hydrogels with rapid self-healing and sustained drug release capability for promoting wound healing.

**中文标题**: 具有快速自愈与持续释药能力、可促进伤口愈合的黏附性可注射纤维素基水凝胶

## 摘要

> [原文]「Injectable biocompatible hydrogels with multiple functions, including self-healing, adhesion, antibacterial activity, and suitable mechanical properties, are highly desirable for enhancing wound healing.」论文首先把问题定义为：理想创伤敷料需要同时具备可注射、生物相容、自愈、黏附、抗菌与合适力学性能等多重功能。  
> [原文]「a new class of multi-functional injectable self-healing cellulose-based hydrogels was synthesised using dynamic covalent acylhydrazone linkages for wound dressing」作者据此构建了一类基于动态共价酰腙键的纤维素基多功能自愈水凝胶，并将其用于创面敷料场景。  
> [原文]「they not only promoted the wound-healing process but also presented excellent haemostatic effects」与[原文]「significantly enhanced angiogenic ability in vivo」摘要给出的核心结论是，该材料在体内兼具促愈合、止血和促血管生成潜力。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「a new class of multi-functional injectable self-healing cellulose-based hydrogels was synthesised using dynamic covalent acylhydrazone linkages for wound dressing」** — 核心创新是用动态共价酰腙键构建纤维素基可注射自愈水凝胶，把“可注射 + 自愈 + 多功能”整合进同一创伤敷料体系。
- **[原文]「drug release and in vitro antibacterial activities against Gram-positive and Gram-negative bacteria confirmed the sustained drug-release capacity of the hydrogels」** — 材料不仅是被动覆盖层，还被设计成可持续释药并覆盖革兰氏阳性菌和阴性菌抗菌需求的活性敷料平台。
- **[原文]「they not only promoted the wound-healing process but also presented excellent haemostatic effects」** — 文章把终点从单纯材料性能推进到功能疗效，直接指向止血与伤口愈合两类临床相关结果。

## 方法

- **[原文]「The carboxymethyl cellulose-graft-adipic dihydrazide (CMC-ADH)/4-Formylbenzoic acid-terminated poly(ethylene glycol) (PEG-FBA) (CMC-ADH/PEG-FBA) hydrogels have adjustable gelation time and excellent self-healing ability.」** — 材料设计采用 CMC-ADH 与 4-Formylbenzoic acid-terminated PEG-FBA 组成的 CMC-ADH/PEG-FBA 水凝胶体系，摘要明确点出其可调凝胶时间和优异自愈能力。
- **[原文]「In addition, drug release and in vitro antibacterial activities against Gram-positive and Gram-negative bacteria confirmed the sustained drug-release capacity of the hydrogels.」** — 体外部分围绕释药和抗菌展开，用以验证持续释药能力及对两类细菌的抑制效果。
- **[原文]「haemostasis and wound-healing effects were investigated using an in vivo haemorrhaging liver mouse model and a full-thickness skin defect model」** — 体内验证采用出血性肝损伤小鼠模型和全层皮肤缺损模型，分别对应止血与创面修复两个应用维度。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The carboxymethyl cellulose-graft-adipic dihydrazide (CMC-ADH)/4-Formylbenzoic acid-terminated poly(ethylene glycol) (PEG-FBA) (CMC-ADH/PEG-FBA) hydrogels have adjustable gelation time and excellent self-healing ability.」** → 关键材料组合中包含“4-Formylbenzoic acid-terminated”这一结构单元，且该体系实现了可调凝胶时间与快速自愈这一对临床操作性很重要的性能组合。
- **[原文]「drug release and in vitro antibacterial activities against Gram-positive and Gram-negative bacteria confirmed the sustained drug-release capacity of the hydrogels」** → 水凝胶的持续释药能力不是孤立声称，而是与对革兰氏阳性菌、阴性菌的体外抗菌活性一起被验证。
- **[原文]「they not only promoted the wound-healing process but also presented excellent haemostatic effects」** → 在体内模型中，材料同时实现促愈合和止血，说明其功能并非单点改进，而是覆盖创伤处理的多个关键环节。
- **[原文]「The CMC-ADH/PEG-FBA gels also exhibited good adhesion to irregular wounds and significantly enhanced angiogenic ability in vivo.」** → 该体系对不规则创面具有良好黏附性，并能显著增强体内血管生成，这对复杂创面贴附和后续组织再生都很关键。
- **[原文]「hydrogels can quickly stop bleeding, provide a moist and closed environment for the wound to prevent bacterial invasion, release ciprofloxacin (CIP), reduce inflammatory reactions, and promote wound tissue regeneration」** → 作者给出的机制链条是“快速止血 + 湿润封闭屏障 + CIP 释放 + 抗炎 + 组织再生促进”，把材料效应与治疗过程连接起来。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出凝胶时间、释药曲线、抗菌强度、止血效率或愈合提升幅度等定量数据，因此目前更适合把这项工作理解为“功能完整性已被证明”，而不是“关键性能边界已被充分量化”。
- **[推断]** 体内结果基于小鼠出血肝模型和全层皮肤缺损模型，距离复杂临床创面、长期安全性和不同给药剂量优化之间仍有转化间隙。
- **[推断]** 摘要强调释放 ciprofloxacin (CIP) 带来的优势，但未说明耐药风险、药物负载上限与空白水凝胶对照贡献，后续值得拆分“材料本体效应”与“药物效应”。

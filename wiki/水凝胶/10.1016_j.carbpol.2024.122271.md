# Zwitterionic cellulose nanofibers-based hydrogels with high toughness, ionic conductivity, and healable capability in cryogenic environments.

**中文标题**: 基于两性离子纤维素纳米纤维的水凝胶：在低温环境下兼具高韧性、离子电导率与可愈合能力

## 摘要

> [原文] "Achieving high toughness, self-healing, and ionic conductivity in cryogenic environments is vital to broaden their applications." 文章把问题定义为低温环境下同时实现高韧性、自愈和离子导电性，这是扩展水凝胶应用范围的关键。  
> [原文] "Herein, we present a novel approach to simultaneously enhance the toughness, self-healing, and ionic conductivity of hydrogels, via inducing non-freezable water within the zwitterionic cellulose-based hydrogel skeleton." 文章提出的核心方案是在两性离子纤维素基水凝胶骨架内诱导非冻结水，从而同步提升三项性能。  
> [原文] "This approach enables resulting hydrogel to achieve an exceptional toughness of 10.8 MJ m-3, rapid self-healing capability (98.9 % in 30 min), and high ionic conductivity (2.9 S m-1), even when subjected to -40 °C" 该体系在 -40 °C 下仍保持 10.8 MJ m-3 韧性、30 min 内 98.9 % 自愈率和 2.9 S m-1 离子电导率，说明其低温适用性很强。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]** "This approach enables resulting hydrogel to achieve an exceptional toughness of 10.8 MJ m-3, rapid self-healing capability (98.9 % in 30 min), and high ionic conductivity (2.9 S m-1), even when subjected to -40 °C, superior to the state-of-the-art hydrogels." 贡献在于把高韧性、快速自愈和高离子导电性同时拉到较高水平，而且这些性能在 -40 °C 仍成立，并且摘要明确声称优于现有先进水凝胶。  
- **[原文]** "Importantly, this strategy harnesses the non-freezable water molecular state of the zwitterionic cellulose nanofibers network, eliminating the need for additional antifreeze and organic solvents." 贡献还在于不依赖额外防冻剂和有机溶剂，而是直接利用两性离子纤维素纳米纤维网络中的非冻结水分子态实现抗冻。  
- **[原文]** "Demonstrating its potential, this hydrogel can be used in smart laminated materials, such as aircraft windshields." 文章把应用场景指向智能层压材料，并给出飞机风挡这一高价值示例。

## 方法

- **[原文]** "Herein, we present a novel approach to simultaneously enhance the toughness, self-healing, and ionic conductivity of hydrogels, via inducing non-freezable water within the zwitterionic cellulose-based hydrogel skeleton." 方法主线是在两性离子纤维素基骨架内诱导非冻结水，用分子层面的水状态调控来解决低温性能问题。  
- **[原文]** "Mechanism analyses reveal that a significant amount of non-freezable water with robust electrostatic interactions is formed within zwitterionic cellulose nanofibers-modified polyurethane molecular networks" 机制分析表明，非冻结水并不是简单困在体系里，而是在两性离子纤维素纳米纤维改性聚氨酯分子网络中，通过强静电相互作用稳定存在。  
- **[原文]** "Furthermore, the dynamic Zn coordination within these supramolecular molecule chains enhances interfacial interactions, thereby promoting rapid subzero self-healing and exceptional mechanical strength." 文章还引入动态 Zn 配位来增强界面相互作用，从而支撑低于 0 °C 条件下的快速自愈和高机械强度。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]** "This approach enables resulting hydrogel to achieve an exceptional toughness of 10.8 MJ m-3, rapid self-healing capability (98.9 % in 30 min), and high ionic conductivity (2.9 S m-1), even when subjected to -40 °C" 最关键的结果是四个定量信息同时成立：韧性 10.8 MJ m-3、自愈率 98.9 %、自愈时间 30 min、离子电导率 2.9 S m-1，并且测试温度到 -40 °C。  
- **[原文]** "a significant amount of non-freezable water with robust electrostatic interactions is formed within zwitterionic cellulose nanofibers-modified polyurethane molecular networks, imparting superior freezing tolerance and versatility to the hydrogel." 关键机理发现是大量带强静电相互作用的非冻结水赋予了材料优异的耐冻性和适用性。  
- **[原文]** "the dynamic Zn coordination within these supramolecular molecule chains enhances interfacial interactions, thereby promoting rapid subzero self-healing and exceptional mechanical strength." 另一个关键发现是 Zn 动态配位不仅参与结构稳定，还直接促进了亚零度自愈和机械增强。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要强调了 -40 °C 下的力学、自愈和导电表现，但没有给出循环冻融稳定性、长期服役寿命和规模化制备信息，这些会决定其是否能真正进入工程应用。  
- **[推断]** 摘要提出了飞机风挡等智能层压材料场景，但没有说明界面粘接、光学透明性、环境老化或真实器件验证结果，后续需要面向具体场景补齐。  
- **[推断]** 文章把优势归因于非冻结水和 Zn 配位协同作用，但各机制贡献的独立定量拆分在摘要里没有展开，后续值得关注更细的机制对比实验。

# Dual cross-linked cellulose-based hydrogel for dendrites-inhibited flexible zinc-ion energy storage devices with ultra-long cycles and high energy density.

**中文标题**: 双重交联纤维素基水凝胶用于抑制枝晶的柔性锌离子储能器件，实现超长循环与高能量密度

## 摘要

> [原文]「This work presents a dual cross-linked cellulose-based hydrogel electrolyte with chemical cross-linking from covalent bonding and physical cross-linking from hydrogen bonding.」本文构建了一种双重交联纤维素基水凝胶电解质，其中化学交联来自共价键，物理交联来自氢键。
>
> [原文]「This electrolyte demonstrated outstanding mechanical strength and porous structure with abundant hydroxyl groups, which facilitates the migration of Zn2+ and suppresses the formation of undesirable zinc dendrite, thereby enhancing the ion conductivity (18.46 ± 0.39 mS cm-1 at room temperature) and extending electrochemical stability window (0-2.23 V).」该电解质凭借高机械强度和富羟基多孔结构促进 Zn2+ 迁移并抑制锌枝晶，在室温下实现了 18.46 ± 0.39 mS cm-1 的离子电导率以及 0-2.23 V 的电化学稳定窗口。
>
> [原文]「Zn||Zn symmetric cells based on this electrolyte demonstrated stable stripping/plating cycles of 3000 h at a current density of 1 mA cm-2. Furthermore, the corresponding flexible zinc-ion hybrid capacitor retains a 90.3 % capacity over 100,000 cycles at 10 A g-1, while remaining functional across various folding angles.」基于该电解质的 Zn||Zn 对称电池在 1 mA cm-2 下实现了 3000 h 稳定沉积/剥离循环，对应柔性锌离子混合电容器在 10 A g-1 下经过 100,000 次循环后仍保持 90.3 % 容量，并且在不同折叠角度下仍可工作。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「This work presents a dual cross-linked cellulose-based hydrogel electrolyte with chemical cross-linking from covalent bonding and physical cross-linking from hydrogen bonding.」** — 提出了纤维素基双重交联水凝胶电解质设计，将共价化学交联与氢键物理交联结合到同一电解质网络中。
- **[原文]「This electrolyte demonstrated outstanding mechanical strength and porous structure with abundant hydroxyl groups, which facilitates the migration of Zn2+ and suppresses the formation of undesirable zinc dendrite, thereby enhancing the ion conductivity (18.46 ± 0.39 mS cm-1 at room temperature) and extending electrochemical stability window (0-2.23 V).」** — 将材料结构特征与电化学功能直接对应起来，说明其通过促进 Zn2+ 迁移和抑制枝晶来同步提升导电性与稳定窗口。
- **[原文]「Hence, this biomass-derived hydrogel electrolyte holds promise for flexible energy storage device applications.」** — 强调了该生物质来源水凝胶电解质面向柔性储能器件应用的潜力。

## 方法

- **[原文]「This work presents a dual cross-linked cellulose-based hydrogel electrolyte with chemical cross-linking from covalent bonding and physical cross-linking from hydrogen bonding.」** — 核心方法是构建兼具共价键化学交联和氢键物理交联的纤维素基水凝胶电解质。
- **[原文]「This electrolyte demonstrated outstanding mechanical strength and porous structure with abundant hydroxyl groups, which facilitates the migration of Zn2+ and suppresses the formation of undesirable zinc dendrite」** — 方法设计依赖高机械强度、富羟基和多孔结构，以同时服务于离子迁移与枝晶抑制。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「thereby enhancing the ion conductivity (18.46 ± 0.39 mS cm-1 at room temperature) and extending electrochemical stability window (0-2.23 V).」** → 该电解质在室温下的离子电导率达到 18.46 ± 0.39 mS cm-1，电化学稳定窗口扩展到 0-2.23 V。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 虽然 [原文]「Hence, this biomass-derived hydrogel electrolyte holds promise for flexible energy storage device applications.」表明其应用前景明确，但摘要没有提供具体纤维素来源、交联剂组成、制备条件以及 full-cell 能量密度的定量结果，因此“high energy density”的量化边界和可制造性仍需正文验证。
- **[推断]** 由于摘要只报告了 [原文]「ion conductivity (18.46 ± 0.39 mS cm-1 at room temperature)」以及 [原文]「3000 h at a current density of 1 mA cm-2」和 [原文]「90.3 % capacity over 100,000 cycles at 10 A g-1」等有限工况，后续还需要考察更宽温区、更高电流密度/面载量及长期封装条件下的稳定性。

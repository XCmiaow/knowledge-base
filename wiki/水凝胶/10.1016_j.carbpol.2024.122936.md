# A flexible, antifreezing, and long-term stable cellulose ionic conductive hydrogel via one-step preparation for flexible electronic sensors.

**一种通过一步法制备、用于柔性电子传感器的柔性、抗冻且长期稳定的纤维素离子导电水凝胶**:

## 摘要

> [原文]「In this work, a simple strategy without additives was provided to prepare an ionic conductive cellulose hydrogel (ICH) in one step through molten salt hydrate.」本文提出了一种无需添加剂、通过熔融盐水合物一步制备离子导电纤维素水凝胶（ICH）的简化方案。
>
> [原文]「The hydrogel featured controllable mechanical properties (0.19 MPa - 0.67 MPa), high ionic conductivity (78.96 mS/cm), excellent freezing resistance (-80 °C).」该水凝胶同时具备 0.19 MPa - 0.67 MPa 的可调力学性能、78.96 mS/cm 的高离子电导率以及 -80 °C 的抗冻能力。
>
> [原文]「the ICH exhibited long-term stability in water-retention ability (75.6 %, after 90 days) and ionic conductivity (85 %, after 90 days) over a wide working temperature range (-80 °C to 40 °C).」作者进一步强调，该材料在 -80 °C to 40 °C 范围内仍可在 90 days 后保持 75.6 % 的保水能力和 85 % 的离子电导稳定性。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Although, antifreezing/water-retaining additives could alleviate these problems, the reduced performance and complex preparation methods seriously limit their development. In this work, a simple strategy without additives was provided to prepare an ionic conductive cellulose hydrogel (ICH) in one step through molten salt hydrate.」** — 核心贡献在于绕开传统抗冻/保水添加剂带来的性能折损与制备复杂性，用无添加剂的一步法同时回应导电水凝胶在宽温域下的性能与工艺问题。
- **[原文]「More importantly, due to the existing metal salts component, the ICH exhibited long-term stability in water-retention ability (75.6 %, after 90 days) and ionic conductivity (85 %, after 90 days) over a wide working temperature range (-80 °C to 40 °C).」** — 文章的真正亮点不是单点性能，而是把长期保水、导电稳定性与宽温工作窗口整合到同一材料体系里。

## 方法

- **[原文]「a simple strategy without additives was provided to prepare an ionic conductive cellulose hydrogel (ICH) in one step through molten salt hydrate」** — 方法上采用熔融盐水合物作为一步法制备路径，直接构建离子导电纤维素水凝胶，重点是“without additives”和“in one step”。
- **[原文]「due to the existing metal salts component」** — 从摘要可见，材料性能维持与体系中已有金属盐组分直接相关，说明该组分既参与导电，也帮助保水与抗冻表现的建立。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The hydrogel featured controllable mechanical properties (0.19 MPa - 0.67 MPa), high ionic conductivity (78.96 mS/cm), excellent freezing resistance (-80 °C).」** → 该材料在力学、导电与低温适应性之间取得了同时成立的组合，其中 0.19 MPa - 0.67 MPa 表明其力学性能可调，78.96 mS/cm 表明其具备较高离子传输能力，-80 °C 则证明其抗冻下限较低。
- **[原文]「the ICH exhibited long-term stability in water-retention ability (75.6 %, after 90 days) and ionic conductivity (85 %, after 90 days) over a wide working temperature range (-80 °C to 40 °C).」** → 长期稳定性数据给出了最关键的应用证据：在 90 days 后，保水能力仍有 75.6 %，离子电导仍保持 85 %，且适用温域覆盖 -80 °C to 40 °C。
- **[原文]「Benefiting from these advantages, the ICH exhibited excellent electromechanical performance in human movement detection and movement direction identification, indicating a promising apply for flexible electronic device.」** → 这些材料特性最终被落实到人体运动检测与运动方向识别场景，说明其性能并非停留在材料表征层面，而已对柔性电子器件应用形成直接支撑。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有交代熔融盐水合物体系的具体组成比例、制备条件、传感响应指标、循环稳定性和生物相容性，因此目前只能确认其具备应用潜力，尚不足以判断其在可穿戴器件中的长期实际部署表现。
- **[推断]** 摘要将长期稳定性归因于金属盐组分，但未展开机理证据；后续值得验证金属盐如何同时影响纤维素网络、含水状态与离子迁移。

# Natural cellulose reinforced multifunctional eutectogels for wearable sensors and epidermal electrodes.

**中文标题**: 天然纤维素增强的用于可穿戴传感器与表皮电极的多功能深共熔凝胶

## 摘要

> [原文]「Wearable electronics significantly impact health monitoring, clinical care, and human-machine interfaces.」可穿戴电子的应用场景直接指向健康监测、临床护理与人机交互。  
> [原文]「Despite these advances, most eutectogels serve only as sensors or epidermal electrodes and rarely fulfill both functions simultaneously. In this study, we present a multifunctional eutectogel designed to function in both ways.」本文瞄准深共熔凝胶通常只能承担传感器或表皮电极单一功能的问题，提出可同时兼顾两种用途的多功能体系。  
> [原文]「Incorporating natural cotton cellulose nanofibers as nanofillers reinforced the tensile strength of the resultant eutectogel by 7.47 times compared to that of the pure eutectogel, reaching 4.93 MPa. This eutectogel exhibited high ionic conductivity (1.22 S m-1), strong adhesion (1562.2 kPa to iron), self-healing ability (80.37% strain recovery and 80.53% tensile strength recovery), a broad temperature tolerance (-40 to 80 °C), and antibacterial properties. It demonstrates high sensitivity for the real-time strain detection of human activities and accurately captures electrophysiological signals, enabling the control of a small car.」作者通过引入天然棉纤维素纳米纤维，实现了力学增强、离子导电、强黏附、自愈、宽温适应和抗菌等多性能集成，并完成了人体活动监测与电生理信号控制小车的应用演示。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「most eutectogels serve only as sensors or epidermal electrodes and rarely fulfill both functions simultaneously. In this study, we present a multifunctional eutectogel designed to function in both ways.」** — 核心贡献是把可穿戴应变传感与表皮电极两类通常分离的功能整合到同一深共熔凝胶材料中。
- **[原文]「Incorporating natural cotton cellulose nanofibers as nanofillers reinforced the tensile strength of the resultant eutectogel by 7.47 times compared to that of the pure eutectogel, reaching 4.93 MPa.」** — 该工作把天然棉纤维素纳米纤维明确作为增强相，用于解决深共熔凝胶力学不足的问题。
- **[原文]「This eutectogel exhibited high ionic conductivity (1.22 S m-1), strong adhesion (1562.2 kPa to iron), self-healing ability (80.37% strain recovery and 80.53% tensile strength recovery), a broad temperature tolerance (-40 to 80 °C), and antibacterial properties.」** — [推断] 文章真正想强调的不是单一指标最优，而是面向柔性可穿戴电子的多性能协同。
- **[原文]「It demonstrates high sensitivity for the real-time strain detection of human activities and accurately captures electrophysiological signals, enabling the control of a small car.」** — 贡献已经从材料表征延伸到实时应变检测、电生理采集和外部器件控制的概念验证。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Eutectogels, which utilize deep eutectic solvents (DES) address the drawbacks of hydrogels, such as weight loss and poor temperature tolerance, as well as the high costs and toxicities associated with ionogels.」** — 方法选型以深共熔凝胶为基础，目标是同时规避传统水凝胶的失水与耐温不足问题，以及离子凝胶的高成本和毒性问题。
- **[原文]「In this study, we present a multifunctional eutectogel designed to function in both ways.」** — 方法导向是按“双功能材料”来设计体系，而不是先做单功能器件再外加兼容性修补。
- **[原文]「Incorporating natural cotton cellulose nanofibers as nanofillers」** — 方法核心是在深共熔凝胶中引入天然棉纤维素纳米纤维作为纳米填料。
- **[原文]「accurately captures electrophysiological signals, enabling the control of a small car」** — [推断] 验证路线同时覆盖传感端和电极端，说明作者按器件级应用而非纯材料级指标来组织实验。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「reinforced the tensile strength of the resultant eutectogel by 7.47 times compared to that of the pure eutectogel, reaching 4.93 MPa」** → 相比纯深共熔凝胶，拉伸强度提升 7.47 倍，最终达到 4.93 MPa。
- **[原文]「high ionic conductivity (1.22 S m-1), strong adhesion (1562.2 kPa to iron)」** → 材料同时实现 1.22 S m-1 的高离子电导率，以及对铁 1562.2 kPa 的强黏附。
- **[原文]「self-healing ability (80.37% strain recovery and 80.53% tensile strength recovery)」** → 自愈后可恢复 80.37% 的应变与 80.53% 的拉伸强度。
- **[原文]「a broad temperature tolerance (-40 to 80 °C), and antibacterial properties」** → 材料可在 -40 到 80 °C 的宽温区间工作，并兼具抗菌特性。
- **[原文]「It demonstrates high sensitivity for the real-time strain detection of human activities and accurately captures electrophysiological signals, enabling the control of a small car.」** → 该体系既能高灵敏实时检测人体活动应变，也能稳定采集电生理信号并驱动小车控制。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出 deep eutectic solvents 的具体组成、天然棉纤维素纳米纤维的添加量、传感灵敏度系数、表皮电极的信噪比或长期循环稳定性，因此目前只能确认其多功能成立，尚不能判断各项性能之间是否存在明显权衡。
- **[推断]** 摘要中的应用演示只写到「enabling the control of a small car」，后续需要看正文才能判断其在复杂动作、长时间贴肤和不同环境湿度下的可靠性与可重复性。

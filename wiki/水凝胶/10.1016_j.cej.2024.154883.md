# Strong adhesive, high conductive and environmentally stable eutectogel based on “Water in deep eutectic Solvent”: Ultrasensitive flexible temperature and strain sensors

**中文标题**: 基于“Water in deep eutectic solvent”的强粘附、高导电、环境稳定共晶凝胶用于超灵敏柔性温度与应变传感

## 摘要

Eutectogel is a promising candidate of conductive materials for flexible electronic sensors, but current eutectogel sensors often suffer from poor mechanics, limited ionic conductivity, and hygroscopic instability. Here, a eutectogel is fabricated by one-step photoinitiated copolymerization of acrylic acid, hydroxyethyl acrylate, and highly branched modified polyethyleneimine in “water in deep eutectic solvent”. The optimized eutectogel reaches adhesive strength of 132 ± 9 kPa on porcine skin, ultrahigh elongation of 3500%, conductivity of 2.7 ± 0.1 mS cm−1, environmental antidrying/antifreezing stability for around 30 days, and a broad working temperature range of −40 to 60 °C. It also shows flame resistance, resistance to tear and crack propagation, and high sensitivity in temperature and strain sensing, including a minimum detectable strain of 0.01%.

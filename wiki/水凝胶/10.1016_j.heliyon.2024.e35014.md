# Polysaccharide hydrogels for skin wound healing

## 摘要

> **[原文]** "Advances in the development and utilization of polysaccharide materials are highly promising, offering prominent applications in the field of tissue engineering for addressing diverse clinical needs, including wound healing, bone regeneration, cartilage repair, and treatment of conditions such as arthritis." 文章指出，多糖材料在组织工程中前景广泛，覆盖伤口愈合、骨再生、软骨修复及关节炎治疗等多类临床需求。  
> **[原文]** "This review presents an overview of the biomedical applications of natural polysaccharide hydrogels and their derivatives." 这是一篇综述，目标是系统梳理天然多糖水凝胶及其衍生物的生物医学应用。  
> **[原文]** "Furthermore, the use of polysaccharide hydrogels in the context of wound-healing applications, including hemostatic effects, antimicrobial activities, anti-inflammatory properties, and promotion of angiogenesis, is presented." 其与伤口愈合最相关的讨论集中在止血、抗菌、抗炎和促血管生成四个功能维度。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]** "Herein, we discuss the latest advancements in the fabrication, physicochemical properties, and biomedical applications of polysaccharide-based hydrogels, including chitosan, hyaluronic acid, alginate, and cellulose." 综述的核心贡献是把多糖基水凝胶的制备、理化性质与生物医学应用放在同一框架下讨论，并明确聚焦壳聚糖、透明质酸、海藻酸盐和纤维素四类代表材料。

## 方法

- **[原文]** "This review presents an overview of the biomedical applications of natural polysaccharide hydrogels and their derivatives." 方法层面上，这篇文章采用综述式整合，对天然多糖水凝胶及其衍生物进行范围梳理。  
- **[原文]** "Various processing techniques applicable to polysaccharide materials are explored, such as the transformation of polysaccharide hydrogels into electrospun nanofibers, microneedles, microspheres, and nanogels." 文章进一步按加工路线展开，覆盖静电纺丝纳米纤维、微针、微球和纳凝胶等转化形态。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]** "Novel polysaccharide materials are popular owing to their inherent stability, biocompatibility, and repeatability." 摘要强调，多糖新材料受关注的直接原因是其内在稳定性、生物相容性和可重复性。  
- **[原文]** "Furthermore, the use of polysaccharide hydrogels in the context of wound-healing applications, including hemostatic effects, antimicrobial activities, anti-inflammatory properties, and promotion of angiogenesis, is presented." 面向皮肤伤口修复时，多糖水凝胶并非单一功能材料，而是同时对应止血、抗菌、抗炎和促血管生成四类修复机制。  
- **[推断]** 摘要未提供具体定量数据、百分比、温度或实验对比数值，说明这里的“关键发现”主要是研究版图与功能分类层面的综合结论，而不是单项实验结果。

## 不足与展望

- **[原文]** "Finally, we address the challenges encountered in the development of polysaccharide hydrogels and outline the potential prospects in this evolving field." 文章明确把“发展中的挑战”和“未来前景”作为结尾重点，说明该领域仍处于持续演化阶段。  
- **[推断]** 仅凭摘要无法判断不同多糖体系在伤口愈合中的性能优先级，也无法比较不同加工形态在具体应用场景中的优势边界。  
- **[推断]** 如果后续精读正文，优先应追踪四类材料（chitosan、hyaluronic acid、alginate、cellulose）与四类加工形态（electrospun nanofibers、microneedles、microspheres、nanogels）的对应关系，以及它们如何映射到止血、抗菌、抗炎和促血管生成机制。

# Muscle-inspired anisotropic carboxymethyl cellulose-based double-network conductive hydrogels for flexible strain sensors

**中文标题**: 仿肌肉各向异性羧甲基纤维素基双网络导电水凝胶用于柔性应变传感

## 摘要

> [原文] "Conductive hydrogels are considered one of the most promising materials for preparing flexible sensors due to their flexible and extensible properties. However, conventional hydrogels' weak mechanical and isotropic properties are greatly limited in practical applications." 该研究围绕导电水凝胶在柔性传感器中的应用潜力展开，同时明确指出传统水凝胶受限于力学性能弱和各向同性问题。  
> [原文] "Here, the internal structure of the hydrogel was regulated by pre-stretching synergistic ion crosslinking to construct a carboxymethyl cellulose-based double network-oriented hydrogel similar to muscle." 作者通过预拉伸与离子交联协同调控内部结构，构建出仿肌肉取向的羧甲基纤维素基双网络导电水凝胶。  
> [原文] "The carboxymethyl cellulose-based double network oriented hydrogels were further assembled as flexible substrates into flexible sensor devices. The hydrogel sensors can monitor simple joint movements as well as complex spatial movements" 该材料进一步被组装为柔性传感器，能够监测简单关节运动和复杂空间运动。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文] "Here, the internal structure of the hydrogel was regulated by pre-stretching synergistic ion crosslinking to construct a carboxymethyl cellulose-based double network-oriented hydrogel similar to muscle."** 通过“预拉伸 + 离子交联”实现取向双网络设计，是本文最核心的材料结构创新。
- **[原文] "Its good mechanical properties and conductive properties can be converted into stable electrical signal output during deformation."** 作者将力学性能与导电性能直接对接到传感输出稳定性，说明该材料并非只提升强度，而是面向可用传感器性能设计。
- **[原文] "The hydrogel sensors can monitor simple joint movements as well as complex spatial movements, which makes them have potential application value in the research field of intelligent response electronic devices such as flexible wearables, intelligent strain sensing, and soft robots."** 论文把应用场景落到柔性可穿戴、智能应变感知和软体机器人，显示其工程转化导向。

## 方法

- **[原文] "the internal structure of the hydrogel was regulated by pre-stretching synergistic ion crosslinking"** 方法核心是用预拉伸与离子交联协同调控水凝胶内部结构，从而获得取向特征。
- **[原文] "construct a carboxymethyl cellulose-based double network-oriented hydrogel similar to muscle"** 材料体系为羧甲基纤维素基双网络导电水凝胶，目标结构是“类似肌肉”的各向异性取向网络。
- **[原文] "The carboxymethyl cellulose-based double network oriented hydrogels were further assembled as flexible substrates into flexible sensor devices."** 在器件层面，作者将该水凝胶直接作为柔性基底组装为传感器件。

## 关键发现

- **[原文] "The introduction of pre-stretching increased the tensile strength of the double-network hydrogel from 1.45鈥疢Pa to 4.32鈥疢Pa"** 预拉伸使双网络水凝胶拉伸强度由 **1.45 MPa** 提升到 **4.32 MPa**，说明取向构筑显著增强了力学性能。
- **[原文] "its light transmittance increased from 67.3鈥? to 84.5鈥?."** 透光率由 **67.3%** 提升到 **84.5%**，表明预拉伸还改善了材料的光学表现。
- **[原文] "the hydrogel's thermal stability and electrical conductivity were improved to a certain extent."** 除强度和透光率外，热稳定性与电导率也获得提升，但摘要没有给出定量幅度。
- **[原文] "The hydrogel sensors can monitor simple joint movements as well as complex spatial movements"** 该材料制成的传感器不仅适用于简单动作识别，也支持更复杂的空间运动监测。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有提供热稳定性、电导率、传感灵敏度、响应时间、循环稳定性等关键定量指标，因此目前更像是“可行性证明”，距离严格性能对标还差实验细节。
- **[推断]** 文章强调“similar to muscle”的取向结构，但摘要未说明各向异性在不同加载方向上的定量差异，后续需要补充方向依赖测试。
- **[推断]** 既然目标应用包括柔性穿戴与软体机器人，后续应重点验证长期疲劳、环境稳定性和真实场景封装兼容性。

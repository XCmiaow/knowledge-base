## 摘要

> [原文]「TEMPO-mediated oxidized cellulose nanofibers are deduced from enormously available wood biomass and functionalized with carboxyl groups.」文章指出，TOCNFs 来源于丰富的木质生物质，并通过羧基功能化获得关键材料特性。  
> [原文]「Due to the presence of functional carboxyl groups, TOCNF-based materials have been studied widely in different fields, including biomedicine, wastewater treatment, bioelectronics and others.」与[原文]「It is expected that this timely review will help further research on the invention of novel material from TOCNFs and its applications in different advanced fields, including biomedicine, bioelectronics, wastewater treatment, and the energy sector.」本文强调 TOCNFs 已广泛进入生物医学、废水处理、生物电子等领域，并被期待进一步推动先进材料与能源相关应用研究。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「In this review, the TEMPO oxidation mechanism, the properties and applications of TOCNFs are elaborated. Most importantly, the recent advanced applications and the beneficial role of TOCNFs in the various abovementioned fields are discussed. Furthermore, the performances and research progress on the fabrication of TOCNFs are summarized.」** — 核心贡献是把 TOCNFs 的氧化机理、性能、应用，以及制备性能与研究进展放在同一篇综述中统一梳理，并突出其“recent advanced applications”和跨领域作用。

## 方法

- **[原文]「In this review, the TEMPO oxidation mechanism, the properties and applications of TOCNFs are elaborated. Furthermore, the performances and research progress on the fabrication of TOCNFs are summarized.」** — 方法上采用综述归纳，围绕氧化机理、制备进展、性能与应用四条主线组织内容，而非报告单一实验体系。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The preparation procedure of TOCNFs is more environmentally friendly compared to other cellulose, for example, MFC and CNCs.」** → 摘要明确把“更环保的制备过程”作为 TOCNFs 相比 MFC 和 CNCs 的重要优势之一。
- **[原文]「Due to the presence of functional carboxyl groups, TOCNF-based materials have been studied widely in different fields, including biomedicine, wastewater treatment, bioelectronics and others.」** → 羧基官能团是 TOCNFs 广泛进入生物医学、废水处理和生物电子等方向的直接材料基础。
- **[原文]「Most importantly, the recent advanced applications and the beneficial role of TOCNFs in the various abovementioned fields are discussed.」** → 文章把“近期先进应用”与“beneficial role”视为综述重心，说明研究关注点已从材料制备延伸到功能落地。
- **[原文]「It is expected that this timely review will help further research on the invention of novel material from TOCNFs and its applications in different advanced fields, including biomedicine, bioelectronics, wastewater treatment, and the energy sector.」** → TOCNFs 的应用外延已覆盖能源领域；**[推断]** 摘要未提供具体数字、百分比或温度等量化性能数据。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要强调综述范围、应用版图与研究潜力，但没有给出具体实验条件、表征指标、性能数值或不同应用场景下的关键瓶颈，因此无法仅凭摘要判断 TOCNFs 的性能上限、工程化难点与最具竞争力的应用入口。

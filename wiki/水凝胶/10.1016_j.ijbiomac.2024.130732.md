## 摘要

> 该综述指出，纳米纤维素基组织黏合剂有望同时实现快速止血与有效伤口愈合，[原文]「Nanocellulose-based tissue adhesives show promise for achieving rapid hemostasis and effective wound healing.」  
> 文章将这一方向置于传统缝线与钉合器存在局限的背景下，强调以3D打印推动生物黏合剂在直接创面黏附、低组织损伤和组织工程中的应用，[原文]「Conventional methods, such as sutures and staples, have limitations, prompting the exploration of bioadhesives for direct wound adhesion and minimal tissue damage.」[原文]「This study explores the potential of nanocellulose-based adhesives for hemostasis and wound healing using 3D printing techniques.」[原文]「This study reviews recent trends in various nanocellulose-based 3D printed hydrogel patches for tissue engineering applications.」  
> 综述还覆盖纳米纤维素的类型、合成、表面改性及其经3D打印实现智能创伤修复的制备路径，[原文]「The review also introduces various types of nanocellulose and their synthesis, surface modification, and bioadhesive fabrication techniques via 3D printing for smart wound healing.」

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「This study reviews recent trends in various nanocellulose-based 3D printed hydrogel patches for tissue engineering applications.」** — 文章的核心贡献是系统梳理纳米纤维素基3D打印水凝胶贴片在组织工程中的最新进展。
- **[原文]「The review also introduces various types of nanocellulose and their synthesis, surface modification, and bioadhesive fabrication techniques via 3D printing for smart wound healing.」** — 文章不仅总结应用，还串联材料类型、合成、表面改性与3D打印制备技术，形成较完整的技术链条。
- **[原文]「Nanocellulose enables the creation of biodegradable adhesives with minimal adverse effects and opens avenues for advanced wound healing and complex tissue regeneration, such as skin, blood vessels, lungs, cartilage, and muscle.」** — 文章强调纳米纤维素材料价值在于可生物降解、低不良效应，并面向皮肤、血管、肺、软骨和肌肉等复杂组织再生场景。

## 方法

- **[原文]「This study explores the potential of nanocellulose-based adhesives for hemostasis and wound healing using 3D printing techniques.」** — 方法层面上，这是一篇围绕止血与创伤愈合应用场景、以3D打印为技术主线的综述。
- **[原文]「The review also introduces various types of nanocellulose and their synthesis, surface modification, and bioadhesive fabrication techniques via 3D printing for smart wound healing.」** — 具体综述框架包括纳米纤维素类型、合成方法、表面改性策略，以及通过3D打印构建生物黏合剂的制备技术。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「This study explores the potential of nanocellulose-based adhesives for hemostasis and wound healing using 3D printing techniques.」** → 关键应用主线明确落在“3D打印”上，说明文章关注的是3D打印赋能的止血与伤口愈合黏合体系。
- **[原文]「This study reviews recent trends in various nanocellulose-based 3D printed hydrogel patches for tissue engineering applications.」** → 文章指出当前研究对象已具体发展到多种“3D printed hydrogel patches”，反映该领域的制剂形态正在走向可打印贴片化。
- **[原文]「Nanocellulose enables the creation of biodegradable adhesives with minimal adverse effects and opens avenues for advanced wound healing and complex tissue regeneration, such as skin, blood vessels, lungs, cartilage, and muscle.」** → 文章将潜在应用拓展到5类复杂组织场景：皮肤、血管、肺、软骨和肌肉，说明其目标不止于表浅创面修复。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出不同纳米纤维素黏合体系之间的定量性能对比，也未报告具体止血时间、黏附强度或临床结果，说明本文更偏向领域综述与路线梳理，而不是建立统一性能基准。
- **[推断]** 文章强调3D打印、生物降解和复杂组织再生潜力，但从摘要看，后续关键问题仍会集中在材料改性与打印工艺如何共同提升真实生理环境下的黏附稳定性与转化可行性。

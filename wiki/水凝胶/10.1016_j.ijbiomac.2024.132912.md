# Novel insights on room temperature-induced cellulose dissolution mechanism via ZnCl2 aqueous solution: Migration, penetration, interaction, and dispersion.

## 摘要

> [原文]「The unique molecular structure of cellulose makes it challenging to dissolve at room temperature (R.T.), and the dissolution mechanism remains unclear.」文章首先界定问题：纤维素因其独特分子结构而在室温（R.T.）下难以溶解，且其溶解机理尚不清楚。  
> [原文]「In this study, we employed ZnCl2 aqueous solution for cellulose dissolution at R.T., proposing a novel four-stage dissolution mechanism.」作者以 ZnCl2 水溶液为室温溶剂体系，提出了一个新的四阶段溶解机制。  
> [原文]「Notably, the solvent recovery rate reached about 95 % based on previous usage during five cycles.」同时，该体系在五次循环后仍可实现约 95 % 的溶剂回收率，说明其绿色回用潜力被作为核心优势之一强调。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「The efficient dissolution of cellulose in ZnCl2 aqueous solution at R.T. involves four indispensable stages: rapid migration of hydrated Zn2+ ions towards cellulose, sufficient penetration between cellulose sheets, strong interaction with cellulose hydroxyl groups, and effective dispersion of separated cellulose chains.」** — 文章的核心贡献是把室温纤维素溶解拆解为四个不可或缺的连续阶段：水合 Zn2+ 向纤维素快速迁移、在纤维素片层间充分渗透、与羟基发生强相互作用，以及对分离后纤维素链进行有效分散。  
- **[原文]「This work contributes to a better understanding of the cellulose dissolution mechanisms within inorganic salt solvents at R.T., thereby guiding future development efforts towards greener and more efficient cellulosic solvents.」** — 作者将这一机制意义上升到无机盐溶剂体系的一般认识，认为其可为更绿色、更高效的纤维素溶剂开发提供指导。

## 方法

- **[原文]「In this study, we employed ZnCl2 aqueous solution for cellulose dissolution at R.T., proposing a novel four-stage dissolution mechanism.」** — 研究方法的起点是在室温（R.T.）条件下采用 ZnCl2 水溶液作为纤维素溶剂，并围绕该体系建立机制假说。  
- **[原文]「The proposed four-stage dissolution mechanism was validated through theoretical calculations and experimental evidence.」** — 机制验证采用“理论计算 + 实验证据”的双重路径，而非仅依赖现象描述。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The hydrated Zn2+ ions in ZnCl2 + 3.5H2O solvent exhibited ideal migration, penetration, interaction, and dispersion abilities, resulting in efficient cellulose dissolution at R.T.」** → 关键定量条件之一是 ZnCl2 + 3.5H2O 这一溶剂组成；作者认为该体系同时满足迁移、渗透、相互作用与分散四项能力，因此能够在室温（R.T.）下实现高效溶解。  
- **[原文]「Moreover, only slight degradation of cellulose occurred in ZnCl2 + 3.5H2O at R.T.」** → 在 ZnCl2 + 3.5H2O、室温（R.T.）条件下，纤维素仅发生轻微降解，说明该体系在提升溶解能力的同时尽量保留了分子完整性。  
- **[原文]「Consequently, the regenerated cellulose materials obtained from ZnCl2 + 3.5H2O (R.T.) exhibited better mechanical properties.」** → 由 ZnCl2 + 3.5H2O（R.T.）再生得到的纤维素材料表现出更好的力学性能，表明溶解过程改进最终转化为了材料性能收益。  
- **[原文]「Notably, the solvent recovery rate reached about 95 % based on previous usage during five cycles.」** → 文中给出的另一组关键数字是五次循环后约 95 % 的溶剂回收率，这直接支撑了其关于可回收性和绿色性的论点。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要虽然给出了四阶段机制、ZnCl2 + 3.5H2O、室温（R.T.）以及约 95 % 回收率和五次循环等关键信息，但没有交代纤维素来源、浓度范围、理论计算模型参数和“better mechanical properties”的具体指标，因此该机制对不同纤维素原料和不同加工场景的普适性仍需结合全文进一步判断。

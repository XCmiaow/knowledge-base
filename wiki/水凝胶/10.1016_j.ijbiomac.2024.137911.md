# Itaconic acid/cellulose-based hydrogels with fire-resistant and anti-freezing properties via vat photopolymerization 3D printing.

**中文标题**: 基于衣康酸/纤维素的阻燃抗冻水凝胶：通过槽式光聚合 3D 打印制备

## 摘要

> [原文]「This approach perfectly integrates renewable itaconic acid and cellulose derivatives with rapid vat photopolymerization (VP) 3D printing technology, which affords a green and efficient route for materials processing.」本文提出将可再生衣康酸、纤维素衍生物与快速 VP 3D 打印结合的绿色高效材料加工路线。  
> [原文]「the resultant 3D-printed biomass-based hydrogels exhibit commendable flame-retardant performance, as evidenced by a V-0 flammability rating and a Limiting Oxygen Index (LOI) value of 60.2 %. They also possess desirable mechanical attributes (95.2 kPa) and exceptional thermal stability, enduring high temperatures for up to 12 min.」所得 3D 打印生物质水凝胶同时表现出优良阻燃性、95.2 kPa 力学性能和可持续 12 min 的耐高温稳定性。  
> [原文]「Notably, these hydrogels exhibit remarkable freeze tolerance, maintaining their functionality even at profoundly low temperatures.」该材料还具备显著抗冻性，在极低温条件下仍能保持功能。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「we describe an innovative and sustainable methodology for the development and production of flame-retardant hydrogels.」** — 核心贡献是提出一种面向阻燃水凝胶设计与制备的创新且可持续的方法。
- **[原文]「This approach perfectly integrates renewable itaconic acid and cellulose derivatives with rapid vat photopolymerization (VP) 3D printing technology」** — 创新点在于把生物基原料体系与快速 VP 3D 打印工艺直接耦合，而不是仅停留在传统浇铸型水凝胶制备。
- **[原文]「This study demonstrates a novel strategy for the design and production of flame-retardant materials, contributing to the pursuit of green sustainability.」** — 论文把阻燃材料开发与绿色可持续制造目标结合起来，强调了方法论层面的普适价值。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Specifically, the biomass-based ink formulated for 3D printing demonstrates excellent visible-light curing properties」** — 方法上先构建适用于 3D 打印的生物质基墨水，再利用可见光固化实现成形。
- **[原文]「achieving a maximum double-bond conversion of 45.3 % within 10 min of exposure to visible-light LED under ambient conditions.」** — 在环境条件下通过可见光 LED 照射 10 min，实现最高 45.3 % 双键转化率，说明该体系具备较快的光固化响应。
- **[原文]「the resultant 3D-printed biomass-based hydrogels exhibit commendable flame-retardant performance」** — 制备完成后再从阻燃、力学、热稳定和抗冻等维度评价打印水凝胶的综合性能。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「achieving a maximum double-bond conversion of 45.3 % within 10 min of exposure to visible-light LED under ambient conditions.」** → 该打印墨水在常温、可见光 LED 条件下 10 min 内即可达到 45.3 % 的最高双键转化率，说明工艺具备快速固化潜力。
- **[原文]「as evidenced by a V-0 flammability rating and a Limiting Oxygen Index (LOI) value of 60.2 %.」** → 阻燃性能达到 V-0 等级，LOI 为 60.2 %，表明材料具有很强的抗燃烧能力。
- **[原文]「They also possess desirable mechanical attributes (95.2 kPa) and exceptional thermal stability, enduring high temperatures for up to 12 min.」** → 材料兼具 95.2 kPa 的力学性能，并可在高温下维持至多 12 min，体现了结构稳定性与热耐受性。
- **[原文]「these hydrogels exhibit remarkable freeze tolerance, maintaining their functionality even at profoundly low temperatures.」** → 除阻燃外，材料还表现出低温服役能力，说明其应用场景不局限于常温环境。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出衣康酸、纤维素衍生物和其他组分的具体配比，也未说明打印分辨率、层厚或后处理条件，当前信息不足以直接复现实验。
- **[推断]** 抗冻性能只给出“极低温下仍保持功能”的定性表述，缺少具体温度下限、循环次数和性能保持率，后续应补充定量低温耐久数据。
- **[推断]** 阻燃、力学和耐热结果较亮眼，但摘要未提供对照样、误差范围或统计重复信息，后续需要通过对比实验验证性能提升来源。

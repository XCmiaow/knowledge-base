# Synthesis of novel deep eutectic solvent-modified nano cellulosic gel formulation-based strain sensor for human motion monitoring.

**中文标题**: 新型深共熔溶剂改性纳米纤维素凝胶配方应变传感器的合成及其用于人体运动监测

## 摘要

> [原文]「Using deep eutectic solvents (DES) to pretreat wheat straw (WS) and extract lignin-containing nanocellulose (LCNC). Acrylic acid/choline chloride (AA/ChCl) polymerizable deep eutectic solvents (PDES) were used as the primary polymerization network, combined with polyvinyl alcohol (PVA).」本文以 DES 预处理麦秸（WS）并提取含木质素纳米纤维素（LCNC），再以 AA/ChCl 型可聚合深共熔溶剂（PDES）结合 PVA 搭建主体网络。  
>
> [原文]「Lignocellulose nanocrystals (LCNC) oxidized by sodium periodate were prepared as dialdehyde-based nanocellulose (DCNC) to serve as the crosslinking agent. Under UV light, a dual-network Polyacrylic acid-Choline chloride/Polyvinyl alcohol/dialdehyde-based nanocellulose ( (PAA-ChCl)/PVA/DCNC) ionic gel was polymerized and prepared, and make it into a sensor.」作者进一步将高碘酸钠氧化后的 LCNC 制成二醛纳米纤维素（DCNC）作为交联剂，并在紫外光下制备出双网络离子凝胶传感器。  
>
> [原文]「The gel had the advantages of porous structure, good mechanical properties, conductive properties, fast electrical recovery and self-healing. The results revealed the relative resistance of the sensor also changed with the change of the human joint, and also responded multiple times for the continuous same cyclic action of the joint, the sensor could also distinguished different fast and slow movements.」结果表明，该凝胶兼具多孔结构、力学性能、导电性、快速电恢复与自愈能力，且传感器能够实时响应关节运动、重复循环动作以及快慢不同的运动节律。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Using deep eutectic solvents (DES) to pretreat wheat straw (WS) and extract lignin-containing nanocellulose (LCNC).」** — 工作的起点是把农业废弃物麦秸转化为含木质素纳米纤维素来源，说明材料设计同时考虑了生物质利用与功能材料构建。
- **[原文]「Acrylic acid/choline chloride (AA/ChCl) polymerizable deep eutectic solvents (PDES) were used as the primary polymerization network, combined with polyvinyl alcohol (PVA).」** — 核心材料创新在于以 AA/ChCl 型 PDES 作为主聚合网络，并与 PVA 复合，构成离子凝胶的主体框架。
- **[原文]「Under UV light, a dual-network Polyacrylic acid-Choline chloride/Polyvinyl alcohol/dialdehyde-based nanocellulose ( (PAA-ChCl)/PVA/DCNC) ionic gel was polymerized and prepared, and make it into a sensor.」** — 论文把双网络凝胶直接落实为应变传感器，而不是停留在凝胶材料层面，体现出明确的器件导向。
- **[原文]「This showed the sensor could detect human movement in real time, making it promising for applications in medical devices, electronic skin and other fields」** — 文章把应用场景明确定位到实时人体运动监测，并延伸到医疗器件与电子皮肤。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Using deep eutectic solvents (DES) to pretreat wheat straw (WS) and extract lignin-containing nanocellulose (LCNC).」** — 前处理路线是先用 DES 处理 WS，再提取 LCNC，为后续凝胶构建提供生物基纳米纤维素原料。
- **[原文]「Lignocellulose nanocrystals (LCNC) oxidized by sodium periodate were prepared as dialdehyde-based nanocellulose (DCNC) to serve as the crosslinking agent.」** — 通过高碘酸钠氧化把 LCNC 转化为 DCNC，并将其作为交联剂引入网络，提高体系结构稳定性。
- **[原文]「Acrylic acid/choline chloride (AA/ChCl) polymerizable deep eutectic solvents (PDES) were used as the primary polymerization network, combined with polyvinyl alcohol (PVA).」** — 主网络由 AA/ChCl 型 PDES 与 PVA 协同组成，体现出离子导电网络与聚合物支撑网络的结合。
- **[原文]「Under UV light, a dual-network Polyacrylic acid-Choline chloride/Polyvinyl alcohol/dialdehyde-based nanocellulose ( (PAA-ChCl)/PVA/DCNC) ionic gel was polymerized and prepared, and make it into a sensor.」** — 制备方式是紫外光诱导聚合，最终形成双网络的 (PAA-ChCl)/PVA/DCNC 离子凝胶并加工为传感器。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The gel had the advantages of porous structure, good mechanical properties, conductive properties, fast electrical recovery and self-healing.」** → 该凝胶的优势不是单一性能，而是结构、力学、电学、恢复能力与自愈性的组合表现。
- **[原文]「The results revealed the relative resistance of the sensor also changed with the change of the human joint」** → 传感器输出的相对电阻能够随人体关节状态变化而变化，说明其具备基础的人体动作映射能力。
- **[原文]「and also responded multiple times for the continuous same cyclic action of the joint」** → 传感器对同一关节连续循环动作可以重复响应，表明其具备一定的循环监测可用性。
- **[原文]「the sensor could also distinguished different fast and slow movements」** → 该器件不仅能检测动作发生，还能区分快慢不同的运动模式。
- **[原文]「This showed the sensor could detect human movement in real time, making it promising for applications in medical devices, electronic skin and other fields」** → 论文将实时检测能力与医疗器件、电子皮肤等应用方向直接关联起来。
- **[推断] [原文]「The results revealed the relative resistance of the sensor also changed with the change of the human joint」** 摘要只报告了“relative resistance”随动作变化这一现象，没有给出灵敏度、响应时间、恢复时间或循环寿命等定量指标，因此当前证据更偏向可行性展示而非性能标杆比较。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断] [原文]「but in real time, the small movement ability of the human body's throat swallowing and bending of the fingers needs to be improved.」** 摘要已直接暴露出对喉部吞咽和手指弯曲这类微小动作的检测能力不足，后续应优先提升低形变场景下的灵敏度、信噪比与稳定性。
- **[推断] [原文]「The gel had the advantages of porous structure, good mechanical properties, conductive properties, fast electrical recovery and self-healing.」** 虽然摘要强调了多项优点，但缺少这些性能之间权衡关系的量化信息，后续需要明确自愈、导电和机械强度能否同时保持。
- **[推断] [原文]「making it promising for applications in medical devices, electronic skin and other fields」** 现阶段更适合将其视为面向医疗器件与电子皮肤的候选材料体系，距离实际应用仍需补充长期佩戴、环境稳定性和器件封装层面的验证。

## 摘要

> [原文]「cellulose derivatives of various kinds, such as cellulose esters and ethers, and nanocelluloses, have become popular bioprintable materials used for making bio-inks because of their affordability, biocompatibility, biodegradability, and printability.」本文指出，纤维素酯、纤维素醚和纳米纤维素之所以成为热门生物墨水候选，关键在于其兼具可负担性、生物相容性、生物可降解性和可打印性。  
> [原文]「This review emphasizes advancements in the design of cellulose-based bio-inks for 3D bioprinting of diverse tissues」；[原文]「Various cross-linking approaches are offered for multicomponent cellulose and nanocellulose-based bio-inks to control the fidelity of the ink and alter the mechanical stiffness in the printed hydrogel construct as a bioactive cue.」综述重点梳理了纤维素基生物墨水在多种组织3D生物打印中的设计进展，并把交联策略、打印保真度和水凝胶构建体力学刚度调控视为关键工程抓手。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「This review emphasizes advancements in the design of cellulose-based bio-inks for 3D bioprinting of diverse tissues as well as the physicochemical attributes of cellulose derivatives and nanocellulose that make them a viable choice for bio-inks in 3D bioprinting.」** — 文章的核心贡献是把“设计进展”和“材料理化属性”放到同一框架下讨论，说明纤维素衍生物与纳米纤维素为何适合作为3D生物打印生物墨水。
- **[原文]「Additionally, cellulose bio-inks' current benefits and drawbacks in 3D printing are thoroughly examined.」** — 文章不只总结优势，也系统纳入现阶段缺点，为后续材料筛选与工艺优化提供了较完整的问题清单。
- **[原文]「By emphasizing the interactions involving cells and the matrix, it additionally examines the effect of functional groups and surface charge on nanocellulose on vital cellular functions (including cell survival, adhesion, and proliferation).」** — 文章把讨论延伸到细胞-基质相互作用层面，强调纳米纤维素表面化学特征会影响细胞生存、黏附和增殖等关键生物学结果。

## 方法

- **[原文]「This review emphasizes advancements in the design of cellulose-based bio-inks for 3D bioprinting of diverse tissues」；[原文]「Additionally, cellulose bio-inks' current benefits and drawbacks in 3D printing are thoroughly examined.」** — 这是一篇综述性工作，方法上主要通过整合不同组织3D生物打印场景中的材料设计进展，并并列分析优势与局限来构建论证。
- **[原文]「Various cross-linking approaches are offered for multicomponent cellulose and nanocellulose-based bio-inks to control the fidelity of the ink and alter the mechanical stiffness in the printed hydrogel construct as a bioactive cue.」** — 方法维度上，文章把多组分纤维素/纳米纤维素生物墨水的交联策略作为分析主轴，用于连接打印成形质量与生物力学微环境设计。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「3D bioprinting of diverse tissues」** → 摘要中唯一明确出现的数字信息是“3D”，表明全文讨论对象明确限定在三维生物打印，而不是一般材料制备或二维培养。
- **[原文]「to control the fidelity of the ink and alter the mechanical stiffness in the printed hydrogel construct as a bioactive cue.」** → 文章把“打印保真度”和“力学刚度”视为可被交联策略协同调控的两个关键输出，其中力学刚度还被进一步定义为生物活性线索。
- **[原文]「the effect of functional groups and surface charge on nanocellulose on vital cellular functions (including cell survival, adhesion, and proliferation).」** → 文章强调纳米纤维素的官能团和表面电荷不是次要表征参数，而是会直接牵动细胞生存、黏附与增殖的重要设计变量。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出任何配方比例、流变参数、交联条件、力学数值或细胞实验指标，因此这篇文章更适合作为选题和设计维度的综述入口，而不能直接支撑具体配方复现。
- **[推断]** 结合「the potential uses of nanocellulose and cellulose derivative-based bio-inks have not been thoroughly explored.」可判断，该领域下一步更需要面向具体组织场景的定量比较研究，以明确不同纤维素体系在打印窗口、结构稳定性和细胞响应上的边界条件。

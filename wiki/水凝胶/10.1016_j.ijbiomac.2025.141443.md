## 摘要

> [原文]「Rigid cellulose fiber (CF) was employed in this study as a modifier to control the rheology, structure, and 3D printing characteristics of corn starch (CS) hydrogels.」本文以刚性纤维素纤维作为改性剂，目标是调控玉米淀粉水凝胶的流变、结构与3D打印特性。  
> [原文]「When CF was present, the CS hydrogel's continuous network structure was destroyed, and its viscosity was greatly decreased.」[原文]「Rheological data revealed that the addition of CF increased the mechanical properties of the CS-CF hydrogel and gave it exceptional self-supporting qualities.」结果显示，CF一方面破坏连续网络并显著降低黏度，另一方面又提升了复合水凝胶的力学性能与自支撑能力。  
> [原文]「CF2 had satisfactory 3D printing qualities, allowing it to completely recreate the preset model with a printing accuracy of 98.1 %.」在打印端，CF2能够较完整复现预设模型，打印精度达到98.1 %。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Rigid cellulose fiber (CF) was employed in this study as a modifier to control the rheology, structure, and 3D printing characteristics of corn starch (CS) hydrogels.」** — 核心贡献在于把刚性纤维素纤维引入玉米淀粉水凝胶体系，并将研究目标直接对准结构、流变与3D打印三个关键维度。
- **[原文]「CS-CF hydrogel outperformed pure CS hydrogel in terms of 3D printing properties.」** — 文章不仅比较材料性质，还明确证明了CS-CF体系在3D打印性能上优于纯CS水凝胶。
- **[原文]「As a result, this study can serve as a reference for enhancing the rheology and 3D printing performance of starch-based hydrogels.」** — 作者将工作定位为淀粉基水凝胶流变调控与打印性能优化的参考方案。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Rigid cellulose fiber (CF) was employed in this study as a modifier to control the rheology, structure, and 3D printing characteristics of corn starch (CS) hydrogels.」** — 研究方法的主线是以CF为单一改性变量，考察其对CS水凝胶体系多维性能的影响。
- **[原文]「Results from low-field nuclear magnetic resonance (LF-NMR) indicated that the hydrogel system considerably shortened the water's relaxation period.」** — 使用LF-NMR观察水分弛豫行为，从而侧面表征水凝胶体系内部水分状态变化。
- **[原文]「Rheological data revealed that the addition of CF increased the mechanical properties of the CS-CF hydrogel and gave it exceptional self-supporting qualities.」** — 通过流变数据评估复合水凝胶的力学性质与自支撑能力。
- **[原文]「The appearance and precision of the printed object were measured」** — 通过打印对象的外观与精度来评价3D打印表现。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The findings demonstrated that CS and CF had a robust hydrogen connection, which facilitated CF's dispersion inside the CS matrix.」** → CS与CF之间存在较强氢键作用，这有助于CF在CS基体内部均匀分散。
- **[原文]「When CF was present, the CS hydrogel's continuous network structure was destroyed, and its viscosity was greatly decreased.」** → CF的加入会破坏CS水凝胶原有连续网络，并显著降低体系黏度。
- **[原文]「Results from low-field nuclear magnetic resonance (LF-NMR) indicated that the hydrogel system considerably shortened the water's relaxation period.」** → LF-NMR结果表明，该水凝胶体系会明显缩短水的弛豫时间。
- **[原文]「Rheological data revealed that the addition of CF increased the mechanical properties of the CS-CF hydrogel and gave it exceptional self-supporting qualities.」** → 尽管黏度下降，CF仍提升了CS-CF水凝胶的力学性能，并赋予其优异的自支撑特性。
- **[原文]「CS-CF hydrogel outperformed pure CS hydrogel in terms of 3D printing properties.」** → 复合体系在3D打印性能上整体优于纯CS体系。
- **[原文]「CF2 had satisfactory 3D printing qualities, allowing it to completely recreate the preset model with a printing accuracy of 98.1 %.」** → CF2是摘要中表现最突出的配方，能够完整复现预设模型，且打印精度达到98.1 %。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要只给出了CF2的98.1 %打印精度，未报告其他CF水平的具体数值、误差范围、样本量或统计显著性，因此最优配方结论仍需正文数据支撑。
- **[推断]** 摘要覆盖了氢键作用、LF-NMR、流变与打印外观/精度，但没有说明打印后结构稳定性、长期储存稳定性或实际应用场景下的耐受性，应用外推仍有限。

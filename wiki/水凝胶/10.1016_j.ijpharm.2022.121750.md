# Drug-loaded mesoporous silica on carboxymethyl cellulose hydrogel: development of innovative 3D printed hydrophilic films.

**中文标题**: 载药介孔二氧化硅-羧甲基纤维素水凝胶：创新型 3D 打印亲水薄膜的开发

## 摘要

> [原文]「3D printing has been explored as an emerging technology for the development of versatile and printable materials for drug delivery.」本文将 3D 打印定位为药物递送材料开发中的新兴技术，并以此作为体系设计起点。  
> [原文]「Herein, a mesoporous silica with nanostructured pores, SBA-15, was used as a drug carrier for triamcinolone acetonide, a hydrophobic drug, with the aim of incorporating the drug formulation in a hydrophilic printable ink.」作者用具有纳米结构孔道的 SBA-15 负载疏水性曲安奈德醋酸酯，目标是把疏水药物装入亲水、可打印墨水中。  
> [原文]「The 3D printed films showed complete drug release after 12 h, and the presence of the triamcinolone acetonide-loaded SBA-15 improved their in vitro mucoadhesion」结果上，3D 打印薄膜可在 12 h 后实现完全释药，同时载药 SBA-15 提升了体外黏膜黏附性。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Besides representing an innovative platform to develop water-based mucoadhesive formulations containing a hydrophobic drug, this is the first report proposing the development of SSE 3D printed nanomedicines containing drug-loaded mesoporous silica.」** — 核心贡献有两点：一是提出了“水基黏膜黏附制剂 + 疏水药物”的新平台，二是将载药介孔二氧化硅引入 SSE 3D 打印纳米药物，且作者明确声称这是首个相关报道。

## 方法

- **[原文]「a mesoporous silica with nanostructured pores, SBA-15, was used as a drug carrier for triamcinolone acetonide」** — 先用 SBA-15 作为介孔载体吸附曲安奈德醋酸酯，解决疏水药物直接进入亲水体系的困难。  
- **[原文]「The adsorption of the drug in the SBA-15 pores was confirmed by the decrease in its surface area and pore volume, along with an increase in the apparent aqueous solubility of triamcinolone acetonide, as shown by in vitro release studies.」** — 通过表面积和孔体积下降，以及表观水溶性提升来证明药物进入 SBA-15 孔道。  
- **[原文]「a hydrophilic ink composed of carboxymethyl cellulose containing drug-loaded SBA-15 was formulated and 3D printed as hydrophilic polymeric film using the semisolid extrusion technique (SSE).」** — 随后将载药 SBA-15 分散进羧甲基纤维素亲水墨水，并采用半固体挤出技术（SSE）打印成亲水聚合物薄膜。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The 3D printed films showed complete drug release after 12 h」** → 定量结果是该体系在 12 h 后达到完全释药，这是摘要里最明确的释放性能数据。  
- **[原文]「the presence of the triamcinolone acetonide-loaded SBA-15 improved their in vitro mucoadhesion」** → 载药 SBA-15 不只是运载疏水药物，还同步增强了薄膜的体外黏膜黏附性。  
- **[原文]「the alliance of 3D printing and nanomaterials has, to date, been little explored in pharmaceutics」** → 研究命中的是药剂学中“3D 打印 + 纳米材料”这一仍少被开发的交叉方向，因此方法学新意强。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要仅给出体外释放与体外黏附结果，缺少体内疗效、安全性、稳定性和长期贮存数据，因此距离实际口腔黏膜治疗应用仍有验证缺口。  
- **[推断]** 摘要没有提供载药量、包封效率、打印精度、力学性能和 mucoadhesion 的具体数值，说明当前更像概念验证而非完整工艺优化。  
- **[推断]** 作者强调这是 “first report”，这通常意味着可比基线有限；下一步应系统比较不同介孔材料、不同聚合物墨水与不同药物的普适性。

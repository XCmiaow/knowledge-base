# Frost-resistant nanocellulose-based organohydrogel with high mechanical strength and transparency.

**中文标题**: 高机械强度与高透明性的抗冻纳米纤维素基有机水凝胶

## 摘要

> [原文]「using bacterial nanocellulose (BC) as a reinforcing agent and polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) as a polymer matrix」与[原文]「constructed via the freezing-thawing method in a new binary solvent system of N, N-dimethylformamide and water (DMF-H2O)」说明本文以 BC/PVA 体系和 DMF-H2O 双溶剂冻融法构建抗冻有机水凝胶。
>
> [原文]「Owing to the solvent-induced crystallization effect」与[原文]「enhanced 3D hydrogen bonding network」指出其性能来源于溶剂诱导结晶和更强的 3D 氢键网络，使材料在[原文]「at room temperature」下达到[原文]「the tensile strength of 2,974 kPa and the stretchability of 277 %」，并且[原文]「demonstrated high transmittance」。
>
> [原文]「retaining the tensile strength of 508 kPa and a stretchability of 190 % even at -70 °C」表明该材料在极低温下仍保持力学可用性，而[原文]「has potential uses in harsh settings」则点明了其应用指向。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「using bacterial nanocellulose (BC) as a reinforcing agent and polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) as a polymer matrix, a frost-resistant organohydrogel was constructed via the freezing-thawing method in a new binary solvent system of N, N-dimethylformamide and water (DMF-H2O)」** — 核心贡献是把 BC 增强、PVA 基体、DMF-H2O 双溶剂与冻融工艺整合为同一抗冻有机水凝胶方案。
- **[原文]「This kind of transparent, frost-resistant organohydrogel has potential uses in harsh settings due to its great mechanical strength.」** — 文章把高透明、抗冻与高强度统一到面向严苛环境应用的材料目标中。

## 方法

- **[原文]「constructed via the freezing-thawing method in a new binary solvent system of N, N-dimethylformamide and water (DMF-H2O), which was designed according to the Hansen Solubility Parameter.」** — 制备方法是基于 Hansen 溶解度参数设计 DMF-H2O 双溶剂，再通过冻融法成胶。
- **[原文]「Owing to the solvent-induced crystallization effect that led to the enhanced 3D hydrogen bonding network during the freezing-thawing process」** — 作者给出的机制解释是溶剂诱导结晶促进了冻融过程中的 3D 氢键网络增强。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「the optimal organohydrogel achieved excellent mechanical properties with the tensile strength of 2,974 kPa and the stretchability of 277 % at room temperature」** → 室温下同时实现 2,974 kPa 拉伸强度和 277 % 伸长率。
- **[原文]「In the visiblelight range, the organohydrogel demonstrated high transmittance.」** → 可见光范围内具备高透过性。
- **[原文]「the presence of a DMF-H2O binary solvent endows it with frost-resistance, retaining the tensile strength of 508 kPa and a stretchability of 190 % even at -70 °C」** → DMF-H2O 双溶剂赋予材料抗冻性，使其在 -70 °C 仍保有 508 kPa 拉伸强度和 190 % 伸长率。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出 “high transmittance” 的具体透过率数值，也没有交代 BC/PVA 配比、冻融循环次数或长期稳定性，因此目前难以仅凭摘要复现实验并全面评估耐久性。

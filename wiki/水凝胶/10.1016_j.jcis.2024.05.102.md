## 摘要

> [原文]「Herein, we propose an all-green strategy using AlCl3/ZnCl2/H2O solvent to create cellulose/polyacrylamide-based hydrogel (AZ-Cel/PAM) with expanded hierarchical topologies.」文章提出一种以 `AlCl3/ZnCl2/H2O` 为核心溶剂体系的全绿色制备路线，用于构建具有扩展层级拓扑的 `AZ-Cel/PAM` 水凝胶电解质。
>
> [原文]「The aqueous AlCl3/ZnCl2 facilitates the efficient dissolution of cellulose at room temperature, and the dispersed Al3+-Zn2+ ions autocatalytic system catalyzes in-situ polymerization of acrylamide (AM) monomer.」其关键过程是在室温下高效溶解纤维素，并利用分散的 `Al3+-Zn2+` 自催化体系原位聚合 `AM` 单体。
>
> [原文]「The resulting hybrid hydrogel exhibits improved mechanical properties (tensile strength of 56.54 kPa and compressive strength of 359.43 kPa) and enhanced ionic conductivity (1.99 S/m).」与[原文]「The assembled supercapacitor with the hydrogel electrolyte, demonstrates an ideal area-specific capacitance of 203.80 mF/cm2.」结果表明该体系同时提升了材料本体性能与超级电容器器件性能。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Herein, we propose an all-green strategy using AlCl3/ZnCl2/H2O solvent to create cellulose/polyacrylamide-based hydrogel (AZ-Cel/PAM) with expanded hierarchical topologies.」** — 贡献在于把绿色溶剂体系、纤维素基骨架与聚丙烯酰胺网络扩展整合成一条自生长制备策略。
- **[原文]「This expands the AM network within the cellulose framework, forming multiple bonding interactions and stable ion channels.」** — 论文的材料设计核心是让 `AM` 网络在纤维素框架内扩展，从而同时形成多重键合作用与稳定离子通道。
- **[原文]「This all-green strategy presents a novel approach to developing sustainable energy storage devices.」** — 工作将该材料路线明确指向可持续储能器件开发，而不只是停留在单纯的凝胶制备层面。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Herein, we propose an all-green strategy using AlCl3/ZnCl2/H2O solvent to create cellulose/polyacrylamide-based hydrogel (AZ-Cel/PAM) with expanded hierarchical topologies.」** — 以 `AlCl3/ZnCl2/H2O` 作为绿色溶剂体系，直接构建 `AZ-Cel/PAM` 水凝胶并引入扩展层级拓扑。
- **[原文]「The aqueous AlCl3/ZnCl2 facilitates the efficient dissolution of cellulose at room temperature, and the dispersed Al3+-Zn2+ ions autocatalytic system catalyzes in-situ polymerization of acrylamide (AM) monomer.」** — 方法学关键是“室温溶解纤维素 + `Al3+-Zn2+` 自催化原位聚合 `AM`”，避免额外高温或复杂引发体系。
- **[原文]「Quantum simulations further clarify the mechanical composition and ion transport mechanism of AZ-Cel/PAM hydrogels.」** — 结合量子模拟解释凝胶的力学组成与离子传输机制，用于支撑实验观察。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The resulting hybrid hydrogel exhibits improved mechanical properties (tensile strength of 56.54 kPa and compressive strength of 359.43 kPa) and enhanced ionic conductivity (1.99 S/m).」** → 该凝胶同时实现了 `56.54 kPa` 拉伸强度、`359.43 kPa` 压缩强度和 `1.99 S/m` 离子电导率，说明力学性能与导电性能并未相互牺牲。
- **[原文]「Furthermore, it also demonstrates excellent adhesion, freeze resistance (-45 °C), and water retention capabilities.」** → 材料除导电与承载外，还具备黏附性、`-45 °C` 抗冻性与保水能力，拓宽了低温和实际接触场景下的使用可能。
- **[原文]「The assembled supercapacitor with the hydrogel electrolyte, demonstrates an ideal area-specific capacitance of 203.80 mF/cm2.」** → 采用该水凝胶电解质组装的超级电容器面积比电容达到 `203.80 mF/cm2`，证明材料性能已能转化为器件级输出。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要已经给出力学强度、电导率、抗冻温度和器件面积比电容，但未展示循环寿命、倍率性能、长期含水稳定性或与其他电解质体系的系统对照，因此其长期电化学稳定性与普适性仍需依赖正文和补充数据进一步判断。

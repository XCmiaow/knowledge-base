# Polymerizable deep eutectic solvent-gels synthesized in situ under molecular engineering control exhibit excellent adhesion, freeze resistance, as well as stretching and humidity sensing capabilities.

**中文标题**: 分子工程调控下原位合成的可聚合深共熔溶剂凝胶兼具优异粘附性、抗冻性、拉伸性与湿度传感能力

## 摘要

> [原文]「Hydrogels generally do not adhere well to different substrates and freeze at sub-zero temperatures, limiting their application.」论文针对传统水凝胶在基底粘附性差且低温易冻结这两个核心瓶颈展开。  
> [原文]「the strategy of replacing water in hydrogels with deep eutectic solvents (DES) was used to address these challenges.」作者提出用深共熔溶剂替代水，并通过 [原文]「choline chloride (ChCl) as hydrogen bond acceptor, acrylic acid (AA) and itaconic acid (IA) as hydrogen bond donors and polymerizable monomers constitute PDES」与 [原文]「PDES-gel (PG) was obtained by adding a thermal initiator to polymerize AA and IA in PDES」构建出 PG。  
> [原文]「without any phase change at -60 °C to 20 °C」以及 [原文]「the breaking elongation of the novelty PG was up to ca. 960 %, tensile strength ca. 1 MPa, outstanding transparency with an average light transmittance of about 95 % in the visible-light range」表明该材料同时具备抗冻、力学和透光性能，并且 [原文]「the novel PG exhibited certain capabilities for moisture detection and deformation sensing.」

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「the strategy of replacing water in hydrogels with deep eutectic solvents (DES) was used to address these challenges.」** — 用 DES 替代水这一设计直接回应了水凝胶“粘不牢”和“低温冻结”的双重应用障碍。
- **[原文]「The new PDES-gel material developed using PDES is expected to provide new ideas for the advancement of wearable devices.」** — 论文将该材料路线明确指向可穿戴器件场景，强调其方法学意义不仅是材料改性，也包括应用方向拓展。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Specifically, choline chloride (ChCl) as hydrogen bond acceptor, acrylic acid (AA) and itaconic acid (IA) as hydrogen bond donors and polymerizable monomers constitute PDES. Afterwards, PDES-gel (PG) was obtained by adding a thermal initiator to polymerize AA and IA in PDES.」** — 方法核心是先以 ChCl/AA/IA 组装可聚合深共熔体系 PDES，再通过热引发聚合在体系内原位形成 PG，实现分子设计与成胶过程的一体化。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「PG is almost water-free, it has remarkable low-temperature tolerance without any phase change at -60 °C to 20 °C.」** → 近乎无水的组成使 PG 在 `-60 °C to 20 °C` 范围内无相变，抗冻性能是该体系最突出的基础优势。
- **[原文]「Thanks to the carboxyl groups and chloride ions contained in PG, it can form non-covalent interactions such as hydrogen bonding and ionic interactions with different substrates, so PG can adhere to various substrate surfaces.」** → PG 的粘附并非依赖单一化学键，而是由羧基与氯离子介导的氢键和离子相互作用共同实现，因此具有多基底适配潜力。
- **[原文]「the breaking elongation of the novelty PG was up to ca. 960 %, tensile strength ca. 1 MPa, outstanding transparency with an average light transmittance of about 95 % in the visible-light range.」** → PG 同时给出 `ca. 960 %` 断裂伸长率、`ca. 1 MPa` 拉伸强度和可见光范围 `about 95 %` 平均透过率，说明其在柔性器件中兼具延展性、承载能力与光学可读性。
- **[原文]「Ultimately, the novel PG exhibited certain capabilities for moisture detection and deformation sensing.」** → 该材料已展示出湿度检测与形变传感能力，证明其不仅是结构材料，也具备功能响应属性。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要虽然给出了 `-60 °C to 20 °C`、`ca. 960 %`、`ca. 1 MPa` 和 `about 95 %` 等关键性能，但没有提供循环稳定性、粘附耐久性、湿度/应变传感的定量指标、响应速度、选择性或生物相容性信息，因此目前更像材料可行性验证，距离完整可穿戴器件评估还有明显空缺。

## 摘要

> [原文]「Hydrogels of cellulose nanofibrils (CNFs) are promising wound dressing candidates due to their biocompatibility, high water absorption, and transparency.」文章首先明确将纤维素纳米原纤维水凝胶定位为有前景的伤口敷料候选，核心原因是其生物相容性、高吸水性与透明性。  
> [原文]「two different commercially available wood species, softwood and hardwood, were subjected to TEMPO-mediated oxidation to proceed with delignification and oxidation in a one-pot process, and thereafter, nanofibrils were isolated using a high-pressure microfluidizer. Furthermore, transparent nanofibril hydrogel networks were prepared by vacuum filtration.」研究使用 2 种商业木材来源，即软木与硬木，经 TEMPO 介导氧化、一锅法脱木素/氧化、高压微流控解离和真空过滤，构建了透明纳米原纤维水凝胶网络。  
> [原文]「Softwood nanofibril hydrogel networks exhibited the best mechanical properties, and in vitro toxicological risk assessment showed no detrimental effect for any of the studied hydrogels on human fibroblast or keratinocyte cells.」结果表明软木体系的力学性能最佳，且所有研究水凝胶在体外对人成纤维细胞和角质形成细胞均未显示不利影响。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「This study demonstrates a straightforward processing route for direct oxidation of different wood species to obtain nanofibril hydrogels for potential use as wound dressings, with softwood having the most potential.」** — 贡献在于提出了面向伤口敷料应用的直接木材氧化制胶路线，并在不同木材来源中明确指出软木体系潜力最高。
- **[原文]「Nanofibril properties and network performance correlated with oxidation were investigated and compared with commercially available TEMPO-oxidized pulp nanofibrils and their networks.」** — 贡献还包括把自制木材来源纳米原纤维及其网络与商业 TEMPO 氧化纸浆纳米原纤维体系做对照，使结果具备更清晰的参照基线。

## 方法

- **[原文]「two different commercially available wood species, softwood and hardwood, were subjected to TEMPO-mediated oxidation to proceed with delignification and oxidation in a one-pot process, and thereafter, nanofibrils were isolated using a high-pressure microfluidizer. Furthermore, transparent nanofibril hydrogel networks were prepared by vacuum filtration.」** — 方法流程是以 2 种商业木材为原料，先做 TEMPO 介导的一锅法脱木素/氧化，再用高压微流控设备解离纳米原纤维，最后通过真空过滤形成透明水凝胶网络。
- **[原文]「Nanofibril properties and network performance correlated with oxidation were investigated and compared with commercially available TEMPO-oxidized pulp nanofibrils and their networks.」** — 表征重点放在纳米原纤维性质、与氧化相关的网络性能，以及与商业 TEMPO 氧化纸浆纳米原纤维网络的比较。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「two different commercially available wood species, softwood and hardwood」** → 研究对象明确包含 2 类商业木材来源，即软木和硬木。
- **[原文]「Softwood nanofibril hydrogel networks exhibited the best mechanical properties」** → 在被比较的体系中，软木纳米原纤维水凝胶网络的力学性能最好。
- **[原文]「in vitro toxicological risk assessment showed no detrimental effect for any of the studied hydrogels on human fibroblast or keratinocyte cells」** → 体外毒理风险评估显示，所有被研究水凝胶对人成纤维细胞和角质形成细胞都未见不利影响。
- **[原文]「with softwood having the most potential」** → 若以伤口敷料为目标应用，作者最终判断软木路线最具潜力。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出具体氧化条件、力学测试数值、吸水指标、透明度定量结果或体内伤口愈合数据，因此目前只能确认工艺可行性、相对力学优势与体外初步生物安全性，尚不足以直接外推到临床或真实创面环境。
- **[推断]** 摘要虽提到与商业 TEMPO 氧化纸浆纳米原纤维及其网络进行了比较，但未说明比较结果的幅度与统计显著性，后续需要回到正文核对性能差异的具体量级。

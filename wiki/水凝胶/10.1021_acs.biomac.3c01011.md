# Ionic Conductive Cellulose-Based Hydrogels with Superior Long-Lasting Moisture and Antifreezing Features for Flexible Strain Sensor Applications.

**中文标题**: 具有优异长效保湿与抗冻特性的离子导电纤维素基水凝胶及其柔性应变传感应用

## 摘要

> 文章针对导电水凝胶在极端条件下实际应用受限的问题，构建了一类由羟乙基纤维素、羟乙基丙烯酸酯、氯化锂和乙二醇/水二元溶剂组成的 HPE-LiCl 水凝胶。[原文]「the utilization of conductive hydrogels in practical applications under extreme conditions remains a significant challenge」 [原文]「a series of inorganic salt-ion-enhanced conductive hydrogels (HPE-LiCl) consisting of hydroxyethyl cellulose, hydroxyethyl acrylate, lithium chloride, and ethylene glycol/water binary solvent were fabricated via a facile one-pot method」
>
> 该体系同时强调自黏附、高拉伸性、抗疲劳、长效保湿和抗冻能力，并可在 -40 °C 到 80 °C 的挑战条件下长期保持柔性与导电性，支撑传感器在恶劣环境及长期储存后仍监测人体运动信号。[原文]「Apart from outstanding self-adhesion, high stretchability, and remarkable fatigue resistance, the HPE-LiCl hydrogels possessed especially excellent antifreezing and long-lasting moisture performances」 [原文]「could maintain satisfactory flexibility and electric conductivity over extended periods of time, even in challenging conditions such as extremely low temperatures (as low as -40 °C) and high temperatures (as high as 80 °C)」 [原文]「the HPE-LiCl-based sensor could timely and accurately monitor various human motion signals even in adverse environments and after long-term storage」

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Hence, this work presents a facile strategy for the design of long-term reliable hydrogels as smart strain sensors, especially used in extreme environments.」** — 核心贡献是把“长期可靠”和“极端环境可用”同时纳入智能应变传感水凝胶设计目标，并给出一条工艺上较简单的纤维素基实现路线。

## 方法

- **[原文]「a series of inorganic salt-ion-enhanced conductive hydrogels (HPE-LiCl) consisting of hydroxyethyl cellulose, hydroxyethyl acrylate, lithium chloride, and ethylene glycol/water binary solvent were fabricated via a facile one-pot method.」** — 采用一锅法制备无机盐离子增强导电水凝胶，材料组成为羟乙基纤维素、羟乙基丙烯酸酯、氯化锂以及乙二醇/水二元溶剂。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「Apart from outstanding self-adhesion, high stretchability, and remarkable fatigue resistance, the HPE-LiCl hydrogels possessed especially excellent antifreezing and long-lasting moisture performances」** → 该体系不仅追求导电，还同时兼顾黏附、形变与耐久相关性能，说明其设计目标面向可穿戴传感的综合服役需求。
- **[原文]「could maintain satisfactory flexibility and electric conductivity over extended periods of time, even in challenging conditions such as extremely low temperatures (as low as -40 °C) and high temperatures (as high as 80 °C)」** → 关键定量边界是最低 -40 °C、最高 80 °C，且强调“over extended periods of time”，表明材料在宽温域与长期使用条件下仍保持柔性和导电性。
- **[原文]「the HPE-LiCl-based sensor could timely and accurately monitor various human motion signals even in adverse environments and after long-term storage.」** → 材料优势被进一步转化为器件层面的可用性，说明其在恶劣环境和长期储存后仍具备人体运动监测能力。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出导电率、应变范围、灵敏度、响应时间、循环次数、含水保持时长等关键定量指标，也未呈现与其他抗冻导电水凝胶的直接对比，因此目前只能确认其方向性优势，尚难判断其性能上限与普适性。

# Single-Component Cellulose Acetate Sulfate Hydrogels for Direct Ink Writing 3D Printing.

**中文标题**: 用于直写式3D打印的单组分纤维素乙酸硫酸酯水凝胶

## 摘要

> [原文]「Hydrogels, typically favored for 3D printing due to their viscoelasticity, are now trending toward ecofriendly alternatives amid growing environmental concerns.」本文将研究背景定位在3D打印水凝胶的环保替代需求上，强调材料选择不仅要满足流变可打印性，也要回应可持续性问题。  
> [原文]「In this study, we crafted cellulose-based hydrogels, specifically employing cellulose acetate sulfate (CAS).」文章以纤维素乙酸硫酸酯（CAS）构建单组分纤维素基水凝胶，核心目标是把同一材料体系同时做成可配墨、可挤出、可成形的直写打印墨水。  
> [原文]「By keeping the acetyl group substitution degree (DSacetyl = 1.8) and CAS molecular weight constant, we varied rheological properties by adjusting sulfate group substitution (DSsulfate = 0.4, 0.7, and 1.0) and CAS concentration (2-5 wt %).」文章的主线是固定 DSacetyl = 1.8 与分子量，仅通过调控 DSsulfate = 0.4、0.7、1.0 以及 CAS 浓度 2-5 wt % 来筛选最优流变窗口，并进一步评估 Ca2+ 离子交联对打印结构稳定性、收缩和溶胀行为的影响。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「In this study, we crafted cellulose-based hydrogels, specifically employing cellulose acetate sulfate (CAS).」** — 贡献在于提出以 CAS 作为单一主体构建纤维素基打印水凝胶，避免依赖多组分复配来获得可打印性。
- **[原文]「Rheological characterizations, including shear-thinning, yield stress, and thixotropy, were performed to identify optimal conditions for formulating CAS hydrogel ink in direct ink writing for 3D printing under selected experimental conditions.」** — 文章把“剪切变稀、屈服应力、触变性”直接作为筛选打印墨水配方的判据，建立了从流变学到直写打印适配性的评价路径。
- **[原文]「Additionally, the effect of ionic cross-linking using Ca2+ ions on the structural integrity of 3D-printed structures was evaluated, demonstrating effective preservation through reinforced polymer networks.」** — 文章进一步说明 Ca2+ 离子交联不仅是后处理步骤，还能显著提升3D打印结构的完整性保持能力。

## 方法

- **[原文]「By keeping the acetyl group substitution degree (DSacetyl = 1.8) and CAS molecular weight constant, we varied rheological properties by adjusting sulfate group substitution (DSsulfate = 0.4, 0.7, and 1.0) and CAS concentration (2-5 wt %).」** — 方法设计上固定乙酰基取代度和分子量，单独考察硫酸基取代度与浓度两个变量对流变行为的影响，用于缩小打印配方搜索空间。
- **[原文]「Rheological characterizations, including shear-thinning, yield stress, and thixotropy, were performed to identify optimal conditions for formulating CAS hydrogel ink in direct ink writing for 3D printing under selected experimental conditions.」** — 实验路径是先做流变表征，再据此确定适合直写打印的油墨窗口，而不是先打印再反推配方。
- **[原文]「Based on rheological findings, CAS hydrogels with DSsulfate 0.7 and concentration of 4 wt % was used for 3D printing, with subsequent evaluation of printing metrics.」** — 最终依据流变结果选定 DSsulfate 0.7、浓度 4 wt % 的 CAS 水凝胶进入实际打印与打印指标评估。
- **[原文]「Additionally, the effect of ionic cross-linking using Ca2+ ions on the structural integrity of 3D-printed structures was evaluated, demonstrating effective preservation through reinforced polymer networks.」** — 在打印后再引入 Ca2+ 离子交联，检验其对结构保持的增强作用。
- **[原文]「The shrinking and swelling behaviors of the 3D-printed structures were also significantly affected by this ionic cross-linking.」** — 同时把收缩与溶胀作为交联后结构响应的重要观察终点。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「By keeping the acetyl group substitution degree (DSacetyl = 1.8) and CAS molecular weight constant, we varied rheological properties by adjusting sulfate group substitution (DSsulfate = 0.4, 0.7, and 1.0) and CAS concentration (2-5 wt %).」** → 在 DSacetyl = 1.8 和恒定分子量前提下，DSsulfate 从 0.4、0.7 到 1.0，以及浓度从 2-5 wt % 的变化，足以系统性调控 CAS 水凝胶的流变性质。
- **[原文]「Based on rheological findings, CAS hydrogels with DSsulfate 0.7 and concentration of 4 wt % was used for 3D printing, with subsequent evaluation of printing metrics.」** → 在被测试的窗口中，DSsulfate 0.7、4 wt % 被选为实际3D打印配方，说明其在可挤出性与成形保持之间达到了更优平衡。
- **[原文]「Additionally, the effect of ionic cross-linking using Ca2+ ions on the structural integrity of 3D-printed structures was evaluated, demonstrating effective preservation through reinforced polymer networks.」** → Ca2+ 交联能够通过增强聚合物网络来有效保持打印结构完整性，表明后交联是该体系稳定成形的关键补强机制。
- **[原文]「The shrinking and swelling behaviors of the 3D-printed structures were also significantly affected by this ionic cross-linking.」** → 离子交联不仅影响静态强度，还会显著改变打印结构的收缩与溶胀行为，说明其对尺寸稳定性和服役响应具有双重影响。
- **[原文]「Building on these findings, this work could broaden the range of cellulose derivatives available for the preparation of cellulose-based hydrogels for 3D printing.」** → 该工作把研究意义从单一 CAS 配方推广到更广泛的纤维素衍生物设计空间，为后续纤维素基打印水凝胶开发提供了材料路线。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要提到进行了 “subsequent evaluation of printing metrics”，但没有给出具体打印指标、误差范围、分辨率或形状保真度数据，因此目前还无法仅凭摘要判断其打印性能相对现有体系的领先程度。
- **[推断]** 摘要说明 Ca2+ 交联会显著影响收缩和溶胀，但未交代这种影响是优化还是代价，后续需要结合定量尺寸变化与力学测试来判断其应用边界。
- **[推断]** 文章当前只在固定 DSacetyl = 1.8 和固定分子量条件下优化 DSsulfate 与浓度，未来可扩展到乙酰化程度、分子量分布和多价离子类型的协同调控。

## 摘要

> [原文]「Hydrogels are excellent materials for fabricating flexible electronic devices, such as flexible sensors.」本文将水凝胶定位为柔性电子，尤其是柔性传感器的重要材料基础。  
> [原文]「obtaining hydrogels with superior swelling capacity and good hydrophilicity suitable for use under extreme environments, such as cold and underwater conditions, is still challenging due to the occurrence of freezing and excessive swelling」作者指出低温冻结与水下过度溶胀，是限制水凝胶在极端环境中应用的核心障碍。  
> [原文]「the design and synthetic strategies of antifreezing/antiswelling hydrogels reported in recent years are comprehensively analyzed along with their mechanisms and applications in flexible motion sensors」因此本文是一篇面向近年研究的综述，重点整合抗冻/抗溶胀水凝胶的设计、合成、机理与柔性运动传感应用。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「comprehensive reviews describing these hydrogels in terms of synthesis and application in sensors are still lacking」** — 文章的直接贡献是补上“合成策略 + 传感应用”这一综述空白。
- **[原文]「This review aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of the research of antifreezing/antiswelling hydrogels and offer valuable insights for researchers engaged in the development of advanced materials suitable for practical applications.」** — 作者不仅总结研究现状，也试图为面向实际应用的先进材料开发提供方法论参考。

## 方法

- **[原文]「the design and synthetic strategies of antifreezing/antiswelling hydrogels reported in recent years are comprehensively analyzed along with their mechanisms and applications in flexible motion sensors」** — 方法上属于文献综述与框架化归纳，而非单一实验研究；分析对象覆盖设计、合成策略、作用机制与传感应用四个层面。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「hydrogels with antifreezing and antiswelling capacities exhibit minimal changes in their physical and chemical properties under extreme conditions with retained original performance, such as mechanical properties, conductivity, and adhesiveness」** → 关键结论是抗冻/抗溶胀能力的价值不在单项性能提升，而在极端条件下尽可能保持力学性能、电导性与黏附性等原始功能。  
- **[原文]「Examples include flexible sensors for monitoring various motion signals, such as changes during sports events」** → 应用侧的代表方向是柔性运动传感，重点场景包括对多种运动信号的监测。  
- **[推断]** 摘要未提供具体数值、百分比或温度，说明本文强调的是研究版图与设计逻辑的系统梳理，而不是定量性能对比。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出不同抗冻/抗溶胀策略的统一评价指标、性能边界或具体环境参数，因此若要比较路线优劣，仍需回到正文与原始文献获取定量证据。
- **[推断]** 后续值得重点关注的问题包括：在低温与水下条件同时存在时，力学、电学与黏附性能之间是否存在更明确的权衡关系，以及这些材料在长期实际使用中的稳定性验证。

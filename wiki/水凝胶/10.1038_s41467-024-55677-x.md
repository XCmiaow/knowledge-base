# Hierarchically aligned heterogeneous core-sheath hydrogels

## 摘要

> 这篇工作面向“尤其是缺乏高精度结构设计的弱水凝胶”的性能瓶颈，[原文]「Such an integration of diverse incompatible mechanical properties is highly desired for man-made materials, especially weak hydrogels which are lack of high-precision structural design.」并提出了一种具有紧密交联鞘层与对齐多孔芯层的分级异质结构设计，[原文]「Herein, we demonstrate the fabrication of hierarchically aligned heterogeneous hydrogels consisting of a compactly crosslinked sheath and an aligned porous core with alignments of nanofibrils at multi-scales by a sequential self-assembly assisted salting out method.」  
> 所得水凝胶同时实现了超高韧性、强度、延展性、刚度、断裂能和抗疲劳阈值，并具备稳定再生与快速黏附能力，[原文]「The produced hydrogel offers ultrahigh mechanical properties among the reported hydrogels, elastomers and natural materials, including a toughness of 1031 MJ · m-3, strength of 55.3 MPa, strain of 3300%, stiffness of 6.8 MPa, fracture energy of 552.7 kJ · m-2 and fatigue threshold of 40.9 kJ · m-2.」[原文]「Furthermore, such a tough and strong hydrogel facilely achieves stable regeneration and rapid adhesion owing to the highly crystallized and aligned network structure.」  
> 再生后的样品在 10 次循环中仍表现出增强的强度、韧性和抗疲劳性，说明该方法面向实际应用具有可恢复性潜力，[原文]「The regenerated specimen presents the reinforced strength, toughness and fatigue resistance over 10 regeneration cycles.」[原文]「This work provides a simple method to produce hydrogels with bioinspired heterostructures and combinational properties for real applications.」

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Herein, we demonstrate the fabrication of hierarchically aligned heterogeneous hydrogels consisting of a compactly crosslinked sheath and an aligned porous core with alignments of nanofibrils at multi-scales by a sequential self-assembly assisted salting out method.」** — 贡献在于把“核鞘异质结构 + 多尺度纳米原纤维对齐 + 顺序自组装辅助盐析”组合成一个可实现的水凝胶构筑路线，用结构分级来同时整合通常彼此不相容的力学性能。

## 方法

- **[原文]「by a sequential self-assembly assisted salting out method」** — 方法核心是顺序自组装辅助盐析。
- **[原文]「consisting of a compactly crosslinked sheath and an aligned porous core with alignments of nanofibrils at multi-scales」** — 结构设计核心是紧密交联鞘层、对齐多孔芯层，以及多尺度纳米原纤维取向对齐。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「The produced hydrogel offers ultrahigh mechanical properties among the reported hydrogels, elastomers and natural materials, including a toughness of 1031 MJ · m-3, strength of 55.3 MPa, strain of 3300%, stiffness of 6.8 MPa, fracture energy of 552.7 kJ · m-2 and fatigue threshold of 40.9 kJ · m-2.」** → 该材料在已报道的水凝胶、弹性体和天然材料中表现出极高综合力学性能，关键指标包括韧性 1031 MJ·m-3、强度 55.3 MPa、应变 3300%、刚度 6.8 MPa、断裂能 552.7 kJ·m-2、疲劳阈值 40.9 kJ·m-2。
- **[原文]「Furthermore, such a tough and strong hydrogel facilely achieves stable regeneration and rapid adhesion owing to the highly crystallized and aligned network structure.」** → 高结晶且对齐的网络结构不仅支撑高强高韧，也带来了稳定再生与快速黏附。
- **[原文]「The regenerated specimen presents the reinforced strength, toughness and fatigue resistance over 10 regeneration cycles.」** → 再生后样品在 10 次再生循环中仍显示增强的强度、韧性和抗疲劳性，说明其可恢复使用潜力较强。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有交代具体组成、盐析条件、样品尺寸、测试环境与统计波动，因此目前只能确认性能结果与结构设计方向，尚无法仅凭摘要判断工艺普适性、放大制造难度和结果重复性。
- **[推断]** 摘要强调“real applications”，但未给出长期服役、环境稳定性、生物相容性或复杂界面黏附对象的数据，后续需要完整正文和补充信息来验证应用边界。

# Biomimetic anti-freezing polymeric hydrogels: keeping soft-wet materials active in cold environments

## 摘要

This review summarizes anti-freezing hydrogels inspired by cold-adapted organisms. It organizes strategies based on salts, alcohols and organohydrogels, ionic liquids and ionogels, and polymer-network modification, while also covering applications enabled by widened temperature tolerance.

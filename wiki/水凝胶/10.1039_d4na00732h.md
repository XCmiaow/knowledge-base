# A rapid one-step synthesis of silver and copper coordinated chlorine functionalized fullerene nanoparticles with enhanced antibacterial activity

**中文标题**: 银与铜配位氯功能化富勒烯纳米颗粒的快速一步法合成及其增强抗菌活性

## 摘要

> [原文]「This research presents an investigation into the synthesis, characterization, and antibacterial evaluation of silver-coordinated chloro-fullerenes nanoparticles (Ag-C60-Cl) and copper-coordinated chloro-fullerenes nanoparticles (Cu-C60-Cl).」本文聚焦 Ag-C60-Cl 与 Cu-C60-Cl 两类纳米颗粒的合成、表征与抗菌性能评估。  
> [原文]「Utilizing an innovative, rapid one-step synthesis approach, the nanoparticles were rigorously characterized using X-ray Photoelectron Spectroscopy (XPS), Scanning Electron Microscopy-Energy Dispersive X-ray Spectrometer (SEM-EDS), High-Resolution Transmission Electron Microscopy (HR-TEM), Fourier-Transform Infrared Spectroscopy (FTIR), and Raman spectroscopy.」研究采用快速一步法制备材料，并结合 XPS、SEM-EDS、HR-TEM、FTIR 与 Raman 光谱进行系统表征。  
> [原文]「These findings demonstrated that the synthesized Ag-C60-Cl and Cu-C60-Cl nanoparticles exhibited minimum inhibitory concentrations (MIC) of 3.9 μg mL−1 and 125 μg mL−1, respectively.」结果显示两类材料均具抑菌活性，其中 Ag-C60-Cl 的 MIC 低至 3.9 μg mL−1，优于 Cu-C60-Cl 的 125 μg mL−1。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Nanoparticle modification demonstrates a remarkable synergetic effect in combating bacteria, particularly resistant bacteria, enhancing their efficacy by simultaneously targeting multiple cellular pathways.」** — 文章将纳米颗粒修饰定位为对抗细菌、尤其耐药菌的协同策略，强调其可通过同时作用多个细胞通路增强抗菌效果。
- **[原文]「This approach positions them as a potent solution against the growing challenge of antimicrobial-resistant (AMR) strains.」** — 研究意义被明确指向 AMR 场景，说明该材料体系面向耐药菌问题具有应用潜力。
- **[原文]「This research presents an investigation into the synthesis, characterization, and antibacterial evaluation of silver-coordinated chloro-fullerenes nanoparticles (Ag-C60-Cl) and copper-coordinated chloro-fullerenes nanoparticles (Cu-C60-Cl).」** — 核心贡献是提出并评估两种金属配位氯功能化富勒烯纳米颗粒体系，而不是仅停留在单一材料合成。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Utilizing an innovative, rapid one-step synthesis approach, the nanoparticles were rigorously characterized using X-ray Photoelectron Spectroscopy (XPS), Scanning Electron Microscopy-Energy Dispersive X-ray Spectrometer (SEM-EDS), High-Resolution Transmission Electron Microscopy (HR-TEM), Fourier-Transform Infrared Spectroscopy (FTIR), and Raman spectroscopy.」** — 方法学上采用快速一步法完成纳米颗粒制备，并用多种光谱与显微技术交叉验证材料组成与结构。
- **[原文]「In conjunction with the analytical techniques, a computational approach was utilized to corroborate the findings from Raman spectroscopy as well as the surface potential of these nanoparticles.」** — 除实验表征外，作者还引入计算方法，用于佐证 Raman 结果以及颗粒表面电势判断。
- **[原文]「Moreover, the antibacterial activities of the synthesized nanoparticles were assessed against Escherichia coli (E. coli) and Methicillin-Resistant Staphylococcus aureus (MRSA).」** — 抗菌评价覆盖 E. coli 与 MRSA，兼顾常见革兰阴性菌与耐药革兰阳性菌模型。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「These findings demonstrated that the synthesized Ag-C60-Cl and Cu-C60-Cl nanoparticles exhibited minimum inhibitory concentrations (MIC) of 3.9 μg mL−1 and 125 μg mL−1, respectively.」** → 定量结果表明 Ag-C60-Cl 的抑菌效力明显强于 Cu-C60-Cl，二者 MIC 分别为 3.9 μg mL−1 和 125 μg mL−1。
- **[原文]「Reactive oxygen species (ROS) quantification, catalase assay, and efflux pump inhibition results revealed promising broad-spectrum antibacterial effects.」** → 机制线索显示，该体系的广谱抗菌效应可能与 ROS 相关过程、catalase assay 所反映的氧化应激响应以及 efflux pump inhibition 有关。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要给出了合成、表征、E. coli/MRSA 抗菌结果与机制线索，但未报告宿主细胞毒性、体内验证、长期稳定性和与未配位对照材料的系统比较，后续若要支持转化应用，需要补足这些关键证据。
- **[推断]** 目前只给出 MIC 的终点结果，缺少更细的剂量-效应、时间-杀菌动力学和耐药性演化数据，后续可围绕这些维度进一步建立机制与应用边界。

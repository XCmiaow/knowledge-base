## 摘要

> [原文]「Cellulose membranes were prepared from an EMIMAc ionic liquid solution by nonsolvent-induced phase separation (NIPS) in coagulation baths of water–acetone mixtures, ethanol–water mixtures and water at different temperatures.」研究以 EMIMAc 离子液体溶液为起点，采用非溶剂诱导相分离（NIPS），系统比较了水–丙酮混合浴、乙醇–水混合浴和不同温度水浴中的纤维素膜再生行为。  
> [原文]「High water volume fractions in the coagulation bath result in a highly reproducible gel-like structure with inhomogeneities observed by small-angle neutron scattering (SANS).」与[原文]「A structural transition of cellulose takes place in water–acetone baths at very low water volume fractions, while a higher water bath temperature increases the size of inhomogeneities in the gel-like structure.」结果表明，凝固浴中的含水比例和水浴温度都会改变湿态凝胶结构，其中高含水比例带来高可重复性结构，极低含水比例可触发结构转变，更高温度则会增大结构不均一性。  
> [原文]「These findings demonstrate the value of SANS for characterising and understanding the structure of regenerated cellulose films in their wet state.」与[原文]「Such insights can improve the engineering and structural tuning of cellulose membranes, either for direct use or as precursors for carbon molecular sieve membranes.」作者据此强调，SANS 对湿态再生纤维素薄膜的结构理解具有直接价值，并能支持纤维素膜及碳分子筛膜前驱体的结构调控。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「These findings demonstrate the value of SANS for characterising and understanding the structure of regenerated cellulose films in their wet state.」** — 核心贡献是证明 SANS 不只是补充表征手段，而是理解湿态再生纤维素薄膜纳米结构的关键工具。
- **[原文]「Such insights can improve the engineering and structural tuning of cellulose membranes, either for direct use or as precursors for carbon molecular sieve membranes.」** — 研究把基础结构表征与膜工程应用直接连接起来，指出这些结构信息可用于优化直接使用的纤维素膜以及碳分子筛膜前驱体。

## 方法

- **[原文]「Cellulose membranes were prepared from an EMIMAc ionic liquid solution by nonsolvent-induced phase separation (NIPS) in coagulation baths of water–acetone mixtures, ethanol–water mixtures and water at different temperatures.」** — 方法上以 EMIMAc 离子液体体系溶解纤维素，再通过 NIPS 在三类凝固浴条件下再生薄膜，实验变量覆盖凝固浴组成和温度。
- **[原文]「High water volume fractions in the coagulation bath result in a highly reproducible gel-like structure with inhomogeneities observed by small-angle neutron scattering (SANS).」** — 结构表征使用 SANS，关注的是湿态凝胶状结构及其中的不均一性，而不是仅看干膜终态。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「High water volume fractions in the coagulation bath result in a highly reproducible gel-like structure with inhomogeneities observed by small-angle neutron scattering (SANS).」** → 高含水体积分数的凝固浴会得到高度可重复的凝胶状结构，但该结构内部仍存在 SANS 可观测的不均一性。
- **[原文]「A structural transition of cellulose takes place in water–acetone baths at very low water volume fractions, while a higher water bath temperature increases the size of inhomogeneities in the gel-like structure.」** → 在水–丙酮凝固浴中，纤维素会在“very low water volume fractions”下发生结构转变；同时，更高的水浴温度会增大凝胶状结构中的不均一尺度。
- **[原文]「Cellulose membranes were prepared from an EMIMAc ionic liquid solution by nonsolvent-induced phase separation (NIPS) in coagulation baths of water–acetone mixtures, ethanol–water mixtures and water at different temperatures.」** → 关键变量不是单一溶剂替换，而是凝固浴体系从水–丙酮到乙醇–水再到不同温度纯水的连续变化，这为比较浴组成与温度效应提供了同一制膜框架。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要给出了 “very low water volume fractions” 和 “different temperatures” 的方向性信息，但没有提供具体体积分数、温度数值或范围，这限制了结果的直接复现实验设计。
- **[推断]** 摘要强调的是湿态结构与 SANS 观察结果，未说明这些纳米结构差异如何进一步映射到分离性能、力学性能或后续碳化后的膜性能，因此应用层面的因果链仍需正文数据支撑。
- **[推断]** 乙醇–水混合浴被纳入方法描述，但摘要没有单独交代其结构结果，说明其作用机制可能需要依赖全文图谱和对照分析才能判断。

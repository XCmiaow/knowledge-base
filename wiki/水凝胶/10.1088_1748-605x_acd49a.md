# Injectable cell-laden hydrogels fabricated with cellulose and chitosan nanofibers for bioprinted liver tissues

**中文标题**: 用纤维素与壳聚糖纳米纤维制备可注射细胞负载水凝胶用于生物打印肝组织

## 摘要

> **[原文]「Bio-based hydrogels as three-dimensional (3D) constructs have attracted attention in advanced tissue engineering. Compared with conventional two-dimensional (2D) cell culture, cells grown in 3D scaffolds are expected to demonstrate the inherent behavior of living organisms of cellular spheroids.」** — 文章将生物基水凝胶定位为先进组织工程中的 3D 构建体，核心动机是相较传统 2D 培养，更好模拟细胞球体的天然生命行为。  
> **[原文]「Herein, we constructed cell-laden nanofiber-based hydrogels in combination with 2,2,6,6-tetramethylpiperidine 1-oxyl-oxidized cellulose nanofiber (TOCNF) and chitosan nanofiber (CsNF) for bioadaptive liver tissue engineering.」** — 作者用 TOCNF 与 CsNF 构建细胞负载纳米纤维水凝胶，目标明确指向生物适配性的肝组织工程。  
> **[原文]「As-designed hydrogels exhibited excellent viscoelastic properties with typical shear-thinning behavior, and had a storage modulus of 1234 Pa ± 68 Pa, suitable for cell culture... which enabled 10 d growth with high gene expression level of albumin of HepG2 spheroids in the 3D gels.」** — 结果上，这种水凝胶兼具适于注射/打印的流变性能与 1234 Pa ± 68 Pa 的储能模量，并支持 HepG2 在 3D 胶内生长 10 d 且维持较高白蛋白基因表达。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「we constructed cell-laden nanofiber-based hydrogels in combination with 2,2,6,6-tetramethylpiperidine 1-oxyl-oxidized cellulose nanofiber (TOCNF) and chitosan nanofiber (CsNF) for bioadaptive liver tissue engineering」** — 核心贡献是提出一种以 TOCNF 和 CsNF 为基础的细胞负载纳米纤维水凝胶体系，并将其直接面向肝组织工程场景。
- **[原文]「The biodegradable cell-laden hydrogels are expected to mimic the cellular microenvironment and provide potential for bioadaptive 3D cell cultures in biomedical applications.」** — 文章的应用价值在于把可降解细胞负载水凝胶作为细胞微环境模拟平台，为生物医学中的适配性 3D 细胞培养提供材料基础。

## 方法

- **[原文]「The carboxylates of TOCNF and amines of CsNF were directly crosslinked via EDC/NHS chemistry.」** — 交联策略是利用 TOCNF 的羧酸盐与 CsNF 的胺基，经 EDC/NHS 化学直接成胶。
- **[原文]「The rheological properties of the solutions for the nanofibers and hydrogels revealed sufficient physical properties for the injection, printing, and plotting process, as well as significant encapsulation of living cells.」** — 方法学验证聚焦于流变学，证明该体系满足注射、打印、绘制以及活细胞包埋的工艺要求。
- **[原文]「Non-cytotoxicity was confirmed using a live/dead assay with mouse-derived fibroblast NIH/3T3 cells. Human hepatocellular carcinoma HepG2 cells could be cultured on a gel surface (2D environment) and encapsulated in the gel structure (3D environment)」** — 生物学评估分两步：先用 NIH/3T3 live/dead assay 验证无细胞毒性，再比较 HepG2 在胶表面 2D 与胶内部 3D 两种培养环境中的表现。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「As-designed hydrogels exhibited excellent viscoelastic properties with typical shear-thinning behavior, and had a storage modulus of 1234 Pa ± 68 Pa, suitable for cell culture.」** → 关键材料参数是典型剪切变稀行为与 1234 Pa ± 68 Pa 的储能模量，这解释了其为何同时适合打印加工和细胞培养。
- **[原文]「which enabled 10 d growth with high gene expression level of albumin of HepG2 spheroids in the 3D gels」** → 在功能层面，3D 胶内 HepG2 细胞球可持续生长 10 d，并保持较高 albumin 基因表达，说明该体系对肝样表型维持有支持作用。
- **[原文]「cells grown in 3D scaffolds are expected to demonstrate the inherent behavior of living organisms of cellular spheroids」** → 文章把 3D 培养优于 2D 的价值明确落在“更接近细胞球天然行为”这一点上，这也是其肝组织工程设计的理论前提。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要没有给出打印分辨率、成形保真度、降解速率、长期培养超过 10 d 的稳定性数据，因此现阶段更像是“材料与初步细胞相容性验证”，距离完整生物打印肝组织功能评价还有空缺。
- **[推断]** 细胞模型使用了 NIH/3T3 与 HepG2，前者是小鼠成纤维细胞，后者是人肝癌细胞；摘要尚未显示原代肝细胞、共培养体系或更高阶肝功能指标，后续应补足更接近真实肝组织的验证。
- **[推断]** 虽然摘要强调 injection, printing, and plotting，但未报告工艺窗口参数，后续可围绕喷嘴条件、剪切损伤与打印后结构稳定性建立更完整的生物制造规范。

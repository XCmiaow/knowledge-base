### 1. 核心发现
本文系统研究了高氯酸锂（LiClO4）掺杂对醋酸纤维素（CA）基聚合物电解质膜性能的影响。研究发现，LiClO4含量的增加能显著提升膜的离子电导率，并在25% (w/w)时达到最佳值。同时，LiClO4的添加对膜的力学性能产生先增强后弱化的影响，其中10% (w/w)为力学性能提升的拐点。

### 2. 创新点
- **[原文]「The need for secondary batteries is increasing every year... This study was conducted to determine the effect of lithium perchlorate content on the polymer electrolyte membranes of cellulose acetate-LiClO4.」**

- **[原文]「The cellulose acetate-LiClO4 membranes were prepared by mixing cellulose acetate and LiClO4 in various compositions using tetrahydrofurane (THF) as solvent.」**
  — 采用溶液浇铸法，以四氢呋喃（THF）为溶剂制备了一系列不同LiClO4掺杂比例的CA-LiClO4复合膜，这是一种相对简单且可控的制备工艺。

### 3. 方法简述
- **[原文]「The cellulose acetate-LiClO4 membranes were prepared by mixing cellulose acetate and LiClO4 in various compositions using tetrahydrofurane (THF) as solvent.」**
  — **制备方法**：采用溶液浇铸法。将醋酸纤维素（CA）和不同比例的LiClO4溶解于四氢呋喃（THF）中，混合均匀后浇铸成膜。
- **[原文]「The effect of LiClO4 ratios on the polymer electrolyte membranes was studied by analysis of the functional groups using FTIR (Fourier Transform Infrared) spectroscopy measurement, the ionic conductivity by EIS (Electrochemical Impedance Spectroscopy) method, and mechanical properties by tensile tester measurements.」**
  — **表征手段**：综合运用傅里叶变换红外光谱（FTIR）分析官能团变化；电化学阻抗谱（EIS）测量离子电导率；拉伸试验机测试力学性能。

### 4. 关键结果（定量数据）
- **[原文]「The ionic conductivity of the membranes increased with the increasing in the ratios of lithium perchlorate content in the membranes and reached the optimum value at 1.79×10−4 S cm−1 corresponded to the cellulose acetate doped with 25% (w/w) LiClO4 membrane.」**
  → **离子电导率**：随LiClO4含量增加而增大，在掺杂量为 **25% (w/w)** 时达到最优值 **1.79×10⁻⁴ S cm⁻¹**。
- **[原文]「The presence of 10% (w/w) LiClO4 content within cellulose acetate membranes can increase the mechanical properties of the membranes from 19.89 to 43.29 MPa for tensile strength, and from 2.55 to 4.53% for elongation at break.」**
  → **力学性能（10% LiClO4）**：相较于纯CA膜，添加 **10% (w/w) LiClO4** 使膜的拉伸强度从 **19.89 MPa 提高至 43.29 MPa**，断裂伸长率从 **2.55% 提高至 4.53%**。
- **[原文]「However, when the cellulose acetate membranes containing ratio of LiClO4 more than 10% (w/w), consequently the tensile strength tended to decrease and the elongation at break was increased.」**
  → **力学性能（>10% LiClO4）**：当LiClO4掺杂比例 **超过10% (w/w)** 后，膜的拉伸强度开始下降，而断裂伸长率继续增加。

# Ionogel-based flexible electronics

**中文标题**: 离子凝胶基柔性电子

## 摘要

> [原文] "Ionogels have gained significant attention due to their unique physicochemical properties—such as non-volatility, excellent thermal and electrochemical stability, mechanical properties and ionic conductivity—making them crucial materials in flexible electronics." 文章指出，离子凝胶因非挥发性、优异热稳定性、电化学稳定性、机械性能与离子电导率，已成为柔性电子的关键材料。
>
> [原文] "This work focuses on reviewing research progress in the design of ionogels with diverse functionalities and their applications in flexible electronics (primarily over the past five years)"; [原文] "Further discussion highlights innovative applications of ionogels in sensors, energy-harvesting devices, storage devices and smart devices"; [原文] "Finally, we propose future directions for ionogels, including multifunctional integration, long-term stability and self-healing capabilities" 这是一篇综述，重点覆盖“primarily over the past five years”的进展，梳理离子凝胶在传感、能量采集、储能与智能器件中的应用，并把多功能集成、长期稳定性和自愈能力列为后续方向。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]** "In this review, we comprehensively introduce the fundamental composition and structure of ionogels based on their structure-property-application relationships." 文章的核心贡献是用“结构-性质-应用”框架系统整理离子凝胶的基础组成与结构。
- **[原文]** "Further discussion highlights innovative applications of ionogels in sensors, energy-harvesting devices, storage devices and smart devices, emphasizing their broad potential in flexible electronics." 文章进一步把重点落到器件端，强调离子凝胶在多类柔性电子场景中的广泛潜力。

## 方法

- **[原文]** "This work focuses on reviewing research progress in the design of ionogels with diverse functionalities and their applications in flexible electronics (primarily over the past five years), compared with previously published reviews." 方法上这是面向近五年研究进展、并与既有综述对照的综述性工作，而非单一实验研究。
- **[原文]** "we comprehensively introduce the fundamental composition and structure of ionogels based on their structure-property-application relationships." 组织方式上按结构-性质-应用关系展开，便于从材料设计映射到器件应用。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]** "primarily over the past five years" → 摘要中唯一明确的时间尺度是“过去五年”，说明文章结论主要反映这一时间窗口内的研究进展。
- **[原文]** "Further discussion highlights innovative applications of ionogels in sensors, energy-harvesting devices, storage devices and smart devices" → 关键应用场景集中在传感、能量采集、储能与智能器件。
- **[原文]** "non-volatility, excellent thermal and electrochemical stability, mechanical properties and ionic conductivity" → 支撑这些应用的关键材料优势是非挥发性、热稳定性、电化学稳定性、机械性能和离子电导率。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要把 "multifunctional integration, long-term stability and self-healing capabilities" 明确列为未来方向，说明现阶段离子凝胶柔性电子在多功能集成、长期稳定性和自愈能力上仍有明显缺口。

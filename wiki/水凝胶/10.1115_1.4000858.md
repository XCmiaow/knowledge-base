### 1. 核心发现
本研究通过将明胶溶液与细菌纤维素（BC）复合，成功制备了一种新型的细菌纤维素/明胶复合支架。该复合支架表现出良好的形态相容性，且相比纯BC，其生物相容性显著提升，证明其具有作为伤口敷料或组织工程支架的潜力。

- **[原文]「The prepared bacterial cellulose/gelatin scaffolds were characterized by scanning electron microscopy, Fourier transform infrared spectroscopy, X-ray diffraction, mechanical test, swelling, and thermal studies.」** — 对该新型复合材料进行了系统全面的物理化学及生物学表征。

### 3. 方法简述
- **[原文]「The novel bacterial cellulose/gelatin composite scaffolds were prepared using aqueous gelatin solution and bacterial cellulose excreted by Acetobacter xylinum.」** — 采用醋酸杆菌（Acetobacter xylinum）分泌的细菌纤维素与明胶水溶液进行复合，制备了新型复合支架。
- **[原文]「...were characterized by scanning electron microscopy, Fourier transform infrared spectroscopy, X-ray diffraction, mechanical test, swelling, and thermal studies.」** — 综合运用了扫描电子显微镜（SEM）、傅里叶变换红外光谱（FTIR）、X射线衍射（XRD）、力学测试、溶胀测试和热分析等多种手段对材料进行表征。

- **[原文]「Young’s modulus of the composite was increased from 3.7 GPa to 3.9 GPa」** → 复合材料的杨氏模量从纯BC的3.7 GPa提高到了3.9 GPa。

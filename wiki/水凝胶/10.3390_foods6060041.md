### 1. 核心发现
本研究首次将羧甲基纤维素（CMC）与冷水电鱼明胶结合，通过溶液浇铸法制备可食用薄膜，发现CMC的添加能显著改善薄膜的机械性能并降低其平衡吸湿性，为开发基于天然聚合物的可生物降解食品包装材料提供了新途径。

### 2. 创新点
- **[原文]「This research for the first time modified and improved the mechanical properties of cold water fish gelatin films in combination with carboxymethyl cellulose.」** — 本文首次报道了利用羧甲基纤维素（CMC）改性并提升冷水电鱼明胶薄膜的机械性能。

### 3. 方法简述
- **[原文]「Cold water fish gelatin films along with carboxymethyl cellulose with concentrations of 0%, 5%, 10%, 20% and 50% were prepared using the casting method.」** — 采用溶液浇铸法制备了CMC浓度为0%（对照）、5%、10%、20%和50%的冷水电鱼明胶复合薄膜。
- **[原文]「The mechanical properties were tested by the American National Standard Method.」** — 依据美国国家标准方法测试了薄膜的机械性能。

### 4. 关键结果（定量数据）
- **[原文]「Studying the absorption isotherm of the resulting composite films specified that the humidity of single-layer water decreased (p < 0.05) and caused a reduction in the equilibrium moisture of these films.」** → 吸附等温线分析表明，CMC的添加导致单层水湿度显著降低（p < 0.05），从而降低了薄膜的平衡吸湿量。
- **[原文]「In the mechanical testing of the composite films, the tensile strength and Young’s modulus significantly increased and the elongation percent significantly decreased with the increase in the concentration of carboxymethyl cellulose.」** → 随着CMC浓度（从0%增至50%）的增加，复合薄膜的拉伸强度和杨氏模量显著升高，而断裂伸长率显著降低。

## 摘要

> [原文] "Hydrogels are soft-wet materials with a hydrophilic three-dimensional network structure offering controllable stretchability, conductivity, and biocompatibility." 水凝胶是具有亲水性三维网络结构的软湿材料，核心优势是可控拉伸性、导电性与生物相容性。  
> [原文] "However, traditional conductive hydrogels only operate in mild environments and exhibit poor environmental tolerance due to their high water content and hydrophilic network, which result in undesirable swelling, susceptibility to freezing at sub-zero temperatures, and structural dehydration through evaporation." 传统导电水凝胶由于高含水量和亲水网络，通常只能在温和环境中工作，主要问题是易溶胀、在零下温度下易冻结、并会因蒸发发生结构脱水。  
> [原文] "Therefore, developing environmentally tolerant conductive hydrogels (ETCHs) is crucial to increasing the application scope of these materials." "In this review, we summarize recent strategies for designing multifunctional conductive hydrogels that possess anti-freezing, anti-drying, and anti-swelling properties." 本文聚焦环境耐受型导电水凝胶（ETCHs），综述其抗冻、抗干燥、抗溶胀设计策略及应用前景。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文] "In this review, we summarize recent strategies for designing multifunctional conductive hydrogels that possess anti-freezing, anti-drying, and anti-swelling properties."** → 文章的核心贡献是系统整理兼具抗冻、抗干燥、抗溶胀三类环境耐受性的多功能导电水凝胶设计策略。
- **[原文] "Furthermore, we briefly introduce some of the applications of ETCHs, including wearable sensors, bioelectrodes, soft robots, and wound dressings."** → 文章将材料设计与应用场景联结起来，覆盖可穿戴传感器、生物电极、软体机器人和伤口敷料。
- **[原文] "The current development status of different types of ETCHs and their limitations are analyzed to further discuss future research directions and development prospects."** → 文章不仅总结进展，也对现有不同类型 ETCHs 的局限进行了归纳，并延伸到未来研究方向。

## 方法

- **[原文] "In this review, we summarize recent strategies for designing multifunctional conductive hydrogels that possess anti-freezing, anti-drying, and anti-swelling properties."** → 方法上属于综述性梳理，按抗冻、抗干燥、抗溶胀等环境耐受目标组织设计策略。
- **[原文] "Furthermore, we briefly introduce some of the applications of ETCHs, including wearable sensors, bioelectrodes, soft robots, and wound dressings."** → 结构上采用“材料设计策略 + 应用场景概览”的综述框架。

## 关键发现

- **[原文] "a hydrophilic three-dimensional network structure"** → 摘要明确指出水凝胶的基础结构是亲水性三维网络，这一“三维”结构描述是理解其力学与传输性能的关键。
- **[原文] "susceptibility to freezing at sub-zero temperatures"** → 传统导电水凝胶在零下温度下存在冻结风险，说明低温稳定性是环境耐受设计中的关键约束。
- **[原文] "anti-freezing, anti-drying, and anti-swelling properties"** → 环境耐受型导电水凝胶需要同时面向抗冻、抗干燥、抗溶胀三项性能，而不是只优化单一指标。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 摘要强调“different types of ETCHs and their limitations”，说明现有 ETCHs 方案之间仍存在明显性能边界，尚未形成可普适迁移的统一最优设计。
- **[推断]** 由于摘要只概述策略、应用与局限，未给出统一测试协议或跨体系定量对比，后续研究可能需要更标准化的环境耐受评价框架。
- **[推断]** 未来突破点很可能在于同时兼顾环境耐受性、导电性、力学性能与生物相容性，而非单一维度强化。

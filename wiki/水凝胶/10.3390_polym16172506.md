## 摘要

> [原文]「Since the last decade, the need for deformable electronics exponentially increased, requiring adaptive energy storage systems, especially batteries and supercapacitors.」本文指出，过去十年可变形电子需求快速增长，直接拉动了对自适应储能系统，尤其是电池与超级电容器的需求。  
> [原文]「Among diverse materials, gel polymer electrolytes (hydrogels, organogels, and ionogels) remain the most studied thanks to the ability to tune the physicochemical and mechanical properties by changing the nature of the precursors, the type of interactions, and the formulation.」本文强调水凝胶、有机凝胶和离子凝胶之所以成为研究重点，在于其理化与力学性质可通过前驱体、相互作用类型和配方进行调控。  
> [原文]「Nevertheless, the exploitation of this category of electrolyte as a possible commercial product is still restrained, due to different issues related to the nature of the gels (ionic conductivity, evaporation of filling solvent, toxicity, etc.).」与[原文]「Therefore, this review aims to resume different strategies to tailor the properties of the gel polymer electrolytes as well as to provide recent advancements in the field toward the elaboration of deformable batteries and supercapacitors.」本文同时概括了商业化受限的关键问题，并将综述目标定位为总结性质调控策略与柔性电池、超级电容器方向的最新进展。

## 核心贡献

- **[原文]「Therefore, this review aims to resume different strategies to tailor the properties of the gel polymer electrolytes as well as to provide recent advancements in the field toward the elaboration of deformable batteries and supercapacitors.」** — 这篇综述的核心贡献是系统整理凝胶聚合物电解质的性质调控策略，并把讨论落点放在可变形电池和超级电容器两类柔性储能器件上。
- **[原文]「Among diverse materials, gel polymer electrolytes (hydrogels, organogels, and ionogels) remain the most studied thanks to the ability to tune the physicochemical and mechanical properties by changing the nature of the precursors, the type of interactions, and the formulation.」** — 文中明确给出三类重点材料，并指出其研究价值来自“可调性”，这是其适配柔性储能场景的理论基础。

## 方法

- **[原文]「This review aims to resume different strategies to tailor the properties of the gel polymer electrolytes」** — 方法上这是一篇综述性文章，主要通过归纳不同性质调控策略来组织论述，而不是报告单一实验体系。
- **[原文]「by changing the nature of the precursors, the type of interactions, and the formulation」** — 文中给出了三条主要调控维度：前驱体性质、相互作用类型与配方设计。

## 关键发现

- **[原文]「Since the last decade, the need for deformable electronics exponentially increased」** → 摘要中唯一明确的时间尺度是“the last decade”，说明该领域在过去十年持续升温。  
- **[原文]「different issues related to the nature of the gels (ionic conductivity, evaporation of filling solvent, toxicity, etc.)」** → 关键瓶颈集中在离子电导率、填充溶剂挥发和毒性等问题，这些因素直接限制其商业化落地。  
- **[原文]「hydrogels, organogels, and ionogels」** → 文章将凝胶聚合物电解质明确划分为水凝胶、有机凝胶和离子凝胶三类，构成后续比较与策略总结的基本框架。  
- **[推断]** 摘要没有提供离子电导率数值、温度范围、容量提升百分比等实验定量指标，因此其“关键发现”主要体现在研究趋势、材料分类与问题归纳，而非性能数据比较。

## 不足与展望

- **[推断]** 从摘要看，本文能够清晰归纳研究方向与主要障碍，但缺少具体体系之间的量化对比信息，因此读者若要进行材料选型或性能评估，还需要进入正文查看更细的实验和表格证据。
- **[推断]** 既然摘要将商业化障碍概括为离子电导率、溶剂挥发和毒性，后续值得重点关注的方向应是兼顾高导电性、环境稳定性与安全性的配方设计。

### 1. 核心发现
本文开发了一种由壳聚糖、κ-卡拉胶和羧甲基纤维素钠（CMC）组成的pH敏感三元体系。研究发现，冷冻速率是决定姜黄素释放行为（突发释放或受控一级释放）和冻干样品微结构（孔径大小）的关键因素。此外，该水凝胶体系在酸性介质中表现出显著的溶胀响应。

### 2. 创新点
- **[原文]「The aim of this study was to develop a pH-sensitive ternary system of chitosan, κ-carrageenan, and carboxymethylcellulose sodium salt (CMC) that can serve as a controlled release system.」** — 首次提出并研究壳聚糖、κ-卡拉胶和CMC钠盐构成的pH敏感三元控释体系。
- **[原文]「Encapsulation of curcumin in chitosan was achieved by transforming the present colloidal suspension into hydrogels via cryotropic gel formation.」** — 采用冷冻凝胶化（cryotropic gel formation）这一特定技术，将壳聚糖胶体悬浮液直接转化为用于封装姜黄素的水凝胶。

### 3. 方法简述
- **[原文]「The frozen hydrogel specimens (cryogels) were freeze-dried for preservation and investigation.」** — 将冷冻得到的水凝胶（即冷冻胶）进行冷冻干燥，以保存样品并进行后续研究。
- **[原文]「The microstructural morphology of freeze-dried specimens was observed with a Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM)」** — 使用扫描电子显微镜（SEM）观察冻干样品的微观形貌。
- **[原文]「Swelling tests of the hydrogels in buffer solutions of different pH values」** — 在不同pH值的缓冲溶液中对水凝胶进行溶胀测试。

### 4. 关键结果（定量数据）
- **[原文]「the cooling rate was found to determine the type of release between burst type and controlled first-order-rate type」** → 冷却速率决定了姜黄素的释放类型是突发释放还是受控的一级动力学释放。
- **[原文]「The faster cooling rate produced specimens with slightly larger pore size and, as a result, higher amount of curcumin released at 2 days.」** → 较快的冷却速率导致冻干样品孔径略大，从而在**2天**时释放出更多姜黄素。
- **[原文]「Swelling tests... showed that acidic media had a pronounced effect on swelling compared to the neutral and alkaline media.」** → 与中性和碱性介质相比，酸性介质对水凝胶的溶胀有显著影响。
- **[原文]「The faster cooling rate produced specimens that had higher structural strength against swelling despite the fact that the specimens had larger pore size and faster controlled release rate.」** → 尽管孔径更大、释放速率更快，但冷却速率更快的样品反而具有更高的抵抗溶胀的结构强度。

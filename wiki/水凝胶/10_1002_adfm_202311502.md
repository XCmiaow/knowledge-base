## 摘要

> <jats:title>Abstract</jats:title><jats:p>Artificial electronic skin (E‐skin), a class of promising materials mimicking the physical‐chemical and sensory performance of the human skin, has gained extensive interest in the field of human health‐monitoring and robotic skins. However, developing E‐skin simultaneously achieving high resilience, hysteresis‐free, and absent external power is always a formidable challenge. Herein, a liquid‐free eutectic gel‐based self‐powered E‐skin with high resilience, fatigue resistance, and conductivity is prepared by introducing hydroxypropyl cellulose (HPC) into metal salt‐based deep eutectic solvents (MDES). The unique structural design of cellulose‐anchored permanent entangled poly(acrylic acid) (PAA) chain, in combination with rapid broken/reconstruction of the dense dynamic sacrificial bonds, realizes the fabrication of high‐elastic E‐skin with negligible hysteresis. This further demonstrates the promising practical application of the cellulose‐based eutectogel with high transmittance (92%), high conductivity (36.6 mS m<jats:sup>−1</jats:sup>), and high resilience (98.1%), and excellent environment stability in robust triboelectric nanogenerator for energy harvesting and high resilience, self‐powered E‐skin for human health‐caring and human‐machine interaction.</jats:p>

## Codex 深读

- 文章通过将羟丙基纤维素（HPC）引入金属盐基深共熔溶剂（MDES），制备了兼具高回弹、抗疲劳和导电性的液体自由共晶凝胶自供能电子皮肤。
- 该材料的结构基础是纤维素锚定的永久缠结聚丙烯酸（PAA）链，以及高密度动态牺牲键的快速断裂与重构，因此电子皮肤表现出高弹性和可忽略的滞后。
- 该纤维素基共晶凝胶具有 92% 透光率、36.6 mS m−1 电导率、98.1% 回弹率和良好的环境稳定性，可用于稳健的摩擦纳米发电机能量采集，以及人体健康护理和人机交互中的高回弹自供能电子皮肤。

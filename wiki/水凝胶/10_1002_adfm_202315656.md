## 摘要

> <jats:title>Abstract</jats:title><jats:p>Conductive hydrogels have shown significant potential in the realm of flexible electronics; however, using water as a solvent introduces problems such as low‐temperature freezing and water loss. One possible solution to address these limitations is replacing water with deep eutectic solvents (DES). In this study, the PHEAA–gelatin–MXene (PGM) eutectogels are constructed with poly(<jats:italic>N</jats:italic>‐hydroxyethyl acrylamide) (PHEAA) and gelatin as the main components, and MXene nanosheets are added as nanofillers. The PGM eutectogels exhibit exceptional tensile and compressive mechanical properties, including remarkable stretchability (940%), high strength (0.5 MPa), high toughness (1.39 MJ m<jats:sup>−3</jats:sup>), and impressive compressive strength (0.3 MPa at 80% strain). Additionally, the PGM eutectogels demonstrate excellent adhesion, anti‐freezing, and long‐term anti‐drying abilities. Moreover, the PGM eutectogels multifunctional sensors allow high sensitivity, which enables accurate real‐time and stable monitoring of human activities over a wide temperature range. Consequently, the PGM eutectogels hold great potential as candidates in fields such as flexible wearable devices, personal healthcare, and human–machine interfaces.</jats:p>

## Codex 深读

- 文章以聚 N-羟乙基丙烯酰胺（PHEAA）和明胶为主体，并引入 MXene 纳米片作为填料，构建了 PHEAA-gelatin-MXene（PGM）共晶凝胶，以深共熔溶剂替代水来缓解低温冻结和失水问题。
- PGM 共晶凝胶具有优异的拉伸和压缩力学性能，包括 940% 拉伸率、0.5 MPa 强度、1.39 MJ m−3 韧性，以及在 80% 应变下 0.3 MPa 的压缩强度。
- 该材料同时具有良好的黏附性、抗冻性和长期抗干燥能力，并可实现高灵敏度传感，从而在宽温范围内稳定监测人体活动，可用于柔性可穿戴设备、个人健康护理和人机交互。

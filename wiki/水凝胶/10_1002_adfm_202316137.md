## 摘要

> <jats:title>Abstract</jats:title><jats:p>The rechargeable zinc‐ion battery is regarded as a promising candidate for the next‐generation energy storage system, however, zinc dendrite growth and hydrogen evolution reaction (HER) have greatly hindered the practical application of the battery. Herein, a functionalized, nano‐engineering Zn<jats:sup>2+</jats:sup> coordinated carboxylate cellulose solid‐state electrolyte (denoted as Zn‐CCNF@XG) for zinc‐ion battery is constructed through a straightforward approach. According to the experimental and density functional theory (DFT) results of dissociation energy, the notably decreased dissociation energy by −COOH is favorable to Zn<jats:sup>2+</jats:sup> de‐coordinating and rapid ion‐hopping in Zn‐CCNF@XG to achieve high ionic conductivity and transference number. More importantly, the engineered molecular channels are beneficial to enlarging the distance between the nanofibril chains, providing a larger space for the movement of Zn<jats:sup>2+</jats:sup>. Benefiting from the coordination of Zn<jats:sup>2+</jats:sup> with −OH in carboxylate cellulose nanofibrils, Zn‐CCNF@XG as a good ionic conductor displays a high ionic conductivity of 1.17 × 10<jats:sup>−4</jats:sup> S cm<jats:sup>−1</jats:sup> and transference number of 0.78. The Zn||NaV<jats:sub>3</jats:sub>O<jats:sub>8</jats:sub>·1.5H<jats:sub>2</jats:sub>O full cell with Zn‐CCNF@XG maintains a capacity retention of 83.46% with a coulombic efficiency of 99.99% after 3000 cycles (1 A g<jats:sup>−1</jats:sup>). The proposed strategy by introducing a functional group to cellulose nanofibrils effectively avoids the dendrite and HER, providing valuable guidelines for the practical application of zinc‐ion batteries.</jats:p>

## Codex 深读

- 文章针对可充锌离子电池中的锌枝晶生长和析氢反应问题，构建了功能化纳米工程 Zn2+ 配位羧化纤维素固态电解质 Zn-CCNF@XG。
- 实验和 DFT 解离能结果表明，−COOH 使解离能降低，有利于 Zn2+ 去配位和在 Zn-CCNF@XG 中快速离子跳跃；同时，工程化分子通道增大了纳米纤维链间距离，为 Zn2+ 运动提供了更大空间。
- 受益于 Zn2+ 与羧化纤维素纳米纤维中 −OH 的配位作用，Zn-CCNF@XG 表现出 1.17 × 10−4 S cm−1 的离子电导率和 0.78 的迁移数；Zn||NaV3O8·1.5H2O 全电池在 1 A g−1 下循环 3000 次后容量保持率为 83.46%，库仑效率为 99.99%。

## 摘要

> <jats:title>Abstract</jats:title><jats:p>Sodium metal batteries have gained attention as a potential solution to the low energy densities presented by current sodium‐ion batteries. However, the commonly available electrolyte systems usually fall short in safety performance. Gel polymer electrolytes, closely resembling liquid electrolytes, offer a promising balance of performance and developmental potential. A proposed polymer plastic‐crystal ionic gel composite electrolyte, featuring a polycation auxiliary chain, innovatively copolymerizes ionic liquid cations with electrolyte additives. These auxiliary chains crosslink with the main chain, attracting anions from the ionic liquid and sodium salts. Both experimental evidence and theoretical calculations affirm that this electrolyte exhibits high cation transference numbers and significant mean square displacement radii. By facilitating uniform sodium ion migration, the electrolyte has powered sodium symmetrical cells for over 550 h and has supported stable cycling of Na<jats:sub>3</jats:sub>V<jats:sub>2</jats:sub>(PO<jats:sub>4</jats:sub>)<jats:sub>3</jats:sub> (NVP)‐sodium metal batteries at a 1 C rate for more than 800 cycles. These achievements underscore its potential in advancing the development of condensed‐state sodium metal batteries.</jats:p>

## Codex 深读

- 文章面向安全性不足的钠金属电池电解质体系，提出了一种聚合物塑晶离子凝胶复合电解质，其中多阳离子辅助链将离子液体阳离子与电解液添加剂共聚到体系中。
- 这些辅助链与主链交联，并吸引来自离子液体和钠盐的阴离子；实验结果和理论计算共同表明，该电解质具有较高的阳离子迁移数和显著的均方位移半径。
- 受益于更均匀的钠离子迁移，该电解质支持钠对称电池稳定运行超过 550 h，并使 Na3V2(PO4)3（NVP）-钠金属电池在 1 C 下稳定循环超过 800 次。

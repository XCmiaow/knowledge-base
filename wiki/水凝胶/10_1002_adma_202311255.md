## 摘要

> <jats:title>Abstract</jats:title><jats:p>Hydrogels are ideal interfacing materials for on‐skin healthcare devices, yet their susceptibility to dehydration hinders their practical use. While incorporating hygroscopic metal salts can prevent dehydration and maintain ionic conductivity, concerns arise regarding metal toxicity due to the passage of small ions through the skin barrier. Herein, an antidehydration hydrogel enabled by the incorporation of zwitterionic oligomers into its network is reported. This hydrogel exhibits exceptional water retention properties, maintaining ≈88% of its weight at 40% relative humidity, 25 °C for 50 days and about 84% after being heated at 50 °C for 3 h. Crucially, the molecular weight design of the embedded oligomers prevents their penetration into the epidermis, as evidenced by experimental and molecular simulation results. The hydrogel allows stable signal acquisition in electrophysiological monitoring of humans and plants under low‐humidity conditions. This research provides a promising strategy for the development of epidermis‐safe and biocompatible antidehydration hydrogel interfaces for on‐skin devices.</jats:p>

## Codex 深读

- 文章针对贴肤健康设备中水凝胶易失水的问题，提出在网络中引入两性离子低聚物的抗失水水凝胶，以避免吸湿性金属盐带来的潜在金属毒性问题。

- 嵌入低聚物的分子量设计可阻止其穿透表皮，这一点得到了实验和分子模拟结果支持；该水凝胶可在低湿环境下稳定采集人体和植物的电生理监测信号。

## Codex 深读（2026-05-09）

### 核心贡献
用两性离子寡聚物（而非金属盐）做保水组分，同时兼顾了抗脱水、离子导通和皮肤安全性，解决贴肤水凝胶在低湿环境下失水导致信号漂移和失效的实际痛点。

### 关键数据
- 在 40% RH、25°C 条件下放置 50 天后，仍保持约 88% 初始重量
- 在 50°C 加热 3 h 后仍保持约 84% 重量
- 在更苛刻的 20% RH 下放置 60 h，仍可保留约 80% 重量
- MPC10 寡聚物分子量约 3000，高于小分子易透皮区间，降低经皮渗透风险
- 对比 PAAm（ECG 信号 18 h 开始不稳），该体系在同条件下持续输出高质量信号

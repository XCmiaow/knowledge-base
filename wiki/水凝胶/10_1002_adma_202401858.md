## 摘要

> <jats:title>Abstract</jats:title><jats:p>Flexible zinc–air batteries are the leading candidates as the next‐generation power source for flexible/wearable electronics. However, constructing safe and high‐performance solid‐state electrolytes (SSEs) with intrinsic hydroxide ion (OH<jats:sup>−</jats:sup>) conduction remains a fundamental challenge. Herein, by adopting the natural and robust cellulose nanofibers (CNFs) as building blocks, the biomass SSEs with penetrating ion and water channels are constructed by knitting the OH<jats:sup>−</jats:sup>‐conductive CNFs and water‐retentive CNFs together via an energy‐efficient tape casting. Benefiting from the abundant ion and water channels with interconnected hydrated OH<jats:sup>−</jats:sup> wires for fast OH<jats:sup>−</jats:sup> conduction under a nanoconfined environment, the biomass SSEs reveal the high water‐uptake, impressive OH<jats:sup>−</jats:sup> conductivity of 175 mS cm<jats:sup>−1</jats:sup> and mechanical robustness simultaneously, which overcomes the commonly existed dilemma between ion conductivity and mechanical property. Remarkably, the flexible zinc–air batteries assemble with biomass SSEs deliver an exceptional cycle lifespan of 310 h and power density of 126 mW cm<jats:sup>−2</jats:sup>. The design methodology for water and ion channels opens a new avenue to design high‐performance SSEs for batteries.</jats:p>

## Codex 深读

- 文章以天然且稳健的纤维素纳米纤维（CNFs）为构筑单元，通过将 OH− 导电 CNFs 与保水 CNFs 以节能流延方式编织在一起，构建了具有贯通离子通道和水通道的生物质固态电解质。
- 在纳米限域环境下，互联的水合 OH− 导线实现了快速 OH− 传导，使该固态电解质同时具有高吸水性、175 mS cm−1 的 OH− 电导率和机械稳健性。
- 采用该电解质组装的柔性锌空气电池表现出 310 h 的循环寿命和 126 mW cm−2 的功率密度。

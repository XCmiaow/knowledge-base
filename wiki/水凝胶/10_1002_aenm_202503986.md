## 摘要

> <jats:title>Abstract</jats:title>
                  <jats:p>
                    Zinc‐ion batteries are attractive candidates for wearable electronics due to their inherent safety and high theoretical capacity, whereas their application remains hindered by interfacial instability and mechanical rigidity. Herein, a 3D‐printed flexible zinc‐ion micro‐battery (FZIMB) is presented employing a zwitterionic hydrogel electrolyte based on carboxymethyl cellulose modified by adenosine monophosphate (CMC‐AMP). The self‐assembly of zwitterionic adenosine monophosphate (AMP) induces biomimetic pseudo‐ionic channels within the hydrogel matrix, forming highly ordered pathways that enable efficient and rapid Zn
                    <jats:sup>2+</jats:sup>
                    migration. Simultaneously, the highly flexible CMC‐AMP electrolyte promotes in situ formation of a robust solid‐electrolyte interphase (SEI) on Zn anodes. The synergistic interaction between pseudo‐ionic channels and adaptive SEI leads to uniform Zn electrodeposition and a reversible interfacial process. Thereupon, 3D‐printed FZIMBs with the CMC‐AMP electrolyte deliver exceptional long‐term cyclability (99.3% capacity retention over 1000 cycles at 1.5 A g
                    <jats:sup>−1</jats:sup>
                    ) and mechanical resilience (98.98% capacity retention after 100 bending cycles). When synergistically integrated with the hydrogel‐based epidermal sensors, the system enables continuous energy supply and electrophysiological signal acquisition, offering a compelling strategy for self‐sustained flexible bioelectronic platforms.
                  </jats:p>

### 核心贡献
针对可穿戴电生理系统「电池既要软又要耐久」的矛盾，提出基于 CMC-AMP 的两性离子水凝胶电解质，与 3D 打印柔性锌离子微电池集成。AMP 自组装形成类生物离子通道，同时诱导更稳定的 Zn 负极界面层，协同提升离子迁移效率、沉积均匀性和弯折可靠性。

### 关键数据
- 在 1.5 A g⁻¹ 下循环 1000 次后容量保持率 99.3%
- 弯折 100 次后容量保持率 98.98%
- 3D 打印柔性锌离子微电池结构
- 目标应用：与表皮传感器耦合的连续供能和电生理监测

## 摘要

> <jats:title>Abstract</jats:title>
                  <jats:p>
                    Hydrogel electrolytes have great potential in electrochemical energy storage devices due to their high flexibility, conductivity, and safety. However, at high temperatures, the mechanical stability and ionic conductivity of hydrogel electrolytes are compromised due to water evaporation and unstabilization of polymer matrix. Herein, we propose a salt‐concentrated hydrogel electrolyte with its matrix showing hydrophobic association effect upon heating for achieving high‐temperature adaptability. The cotton cellulose supports a stable homogeneous polymer‐salt phase in the hydrogel electrolytes. The methylcellulose works through temperature‐dependent hydrophobic interactions, maintaining the stability of the salt‐concentrated hydrogel over a wide temperature range. An all‐biobased hydrogel with both mechanical and conductive stability is realized at high temperatures (more than 60 °C). We have demonstrated a zinc‐ion hybrid supercapacitor with a high specific capacity of 250.3 mAh g
                    <jats:sup>−1</jats:sup>
                    at 120 °C and 85.4% retention for 10,000 cycles at 60 °C. This work is expected to provide a general strategy to achieve high‐temperature hydrogel electrolyte.
                  </jats:p>

### 核心发现
- 这篇文章和本综述高度同频，核心是用疏水缔合和高盐浓缩策略构建 all-cellulose hydrogel electrolyte，解决高温下凝胶失水、离子传导衰减和网络失稳问题。
- 作者最重要的贡献是把“升温后变差”的常规问题反转成“升温后通过疏水缔合进一步稳网”，说明纤维素衍生网络可以主动利用热诱导相互作用获得高温适应性。

### 方法亮点
棉纤维素负责支撑稳定均一的 polymer-salt 相，甲基纤维素提供与温度相关的疏水缔合，从而在高盐环境和升温条件下同步维持力学与导电稳定。这个设计逻辑很适合作为高温端纤维素宽温域凝胶的代表策略。

### 关键数据
- 全生物基凝胶在高于 60 °C 时仍保持机械与导电稳定。
- 组装的锌离子混合超级电容器在 120 °C 下达到约 250.3 mAh g^-1。
- 在 60 °C 循环 10,000 次后仍保持约 85.4% 容量。

### 与本综述（纤维素基宽温域水凝胶）的相关性
相关性极高。这篇几乎可以直接放进“高温稳定纤维素基水凝胶电解质”核心小节，用来补足宽温域研究长期偏重低温抗冻、忽视高温保水和稳网的缺口。

### 局限与展望

## 摘要

> <jats:title>Abstract</jats:title><jats:p>Improvement of mechanical properties of injectable tissue engineering scaffolds is a current challenge. The objective of the current study is to produce a highly porous injectable scaffold with improved mechanical properties. For this aim, cellulose nanocrystals‐reinforced dual crosslinked porous nanocomposite cryogels were prepared using chemically crosslinked methacrylated gelatin (GelMA) and ionically crosslinked hyaluronic acid (HA) through the cryogelation process. The resulting nanocomposites showed highly porous structures with interconnected porosity (&gt;90%) and mean pore size in the range of 130–296 μm. The prepared nanocomposite containing 3%w/v of GelMA, 20 w/w% of HA, and 1%w/v of CNC showed the highest Young's modulus (10 kPa) and excellent reversibility after 90% compression and could regain its initial shape after injection by a 16‐gauge needle in the aqueous media. The in vitro results demonstrated acceptable viability (&gt;90%) and migration of the human chondrocyte cell line (C28/I2), and chondrogenic differentiation of human adipose stem cells. A two‐month in vivo assay on a rabbit's ear model confirmed that the regeneration potential of the prepared cryogel is comparable to the natural autologous cartilage graft, suggesting it is a promising alternative for autografts in the treatment of cartilage defects.</jats:p>

## Codex 深读

该研究针对可注射组织工程支架力学性能不足的问题，采用化学交联的 GelMA、离子交联的 HA 以及纤维素纳米晶，通过冷冻凝胶化制备出双交联多孔纳米复合 cryogel。摘要给出的结果包括：材料具有超过 90% 的连通孔隙率和 130–296 μm 的平均孔径，其中含 3%w/v GelMA、20 w/w% HA 和 1%w/v CNC 的配方表现出最高 10 kPa 的杨氏模量，并可在 90% 压缩后恢复、经 16G 针注射后在水相中恢复初始形状；同时其体外可维持超过 90% 的细胞活性并支持软骨分化，在兔耳两个月体内实验中显示出与天然自体软骨移植物相当的再生潜力。

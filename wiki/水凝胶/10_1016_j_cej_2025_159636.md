# Green synthesis of multifunctional cellulose-based eutectogel using a metal salt hydrate-based deep eutectic solvent for sustainable self-powered sensing

**中文标题**: 基于金属盐水合物深共熔溶剂绿色合成的多功能纤维素基共晶凝胶用于可持续自供电传感

## 摘要

> [原文]「Herein, the authors propose a one-pot strategy in a green deep eutectic solvent system that simultaneously enables efficient cellulose dissolution and sunlight-induced photopolymerization into eutectogel without cross-linking agents or initiators.」这篇工作把纤维素溶解和后续成胶放进同一个绿色 DES 体系里，用一锅法直接完成从 cellulose dissolution 到 sunlight-induced photopolymerization 的连续构建，且不需要额外交联剂或引发剂。
>
> [原文]「The DES, composed of zinc chloride hydrate as hydrogen-bond acceptor and acrylic acid as donor, facilitates cellulose dissolution by breaking the hydrogen bonds within cellulose chains.」作者把体系关键放在 ZnCl2 hydrate/AA 构成的金属盐水合物型 DES 上，认为其通过破坏纤维素链内/链间氢键来实现高效溶解。
>
> [原文]「The resulting cellulose eutectogel yields superior fracture strength of 14 MPa, fracture strain of 980%, toughness of 65 MJ m−3, self-healing behavior, high conductivity, and freeze resistance down to −50 °C」并且所得 cellulose eutectogel 同时具备高强度、高延展、高韧性、自愈、导电与低温抗冻特征。
>
> [原文]「can serve as a soft electrode for triboelectric nanogenerators for wireless motion detection and human-computer interaction」文章进一步把该凝胶作为 TENG 软电极，用于无线运动检测与人机交互，说明其目标不是单纯材料性能展示，而是面向自供电器件应用。

- **[原文]「The DES, composed of zinc chloride hydrate as hydrogen-bond acceptor and acrylic acid as donor」** — 配方核心是以 ZnCl2 hydrate 为氢键受体、acrylic acid 为供体构建 DES。
- **[原文]「facilitates cellulose dissolution by breaking the hydrogen bonds within cellulose chains」** — 纤维素溶解机制来自 DES 对原有氢键网络的扰动和重构。
- **[原文]「one-pot strategy」** — 工艺路线是一锅法，先在 DES 中实现 cellulose dissolution，再经 sunlight-induced photopolymerization 原位得到 eutectogel。
- **[原文]「without cross-linking agents or initiators」** — 该方法刻意规避额外化学交联剂/引发剂，强调绿色与流程简化。

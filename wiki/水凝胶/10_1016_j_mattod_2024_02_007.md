## 摘要

This paper reports the first all-cellulose hydrogel with ultrahigh stretchability exceeding 40000%. By ring-opening the rigid anhydroglucose units and rebuilding a hydrogen-bonded network, the authors create a physically crosslinked hydrogel that also self-heals within seconds and can be drawn into flexible cellulose fibers.

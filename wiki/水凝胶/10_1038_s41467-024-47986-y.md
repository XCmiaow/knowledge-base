## 摘要

> <jats:title>Abstract</jats:title><jats:p>Self-powered skin attachable and detachable electronics are under intense development to enable the internet of everything and everyone in new and useful ways. Existing on-demand separation strategies rely on complicated pretreatments and physical properties of the adherends, achieving detachable-on-demand in a facile, rapid, and universal way remains challenging. To overcome this challenge, an ingenious cellulose nanofiber-mediated manifold dynamic synergy strategy is developed to construct a supramolecular hydrogel with both reversible tough adhesion and easy photodetachment. The cellulose nanofiber-reinforced network and the coordination between Fe ions and polymer chains endow the dynamic reconfiguration of supramolecular networks and the adhesion behavior of the hydrogel. ## Codex 深读

该工作提出一种由纤维素纳米纤维介导的多重动态协同策略，用于构建同时具备可逆强黏附和便捷光脱附能力的超分子水凝胶，以服务于可贴附、可拆卸的自供能电子皮肤。摘要指出，纤维素纳米纤维增强网络以及 Fe 离子与聚合物链之间的配位作用共同赋予了网络动态重构与黏附行为调节能力，使该策略能够简单快速地制备出韧性可调幅度最高 86%、按需黏附能调节幅度最高 93%、电导率最高 12 mS cm−1 的水凝胶，并可扩展到多种生物大分子和石油基聚合物体系，还进一步用于构建可黏附-可脱附的摩擦纳米发电机。

## 摘要

The paper reports an ultrathin cellulose-based gel electrolyte, about 10 micrometers thick, engineered with a gradient hydropenic interface for flexible zinc batteries. The design promotes interfacial stability, suppresses parasitic water-driven reactions, and enables high-energy, flexible Zn battery operation.

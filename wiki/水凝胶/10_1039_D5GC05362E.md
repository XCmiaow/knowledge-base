## 摘要

This work presents cellulose-reinforced deep eutectic gels that are eco-designed for flexible electronics. By integrating cellulose into a DES-based gel network, the authors achieve simultaneous improvement in mechanical strength, ionic conductivity, and freeze tolerance while preserving sustainability-oriented material selection.

# Mechanically robust, thermally stable, bio-sourced and flexible eutectogel electrolytes for MXene-based symmetric supercapacitors

## 摘要

Environmentally benign and novel quasi-solid-state gel electrolytes comprising gelatin and an eco-friendly deep eutectic solvent (DES) or DES-water mixtures (water content approximately 20 wt% and approximately 50 wt%) have been prepared and characterized. These gel electrolytes show promising mechanical properties with up to approximately 200% stretchability, along with interesting frequency-dependent rheological properties such as thixotropy, complex moduli crossover, and a transition from liquid-like to solid-like state. The introduction of up to approximately 50 wt% water in the gel electrolyte significantly increases ionic conductivity by approximately three orders of magnitude compared to the water-free gel electrolyte while retaining non-flammability and supporting a broad operational temperature range from -20 to 150 degrees C. Furthermore, the quasi-solid-state gel electrolytes exhibit very low water loss and robust thermal stability. Symmetric supercapacitors using Ti3C2Tx MXene electrodes and optimized gel electrolytes show enhanced electrode-electrolyte interfacial contact, excellent electrochemical stability, and an energy density of 34 Wh kg-1 at a power density of 14.4 kW kg-1, with about 92.1% capacity retention after 8000 cycles. This work highlights gelatin-DES-water gel electrolytes as promising sustainable, high-performance materials for flexible and safe energy storage devices.

- 作者的机制主线是：适量水分在明胶-DES 网络中改善离子迁移与界面浸润，从而显著提升导电和器件性能，同时凝胶网络仍维持足够的结构完整性喵~「Abstract」
- 流变中的触变性、模量交叉与液态到固态的频率依赖转变，说明该体系具有可重构网络特征，这有助于柔性器件在形变下维持接触喵~「Abstract」
- 器件层面性能提升被归因于更好的 electrode-electrolyte interfacial contact，而不是单纯依赖高本体电导喵~「Abstract」「Fig. S10-S13」

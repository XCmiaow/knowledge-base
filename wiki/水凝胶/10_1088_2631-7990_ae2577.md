However, challenges such as hydrogel dehydration, poor temperature adaptability, and weak mechanical strength hinder the engineering of ultrathin, gas-permeable organohydrogel epidermal electrodes for long-term use in complex environments. Here, we report an ultrathin, robust, gas-permeable, and freeze-resistant organohydrogel epidermal electrode for high-quality electrophysiological monitoring. The 17 µm-thick reinforced structure is readily achieved by dipping coating polyurethane nanomeshes into a high-temperature (55 °C–75 °C) gelatin-deep eutectic solvent solution and gelling at room temperature. The introduction of deep eutectic solvents significantly enhances the anti-freezing and anti-drying properties of the organohydrogel. ## Codex 深读

- **核心发现**: 这篇 IOP 开放获取论文做出了超薄、透气、抗冻、抗干燥的 organohydrogel 表皮电极，关键贡献是把气体/水汽通透性、低温适应性和长时电生理稳定性同时做到可穿戴级别
- **方法亮点**: 用聚氨酯纳米网作骨架增强，再把其浸涂到 55-75 °C 的明胶-深共熔溶剂体系中，最后室温凝胶化，避免了“做薄就脆、加抗冻剂就闷”的常见矛盾
- **关键数据**: 电极厚度仅 17 μm；可承受 527% 拉伸并通过 1000 次 100% 应变循环；界面黏附能 135.9 μJ/cm²；气体渗透率 2.1 × 10^-2 cm³·cm^-2·s^-1·cmHg^-1，水汽透过率 1130.5 g·m^-2·day^-1；可在 -25 °C 保持抗冻，并在 7 天后仍有 98.6% 质量保持率
- **综述价值**: ★★★★☆，虽然不是纤维素体系，但它是“宽温域凝胶电子皮肤”的高质量样板，对你综述中温度适应性设计和长期贴肤监测部分很有借鉴意义

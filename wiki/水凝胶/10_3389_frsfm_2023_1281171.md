# Carboxylated cellulose nanocrystal cryogel monoliths: a multi-tool study of morphology and porosity of pure and magnetite nanoparticle-decorated CNC scaffolds

## 摘要

The article investigates cryogel monoliths made from carboxylated cellulose nanocrystals, including pure CNC scaffolds and scaffolds decorated with magnetite nanoparticles. A multi-tool characterization strategy is used to quantify morphology, porosity, and structure-property relationships relevant to soft-matter and scaffold applications.

## 摘要

This review discusses cellulose cryogels as biomedical materials. It covers cellulose dissolution, regeneration, freezing and freeze-drying, then links processing variables such as cellulose origin, solvent choice, freezing rate, and fillers to the resulting morphology, porosity, and biomedical performance.

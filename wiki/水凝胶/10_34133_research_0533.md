# A Cellulose Ionogel with Rubber-Like Stretchability for Low-Grade Heat Harvesting

## 摘要

This paper reports a cellulose ionogel designed for low-grade heat harvesting. By integrating a cellulose network with ionic-liquid-based transport pathways, the authors achieve rubber-like stretchability together with thermoelectric functionality and mechanical durability.
